Montréal (1881–1901), Quebec

Montréal (1881–1901) was a Census Division in Quebec as recorded in the 1881–1901 Canadian census series. It comprised the constituent Census Subdivisions listed below for each year it appears in the published volumes. City of Montréal as census division (1921); not a historic county
