Laval, Quebec

Laval was a Census Division in Quebec as recorded in the 1861–1911 Canadian census series. It comprised the constituent Census Subdivisions listed below for each year it appears in the published volumes. Historic Laval County (Île Jésus, 1853-1965), no Wikidata entity for the historic county; modern Laval (Q141810) is post-amalgamation city
