Labrador, Quebec

Labrador was a Census Division in Quebec as recorded in the 1871 Canadian census series. It comprised the constituent Census Subdivisions listed below for each year it appears in the published volumes. Quebec-claimed Labrador CD (pre-1927 Privy Council ruling); not equivalent to modern NL Labrador
