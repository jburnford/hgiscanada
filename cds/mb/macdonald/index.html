Macdonald, Manitoba

Macdonald was a Census Division in Manitoba as recorded in the 1901–1911 Canadian census series. It comprised the constituent Census Subdivisions listed below for each year it appears in the published volumes. MB 1921 federal electoral district named after John A. Macdonald; no modern equivalent.
