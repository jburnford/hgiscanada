Calgary, Alberta

Calgary was a Census Division in Alberta as recorded in the 1911 Canadian census series. It comprised the constituent Census Subdivisions listed below for each year it appears in the published volumes. AB 1921 federal electoral district named after Calgary; centroid in foothills west of city. Not coterminous with City of Calgary.
