St. John City, New Brunswick

St. John City was a Census Division in New Brunswick as recorded in the 1881 Canadian census series. It comprised the constituent Census Subdivisions listed below for each year it appears in the published volumes. NB 1921 census subdivision for City of Saint John (city portion only); historic city pre-amalgamation has no separate Wikidata entity.
