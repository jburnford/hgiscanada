Peterborough East (1891–1911), Ontario

Peterborough East (1891–1911) was a Census Division in Ontario as recorded in the 1891–1911 Canadian census series. It comprised the constituent Census Subdivisions listed below for each year it appears in the published volumes. ON federal electoral subdivision (east half of Peterborough County); no separate Wikidata entity
