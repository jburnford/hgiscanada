Simcoe East (1891–1911), Ontario

Simcoe East (1891–1911) was a Census Division in Ontario as recorded in the 1891–1911 Canadian census series. It comprised the constituent Census Subdivisions listed below for each year it appears in the published volumes. ON federal electoral subdivision (east part of Simcoe County); no separate Wikidata entity
