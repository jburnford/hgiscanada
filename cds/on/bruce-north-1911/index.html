Bruce North (1911), Ontario

Bruce North (1911) was a Census Division in Ontario as recorded in the 1911 Canadian census series. It comprised the constituent Census Subdivisions listed below for each year it appears in the published volumes. ON federal electoral subdivision (North Bruce); no Wikidata entity.
