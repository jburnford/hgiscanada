Victoria South (1871–1901), Ontario

Victoria South (1871–1901) was a Census Division in Ontario as recorded in the 1871–1901 Canadian census series. It comprised the constituent Census Subdivisions listed below for each year it appears in the published volumes. ON federal electoral subdivision (south half of Victoria County); no separate Wikidata entity
