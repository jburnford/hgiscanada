Toronto City, Ontario

Toronto City was a Census Division in Ontario as recorded in the 1881–1891 Canadian census series. It comprised the constituent Census Subdivisions listed below for each year it appears in the published volumes. Historic 19th/early-20th-century census City of Toronto; boundaries differ from modern Toronto (Q172)
