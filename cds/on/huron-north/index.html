Huron North (1871–1881), Ontario

Huron North (1871–1881) was a Census Division in Ontario as recorded in the 1871–1881 Canadian census series. It comprised the constituent Census Subdivisions listed below for each year it appears in the published volumes. ON federal electoral subdivision (North Huron); no Wikidata entity.
