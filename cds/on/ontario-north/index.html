Ontario North, Ontario

Ontario North was an Census Division in Ontario as recorded in the 1871–1911 Canadian census series. It comprised the constituent Census Subdivisions listed below for each year it appears in the published volumes. ON federal electoral subdivision (North Ontario County); no Wikidata entity.
