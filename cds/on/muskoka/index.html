Muskoka (1871–1881), Ontario

Muskoka (1871–1881) was a Census Division in Ontario as recorded in the 1871–1881 Canadian census series. It comprised the constituent Census Subdivisions listed below for each year it appears in the published volumes. Historic Muskoka District as 19th-century CD; not coterminous with modern Muskoka District Municipality (Q382508).
