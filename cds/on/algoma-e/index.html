Algoma E, Ontario

Algoma E was an Census Division in Ontario as recorded in the 1911 Canadian census series. It comprised the constituent Census Subdivisions listed below for each year it appears in the published volumes. ON federal/provincial electoral subdivision (East half of Algoma District); no Wikidata entity.
