Victoria North, Ontario

Victoria North was a Census Division in Ontario as recorded in the 1871–1901 Canadian census series. It comprised the constituent Census Subdivisions listed below for each year it appears in the published volumes. ON federal electoral subdivision (north half of Victoria County); no separate Wikidata entity
