Yale, British Columbia

Yale was a Census Division in British Columbia as recorded in the 1881–1921 Canadian census series. It comprised the constituent Census Subdivisions listed below for each year it appears in the published volumes. BC 1921 federal electoral district named after Yale; no modern county/CD equivalent.
