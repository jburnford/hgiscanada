Cape Breton N. & Victoria, Nova Scotia

Cape Breton N. & Victoria was a Census Division in Nova Scotia as recorded in the 1911 Canadian census series. It comprised the constituent Census Subdivisions listed below for each year it appears in the published volumes. NS composite CD covering northern Cape Breton + Victoria County; no single Wikidata entity.
