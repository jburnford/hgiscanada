Queens, Nova Scotia

Queens was a Census Division in Nova Scotia as recorded in the 1871–1891 Canadian census series. It comprised the constituent Census Subdivisions listed below for each year it appears in the published volumes. This CD is grounded to Wikidata Q2006307.
