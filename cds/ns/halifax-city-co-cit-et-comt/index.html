Halifax, City & Co—Cité et comté, Nova Scotia

Halifax, City & Co—Cité et comté was a Census Division in Nova Scotia as recorded in the 1901 Canadian census series. It comprised the constituent Census Subdivisions listed below for each year it appears in the published volumes.
