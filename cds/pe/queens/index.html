Queens, Prince Edward Island

Queens was a Census Division in Prince Edward Island as recorded in the 1911–1921 Canadian census series. It comprised the constituent Census Subdivisions listed below for each year it appears in the published volumes. This CD is grounded to Wikidata Q2006290.
