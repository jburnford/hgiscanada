Prince East, Prince Edward Island

Prince East was a Census Division in Prince Edward Island as recorded in the 1901 Canadian census series. It comprised the constituent Census Subdivisions listed below for each year it appears in the published volumes. Federal electoral subdivision (east half of Prince County); no separate Wikidata entity
