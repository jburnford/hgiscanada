Northwest Territories, Northwest Territories

Northwest Territories was a Census Division in Northwest Territories as recorded in the 1911–1921 Canadian census series. It comprised the constituent Census Subdivisions listed below for each year it appears in the published volumes. This CD is grounded to Wikidata Q2007.
