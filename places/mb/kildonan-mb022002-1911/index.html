Kildonan, Manitoba (1911 census)

Kildonan was a township in Manitoba (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,255. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.942°N, 97.099°W.

Population

In 1911, Kildonan had a population of 3,255: 176 male and 122 female residents. Population density was 8.0 people per square mile.
