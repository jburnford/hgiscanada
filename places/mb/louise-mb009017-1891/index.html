Louise, Manitoba (1891 census)

Louise was a census subdivision in Manitoba, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,551. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.133°N, 98.815°W.

Population

In 1891, Louise had a population of 2,551: 1,396 male and 1,155 female residents. Population density was 5.7 people per square mile.
