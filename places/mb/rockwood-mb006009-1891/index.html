Rockwood, Manitoba (1891 census)

Rockwood was a township in Manitoba (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,901. The administrative centroid was at approximately 50.329°N, 97.288°W.

Population

In 1891, Rockwood had a population of 2,901: 1,610 male and 1,291 female residents.
