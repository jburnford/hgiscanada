Mossy River, Manitoba (1921 census)

Mossy River was a census subdivision in Manitoba, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 2,681. The administrative centroid was at approximately 51.656°N, 99.988°W.

Population

In 1921, Mossy River had a population of 2,681: 1,446 male and 1,235 female residents. Population density was 4.7 people per square mile.
