Elton, Manitoba (1891 census)

Elton was a township in Manitoba (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,534. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.975°N, 99.866°W.

Population

In 1891, Elton had a population of 1,534: 926 male and 608 female residents.
