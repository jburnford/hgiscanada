Oakland, Manitoba (1901 census)

Oakland was a census subdivision in Manitoba, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 2,128. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.621°N, 99.849°W.

Population

In 1901, Oakland had a population of 2,128: 1,161 male and 967 female residents. Population density was 9.5 people per square mile.
