Bifrost, Manitoba (1921 census)

Bifrost was a census subdivision in Manitoba, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 5,732. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q1658083. The administrative centroid was at approximately 51.041°N, 96.988°W.
