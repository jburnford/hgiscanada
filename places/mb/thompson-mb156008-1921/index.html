Thompson, Manitoba (1921 census)

Thompson was a census subdivision in Manitoba, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 2,180. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.364°N, 98.249°W.

Population

In 1921, Thompson had a population of 2,180: 1,214 male and 966 female residents. Population density was 10.6 people per square mile.
