Elm River, Manitoba (1891 census)

Elm River was a census subdivision in Manitoba, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 567. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.666°N, 98.284°W.

In 1891, Elm River had a population of 567: 318 male and 249 female residents.
