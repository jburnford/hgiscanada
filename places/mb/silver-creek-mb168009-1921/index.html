Silver Creek, Manitoba (1921 census)

Silver Creek was a census subdivision in Manitoba, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 1,797. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q534895. The administrative centroid was at approximately 50.730°N, 101.097°W.

Population

In 1921, Silver Creek had a population of 1,797: 979 male and 818 female residents. Population density was 8.7 people per square mile.
