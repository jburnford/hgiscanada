Brokenhead, Manitoba (1921 census)

Brokenhead was a census subdivision in Manitoba, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 4,121. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q1658376. The administrative centroid was at approximately 50.129°N, 96.515°W.

Population

In 1921, Brokenhead had a population of 4,121: 2,198 male and 1,923 female residents. Population density was 14.3 people per square mile.
