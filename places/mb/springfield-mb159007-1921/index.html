Springfield, Manitoba (1921 census)

Springfield was a township in Manitoba (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 5,660. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.915°N, 96.537°W.

Population

In 1921, Springfield had a population of 5,660: 3,108 male and 2,552 female residents.
