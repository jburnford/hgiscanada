Turtle Mountain, Manitoba (1921 census)

Turtle Mountain was a township in Manitoba (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 2,541. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q1660423. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.146°N, 99.680°W.
