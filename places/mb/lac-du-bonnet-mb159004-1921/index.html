Lac du Bonnet, Manitoba (1921 census)

Lac du Bonnet was a census subdivision in Manitoba, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 2,141. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q281311. The administrative centroid was at approximately 50.250°N, 96.106°W.
