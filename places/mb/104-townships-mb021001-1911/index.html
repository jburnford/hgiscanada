104 townships, Manitoba (1911 census)

104 townships was a township in Manitoba (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 22,932. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.349°N, 96.521°W.

Population

In 1911, 104 townships had a population of 22,932: 143 male and 129 female residents. Population density was 7.3 people per square mile.
