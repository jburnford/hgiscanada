Unorganized territory between tps . 18 & 23 r. 16 & 26 W.P.M., Manitoba (1921 census)

Unorganized territory between tps . 18 & 23 r. 16 & 26 W.P.M. was a census subdivision in Manitoba, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 1,445. The administrative centroid was at approximately 50.783°N, 100.213°W.
