Brenda, Manitoba (1891 census)

Brenda was a township in Manitoba (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,016. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.401°N, 100.782°W.

In 1891, Brenda had a population of 1,016: 612 male and 404 female residents.
