Russell, Silver Creek, Manitoba (1891 census)

Russell, Silver Creek was a census subdivision in Manitoba, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,407. The administrative centroid was at approximately 50.731°N, 101.230°W.

Population

In 1891, Russell, Silver Creek had a population of 1,407: 850 male and 557 female residents.
