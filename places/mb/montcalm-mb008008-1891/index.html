Montcalm, Manitoba (1891 census)

Montcalm was a township in Manitoba (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,688. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.140°N, 97.353°W.

Population

In 1891, Montcalm had a population of 1,688: 903 male and 785 female residents.
