Saskatchewan, Manitoba (1901 census)

Saskatchewan was a census subdivision in Manitoba, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 935. The administrative centroid was at approximately 50.196°N, 100.079°W.

Population

In 1901, Saskatchewan had a population of 935: 563 male and 372 female residents. Population density was 4.2 people per square mile.
