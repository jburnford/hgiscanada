Odanah, Manitoba (1921 census)

Odanah was a census subdivision in Manitoba, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 1,335. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q1660936. The administrative centroid was at approximately 50.152°N, 99.797°W.

Population
