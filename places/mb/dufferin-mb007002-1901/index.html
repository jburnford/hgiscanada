Dufferin, Manitoba (1901 census)

Dufferin was a census subdivision in Manitoba, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 5,527. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.576°N, 98.072°W.

Population

In 1901, Dufferin had a population of 5,527: 3,206 male and 2,321 female residents.
