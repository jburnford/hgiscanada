Westbourne, Manitoba (1921 census)

Westbourne was a census subdivision in Manitoba, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 2,888. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q1661880. The administrative centroid was at approximately 50.248°N, 98.899°W.
