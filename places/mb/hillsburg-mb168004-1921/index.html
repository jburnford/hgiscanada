Hillsburg, Manitoba (1921 census)

Hillsburg was a census subdivision in Manitoba, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 1,316. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q1660364. The administrative centroid was at approximately 51.288°N, 101.120°W.
