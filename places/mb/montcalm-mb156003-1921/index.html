Montcalm, Manitoba (1921 census)

Montcalm was a census subdivision in Manitoba, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 2,906. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q1660784. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.184°N, 97.322°W.
