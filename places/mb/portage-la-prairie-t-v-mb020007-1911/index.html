Portage la Prairie t-v, Manitoba (1911 census)

Portage la Prairie t-v was a township in Manitoba (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 5,892. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.979°N, 98.293°W.

Population

In 1911, Portage la Prairie t-v had a population of 5,892: 92 male and 71 female residents. Population density was 11.8 people per square mile.
