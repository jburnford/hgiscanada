Dauphin, Manitoba (1921 census)

Dauphin was a census subdivision in Manitoba, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 6,059. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q1658912. The administrative centroid was at approximately 51.202°N, 100.048°W.
