Lawrence, Manitoba (1921 census)

Lawrence was a census subdivision in Manitoba, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 2,102. The administrative centroid was at approximately 51.442°N, 99.529°W.

Population

In 1921, Lawrence had a population of 2,102: 1,193 male and 909 female residents. Population density was 3.0 people per square mile.
