Cypress N, Manitoba (1891 census)

Cypress N was a township in Manitoba (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,661. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.931°N, 99.380°W.

Population

In 1891, Cypress N had a population of 2,661: 1,528 male and 1,133 female residents.
