Winnipeg c pt, Manitoba (1911 census)

Winnipeg c pt was a census subdivision in Manitoba, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 128,157. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.865°N, 97.166°W.

Population

In 1911, Winnipeg c pt had a population of 128,157: 70,110 male and 58,047 female residents. Population density was 15980.5 people per square mile.
