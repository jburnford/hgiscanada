Winchester, Manitoba (1901 census)

Winchester was a census subdivision in Manitoba, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 2,187. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.267°N, 100.580°W.

Population

In 1901, Winchester had a population of 2,187: 1,220 male and 967 female residents. Population density was 7.3 people per square mile.
