Rosser, Manitoba (1921 census)

Rosser was a census subdivision in Manitoba, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 3,730. The administrative centroid was at approximately 50.004°N, 97.404°W.

Population

In 1921, Rosser had a population of 3,730: 2,019 male and 1,711 female residents. Population density was 21.2 people per square mile.
