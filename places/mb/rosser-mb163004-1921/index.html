Rosser, Manitoba (1921 census)

Rosser was a census subdivision in Manitoba, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 3,730. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q1661244. The administrative centroid was at approximately 50.004°N, 97.404°W.
