Sifton, Manitoba (1891 census)

Sifton was a township in Manitoba (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,504. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.665°N, 100.669°W.

Population

In 1891, Sifton had a population of 1,504: 873 male and 631 female residents.
