Plessis (Fort Alexander), Manitoba (1891 census)

Plessis (Fort Alexander) was a township in Manitoba (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,296. The administrative centroid was at approximately 51.460°N, 95.876°W.

Population

In 1891, Plessis (Fort Alexander) had a population of 1,296: 663 male and 633 female residents.
