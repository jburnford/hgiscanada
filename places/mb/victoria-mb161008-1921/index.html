Victoria, Manitoba (1921 census)

Victoria was a census subdivision in Manitoba, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 2,036. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q1661723. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.659°N, 98.926°W.

Population

In 1921, Victoria had a population of 2,036: 1,116 male and 920 female residents. Population density was 7.3 people per square mile.
