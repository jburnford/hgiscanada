Pipestone, Manitoba (1921 census)

Pipestone was a census subdivision in Manitoba, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 3,251. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q1660980. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.666°N, 101.148°W.
