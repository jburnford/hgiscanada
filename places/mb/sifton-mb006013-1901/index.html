Sifton, Manitoba (1901 census)

Sifton was a census subdivision in Manitoba, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 2,025. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.665°N, 100.669°W.

Population

In 1901, Sifton had a population of 2,025: 1,118 male and 907 female residents. Population density was 6.0 people per square mile.
