Elgin, New Brunswick (1871 census)

Elgin was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,768. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q5360046. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.806°N, 65.033°W.

Population

In 1871, Elgin had a population of 1,768: 952 male and 816 female residents. Population density was 8.6 people per square mile.
