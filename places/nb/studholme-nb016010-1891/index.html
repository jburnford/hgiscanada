Studholme, New Brunswick (1891 census)

Studholme was a township in New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,785. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.813°N, 65.575°W.

Population

In 1891, Studholme had a population of 2,785: 1,445 male and 1,340 female residents.
