Shediac, New Brunswick (1871 census)

Shediac was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 5,756. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7492307. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.185°N, 64.500°W.
