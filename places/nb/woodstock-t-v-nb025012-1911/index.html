Woodstock, T-V, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Woodstock, T-V was a township in New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,856. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q2559651. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.145°N, 67.588°W.
