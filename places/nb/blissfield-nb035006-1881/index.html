Blissfield, New Brunswick (1881 census)

Blissfield was a census subdivision in Northumberland County, New Brunswick, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 902. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q664434. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.600°N, 66.042°W.

Population

In 1881, Blissfield had a population of 902: 464 male and 438 female residents. Population density was 1.8 people per square mile.
