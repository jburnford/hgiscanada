Harvey, New Brunswick (1891 census)

Harvey was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,614. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365764. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.706°N, 64.812°W.

Population

In 1891, Harvey had a population of 1,614: 869 male and 745 female residents. Population density was 14.1 people per square mile.
