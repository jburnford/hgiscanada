Beresford, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Beresford was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 4,917. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.735°N, 65.866°W.

Population

In 1911, Beresford had a population of 4,917: 2,553 male and 2,364 female residents. Population density was 22.8 people per square mile.
