St. Stephen's, New Brunswick (1881 census)

St. Stephen's was a census subdivision in Charlotte County, New Brunswick, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,163. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.218°N, 67.323°W.

Population

In 1881, St. Stephen's had a population of 1,163: 594 male and 569 female residents. Population density was 29.9 people per square mile.
