St. Marys, New Brunswick (1881 census)

St. Marys was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 3,873. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365737. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.168°N, 66.494°W.
