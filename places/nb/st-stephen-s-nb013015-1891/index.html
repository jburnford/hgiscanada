St. Stephen's, New Brunswick (1891 census)

St. Stephen's was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 708. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.218°N, 67.323°W.

Population

In 1891, St. Stephen's had a population of 708: 344 male and 364 female residents.
