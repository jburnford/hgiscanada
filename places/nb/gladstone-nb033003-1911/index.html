Gladstone, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Gladstone was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,061. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q5566340. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.628°N, 66.726°W.
