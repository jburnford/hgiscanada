Andover, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Andover was a township in New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,331. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.705°N, 67.751°W.

Population

In 1911, Andover had a population of 1,331: 728 male and 603 female residents. Population density was 27.7 people per square mile.
