St. John, Sydney, Ward—Quartier, New Brunswick (1911 census)

St. John, Sydney, Ward—Quartier was a township in New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,414. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.266°N, 66.054°W.
