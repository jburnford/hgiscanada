Campobello, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Campobello was a township in New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,230. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365793. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.888°N, 66.936°W.
