Perth, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Perth was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,777. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.710°N, 67.605°W.

Population

In 1911, Perth had a population of 1,777: 941 male and 836 female residents. Population density was 13.8 people per square mile.
