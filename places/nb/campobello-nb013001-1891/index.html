Campobello, New Brunswick (1891 census)

Campobello was a township in New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,188. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365793. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.888°N, 66.936°W.

Population

In 1891, Campobello had a population of 1,188: 621 male and 567 female residents.
