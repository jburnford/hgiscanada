West Isles, New Brunswick (1911 census)

West Isles was a township in New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,385. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7985568. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.982°N, 66.975°W.
