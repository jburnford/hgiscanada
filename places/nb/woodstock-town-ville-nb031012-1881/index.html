Woodstock, Town—Ville, New Brunswick (1881 census)

Woodstock, Town—Ville was a town in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,487. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q2559651. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.145°N, 67.588°W.
