North Lake, New Brunswick (1891 census)

North Lake was a township in New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 755. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365860. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.757°N, 67.668°W.
