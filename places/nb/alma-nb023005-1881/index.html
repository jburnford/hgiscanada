Alma, New Brunswick (1881 census)

Alma was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,263. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365750. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.634°N, 65.008°W.

Population

In 1881, Alma had a population of 1,263: 674 male and 589 female residents. Population density was 11.6 people per square mile.
