New Bandon, New Brunswick (1881 census)

New Bandon was a township in Gloucester County, New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,390. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365854. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.697°N, 65.267°W.

Population

In 1881, New Bandon had a population of 2,390: 1,222 male and 1,168 female residents.
