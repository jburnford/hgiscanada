Southampton, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Southampton was a township in New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,194. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365928. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.079°N, 67.308°W.
