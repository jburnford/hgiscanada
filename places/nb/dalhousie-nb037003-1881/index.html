Dalhousie, New Brunswick (1881 census)

Dalhousie was a census subdivision in Restigouche County, New Brunswick, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,353. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365804. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.761°N, 66.512°W.

Population

In 1881, Dalhousie had a population of 2,353: 1,228 male and 1,125 female residents.
