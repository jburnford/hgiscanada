Norton, New Brunswick (1891 census)

Norton was a township in New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,605. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.597°N, 65.745°W.

Population

In 1891, Norton had a population of 1,605: 820 male and 785 female residents.
