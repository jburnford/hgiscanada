Blackville, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Blackville was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,591. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365779. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.673°N, 65.800°W.
