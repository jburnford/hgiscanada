St. Andrews, New Brunswick (1911 census)

St. Andrews was a township in New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 238. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365886. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.120°N, 67.065°W.

Population

In 1911, St. Andrews had a population of 238: 134 male and 104 female residents. Population density was 17.7 people per square mile.
