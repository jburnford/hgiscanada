St. Jacques, New Brunswick (1911 census)

St. Jacques was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,372. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365899. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.560°N, 68.343°W.
