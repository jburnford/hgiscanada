Sackville, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Sackville was a township in New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,514. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365883. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.972°N, 64.379°W.

Population

In 1911, Sackville had a population of 3,514: 1,887 male and 1,627 female residents. Population density was 22.4 people per square mile.
