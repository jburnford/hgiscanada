Sackville, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Sackville was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,514. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365883. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.972°N, 64.379°W.
