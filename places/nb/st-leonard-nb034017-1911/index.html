St. Leonard, New Brunswick (1911 census)

St. Leonard was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,026. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.248°N, 67.799°W.

Population

In 1911, St. Leonard had a population of 2,026: 1,082 male and 944 female residents. Population density was 12.2 people per square mile.
