Grand Falls, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Grand Falls was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,400. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q5594542. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.925°N, 67.746°W.
