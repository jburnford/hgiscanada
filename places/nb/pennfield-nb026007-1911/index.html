Pennfield, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Pennfield was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,146. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365867. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.187°N, 66.689°W.
