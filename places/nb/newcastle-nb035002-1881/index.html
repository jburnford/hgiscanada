Newcastle, New Brunswick (1881 census)

Newcastle was a census subdivision in Northumberland County, New Brunswick, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 4,209. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365857. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.156°N, 65.553°W.
