Milltown, Town—Ville, New Brunswick (1881 census)

Milltown, Town—Ville was a town in Charlotte County, New Brunswick, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,664. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.173°N, 67.307°W.

Population

In 1881, Milltown, Town—Ville had a population of 1,664: 835 male and 829 female residents. Population density was 585.3 people per square mile.
