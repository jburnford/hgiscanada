St. Mary's, New Brunswick (1881 census)

St. Mary's was a township in Kent County, New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,904. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.375°N, 64.904°W.

Population

In 1881, St. Mary's had a population of 2,904: 1,483 male and 1,421 female residents.
