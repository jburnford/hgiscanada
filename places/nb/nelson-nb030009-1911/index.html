Nelson, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Nelson was a township in New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,985. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365853. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.847°N, 65.590°W.
