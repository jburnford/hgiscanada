Aberdeen, New Brunswick (1881 census)

Aberdeen was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,399. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3296641. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.507°N, 67.281°W.

Population

In 1881, Aberdeen had a population of 1,399: 752 male and 647 female residents. Population density was 7.9 people per square mile.
