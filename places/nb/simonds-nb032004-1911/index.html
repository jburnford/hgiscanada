Simonds, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Simonds was a township in New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,878. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7520238. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.309°N, 65.859°W.

Population

In 1911, Simonds had a population of 2,878: 1,572 male and 1,306 female residents. Population density was 15.2 people per square mile.
