Durham, New Brunswick (1881 census)

Durham was a census subdivision in Restigouche County, New Brunswick, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,783. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365814. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.764°N, 66.037°W.
