St. George, Town & Parish — Ville et Paroisse, New Brunswick (1891 census)

St. George, Town & Parish — Ville et Paroisse was a parish in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,757. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.288°N, 66.850°W.

In 1891, St. George, Town & Parish — Ville et Paroisse had a population of 2,757: 1,460 male and 1,297 female residents.
