St. John, Sydney, Ward—Quartier, New Brunswick (1881 census)

St. John, Sydney, Ward—Quartier was a census subdivision in St. John (City—Ville) County, New Brunswick, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,484. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.266°N, 66.054°W.
