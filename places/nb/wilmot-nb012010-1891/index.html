Wilmot, New Brunswick (1891 census)

Wilmot was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,928. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q8022837. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.347°N, 67.710°W.
