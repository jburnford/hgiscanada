Richmond, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Richmond was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,470. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365875. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.078°N, 67.725°W.
