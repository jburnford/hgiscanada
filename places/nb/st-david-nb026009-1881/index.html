St. David, New Brunswick (1881 census)

St. David was a census subdivision in Charlotte County, New Brunswick, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,864. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365890. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.290°N, 67.198°W.

Population

In 1881, St. David had a population of 1,864: 939 male and 925 female residents. Population density was 24.1 people per square mile.
