Ludlow, New Brunswick (1881 census)

Ludlow was a census subdivision in Northumberland County, New Brunswick, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 750. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365842. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.599°N, 66.362°W.

Population

In 1881, Ludlow had a population of 750: 412 male and 338 female residents. Population density was 1.9 people per square mile.
