Westmorland, New Brunswick (1881 census)

Westmorland was a township in Westmoreland County, New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,231. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.989°N, 64.187°W.

Population

In 1881, Westmorland had a population of 2,231: 1,117 male and 1,114 female residents.
