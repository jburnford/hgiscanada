Douglas, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Douglas was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,723. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365810. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.275°N, 66.935°W.

Population

In 1911, Douglas had a population of 2,723: 1,452 male and 1,271 female residents. Population density was 4.8 people per square mile.
