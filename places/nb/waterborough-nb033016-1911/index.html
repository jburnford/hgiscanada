Waterborough, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Waterborough was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,087. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.061°N, 65.821°W.

Population

In 1911, Waterborough had a population of 1,087: 539 male and 548 female residents. Population density was 5.3 people per square mile.
