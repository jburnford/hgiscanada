Waterborough, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Waterborough was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,087. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365937. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.061°N, 65.821°W.
