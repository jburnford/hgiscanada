St. John City, Lorne ward-quartier, New Brunswick (1881 census)

St. John City, Lorne ward-quartier was a census subdivision in St. John (County) County, New Brunswick, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 3,166. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.273°N, 66.085°W.

Population

In 1881, St. John City, Lorne ward-quartier had a population of 3,166: 1,525 male and 1,641 female residents. Population density was 8296.7 people per square mile.
