Blackville, New Brunswick (1881 census)

Blackville was a census subdivision in Northumberland County, New Brunswick, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,065. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365779. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.673°N, 65.800°W.
