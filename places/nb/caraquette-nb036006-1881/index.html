Caraquette, New Brunswick (1881 census)

Caraquette was a township in Gloucester County, New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 3,837. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.743°N, 64.914°W.
