Lancaster, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Lancaster was a township in New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 5,886. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.228°N, 66.182°W.

Population

In 1911, Lancaster had a population of 5,886: 3,022 male and 2,864 female residents. Population density was 128.4 people per square mile.
