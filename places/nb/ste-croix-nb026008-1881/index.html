Ste. Croix, New Brunswick (1881 census)

Ste. Croix was a census subdivision in Charlotte County, New Brunswick, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 747. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365913. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.206°N, 67.112°W.

Population

In 1881, Ste. Croix had a population of 747: 403 male and 344 female residents. Population density was 22.2 people per square mile.
