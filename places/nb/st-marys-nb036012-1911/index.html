St. Marys, New Brunswick (1911 census)

St. Marys was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,857. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.168°N, 66.493°W.

Population

In 1911, St. Marys had a population of 3,857: 1,956 male and 1,901 female residents. Population density was 12.5 people per square mile.
