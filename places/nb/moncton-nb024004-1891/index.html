Moncton, New Brunswick (1891 census)

Moncton was a township in New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 5,165. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.160°N, 64.868°W.

Population

In 1891, Moncton had a population of 5,165: 2,691 male and 2,474 female residents.
