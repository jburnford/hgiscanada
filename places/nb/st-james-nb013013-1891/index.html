St. James, New Brunswick (1891 census)

St. James was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,266. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365900. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.383°N, 67.343°W.
