Chatham, New Brunswick (1881 census)

Chatham was a census subdivision in Northumberland County, New Brunswick, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 5,762. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.021°N, 65.445°W.

Population

In 1881, Chatham had a population of 5,762: 2,943 male and 2,819 female residents.
