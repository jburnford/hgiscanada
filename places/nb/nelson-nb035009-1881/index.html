Nelson, New Brunswick (1881 census)

Nelson was a census subdivision in Northumberland County, New Brunswick, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,724. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365853. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.806°N, 65.544°W.
