Colborne, New Brunswick (1871 census)

Colborne was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 853. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365802. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.766°N, 66.271°W.

Population

In 1871, Colborne had a population of 853: 449 male and 404 female residents. Population density was 2.8 people per square mile.
