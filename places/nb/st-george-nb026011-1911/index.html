St. George, New Brunswick (1911 census)

St. George was a township in New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,839. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365892. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.288°N, 66.850°W.
