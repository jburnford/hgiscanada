Cambridge, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Cambridge was a township in New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 984. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q657125. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.818°N, 66.044°W.
