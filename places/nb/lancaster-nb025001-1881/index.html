Lancaster, New Brunswick (1881 census)

Lancaster was a township in St. John (County) County, New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 4,333. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.228°N, 66.182°W.
