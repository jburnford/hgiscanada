Chatham, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Chatham was a township in New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,088. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365797. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.021°N, 65.445°W.
