Dundas, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Dundas was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,281. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365816. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.321°N, 64.676°W.
