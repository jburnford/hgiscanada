Douglas, New Brunswick (1881 census)

Douglas was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,916. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365810. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.275°N, 66.935°W.

Population

In 1881, Douglas had a population of 2,916: 1,535 male and 1,381 female residents. Population density was 5.0 people per square mile.
