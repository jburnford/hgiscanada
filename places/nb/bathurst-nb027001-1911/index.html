Bathurst, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Bathurst was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 5,248. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q810807. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.469°N, 65.712°W.

Population

In 1911, Bathurst had a population of 5,248: 2,702 male and 2,546 female residents. Population density was 6.2 people per square mile.
