Westfield, New Brunswick (1891 census)

Westfield was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,172. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365944. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.341°N, 66.291°W.

Population

In 1891, Westfield had a population of 1,172: 606 male and 566 female residents. Population density was 7.9 people per square mile.
