North Esk, New Brunswick (1881 census)

North Esk was a township in Northumberland County, New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,538. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.255°N, 66.255°W.

Population

In 1881, North Esk had a population of 1,538: 821 male and 717 female residents.
