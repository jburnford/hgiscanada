Southampton, New Brunswick (1881 census)

Southampton was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,244. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365928. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.079°N, 67.308°W.

Population

In 1881, Southampton had a population of 2,244: 1,172 male and 1,072 female residents. Population density was 11.9 people per square mile.
