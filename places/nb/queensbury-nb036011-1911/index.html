Queensbury, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Queensbury was a township in New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,199. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365876. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.994°N, 67.065°W.
