Waterborough, New Brunswick (1871 census)

Waterborough was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,369. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.061°N, 65.821°W.

Population

In 1871, Waterborough had a population of 1,369: 712 male and 657 female residents. Population density was 6.7 people per square mile.
