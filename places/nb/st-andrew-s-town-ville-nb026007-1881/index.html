St. Andrew's, Town—Ville, New Brunswick (1881 census)

St. Andrew's, Town—Ville was a township in Charlotte County, New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,128. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.118°N, 67.065°W.

Population

In 1881, St. Andrew's, Town—Ville had a population of 2,128: 1,047 male and 1,081 female residents.
