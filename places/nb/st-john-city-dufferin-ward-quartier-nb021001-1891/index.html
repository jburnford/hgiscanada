St. John City, Dufferin ward-quartier, New Brunswick (1891 census)

St. John City, Dufferin ward-quartier was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 3,964. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.282°N, 66.070°W.

In 1891, St. John City, Dufferin ward-quartier had a population of 3,964: 1,922 male and 2,042 female residents.
