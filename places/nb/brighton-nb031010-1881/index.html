Brighton, New Brunswick (1881 census)

Brighton was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,496. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365787. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.330°N, 67.363°W.

Population

In 1881, Brighton had a population of 2,496: 1,275 male and 1,221 female residents. Population density was 12.1 people per square mile.
