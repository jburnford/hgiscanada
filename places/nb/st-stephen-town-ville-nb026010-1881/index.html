St. Stephen, Town—Ville, New Brunswick (1881 census)

St. Stephen, Town—Ville was a township in Charlotte County, New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,338. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.198°N, 67.280°W.
