Perth, New Brunswick (1891 census)

Perth was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,792. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.715°N, 67.609°W.

Population

In 1891, Perth had a population of 1,792: 978 male and 814 female residents. Population density was 13.1 people per square mile.
