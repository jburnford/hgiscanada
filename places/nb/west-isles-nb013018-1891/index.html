West Isles, New Brunswick (1891 census)

West Isles was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,644. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7985568. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.982°N, 66.975°W.
