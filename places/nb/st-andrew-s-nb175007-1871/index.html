St. Andrew’s, New Brunswick (1871 census)

St. Andrew’s was a township in New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 2,961. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.181°N, 67.098°W.

In 1871, St. Andrew’s had a population of 2,961: 1,488 male and 1,473 female residents.
