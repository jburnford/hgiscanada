Kingston, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Kingston was a township in New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,494. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q6413572. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.476°N, 66.019°W.
