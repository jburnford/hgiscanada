Bright, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Bright was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,463. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365789. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.124°N, 67.059°W.
