Hillsborough, New Brunswick (1881 census)

Hillsborough was a township in New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 3,012. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.905°N, 64.766°W.

Population

In 1881, Hillsborough had a population of 3,012: 1,522 male and 1,490 female residents.
