Aberdeen, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Aberdeen was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,061. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3296641. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.507°N, 67.281°W.
