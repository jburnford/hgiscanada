Musquash, New Brunswick (1881 census)

Musquash was a census subdivision in St. John (County) County, New Brunswick, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 998. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365852. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.183°N, 66.353°W.
