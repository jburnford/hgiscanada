Glenelg, New Brunswick (1911 census)

Glenelg was a township in New Brunswick (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,294. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q5568476. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.946°N, 65.282°W.

Population

In 1911, Glenelg had a population of 1,294: 694 male and 600 female residents. Population density was 5.9 people per square mile.
