Douglas, New Brunswick (1921 census)

Douglas was a census subdivision in New Brunswick, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 3,094. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3365810. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.275°N, 66.935°W.
