Grimsby, Village, Ontario (1911 census)

Grimsby, Village was a village in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,669. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q424901. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.198°N, 79.563°W.
