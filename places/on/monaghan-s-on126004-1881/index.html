Monaghan S, Ontario (1881 census)

Monaghan S was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,148. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.167°N, 78.304°W.

Population

In 1881, Monaghan S had a population of 1,148: 596 male and 552 female residents. Population density was 32.2 people per square mile.
