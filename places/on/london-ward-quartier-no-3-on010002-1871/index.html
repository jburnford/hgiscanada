London, Ward—Quartier No. 3, Ontario (1871 census)

London, Ward—Quartier No. 3 was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 2,736. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.989°N, 81.255°W.
