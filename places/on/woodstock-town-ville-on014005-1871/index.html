Woodstock, Town—Ville, Ontario (1871 census)

Woodstock, Town—Ville was a town in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 3,982. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q1642364. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.130°N, 80.753°W.
