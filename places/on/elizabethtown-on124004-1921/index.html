Elizabethtown, Ontario (1921 census)

Elizabethtown was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 3,743. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.656°N, 75.789°W.

Population

In 1921, Elizabethtown had a population of 3,743: 1,932 male and 1,811 female residents. Population density was 26.4 people per square mile.
