Silver Islet, Ontario (1881 census)

Silver Islet was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 448. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.519°N, 88.722°W.

Population

In 1881, Silver Islet had a population of 448: 270 male and 178 female residents. Population density was 2.5 people per square mile.
