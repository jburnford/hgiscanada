Grantham, Ontario (1911 census)

Grantham was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,439. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q5596641. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.178°N, 79.213°W.

Population

In 1911, Grantham had a population of 2,439: 1,291 male and 1,148 female residents. Population density was 72.4 people per square mile.
