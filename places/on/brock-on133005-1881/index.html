Brock, Ontario (1881 census)

Brock was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 4,378. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q387094. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.288°N, 79.074°W.
