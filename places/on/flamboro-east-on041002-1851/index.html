Flamboro, East, Ontario (1851 census)

Flamboro, East was a township in Wentworth County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,903. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.375°N, 79.951°W.

Population

In 1851, Flamboro, East had a population of 2,903: 1,508 male and 1,395 female residents.
