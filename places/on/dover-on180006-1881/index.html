Dover, Ontario (1881 census)

Dover was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 4,447. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.437°N, 82.341°W.

Population

In 1881, Dover had a population of 4,447: 2,339 male and 2,108 female residents. Population density was 38.5 people per square mile.
