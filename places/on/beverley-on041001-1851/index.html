Beverley, Ontario (1851 census)

Beverley was a township in Wentworth County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 5,620. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q4899444. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.319°N, 80.145°W.

Population

In 1851, Beverley had a population of 5,620: 3,097 male and 2,523 female residents.
