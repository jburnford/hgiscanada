Timmins, T-V, Ontario (1921 census)

Timmins, T-V was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 3,843. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q991310. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.476°N, 81.335°W.
