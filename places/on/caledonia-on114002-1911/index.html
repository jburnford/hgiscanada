Caledonia, Ontario (1911 census)

Caledonia was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,247. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.463°N, 74.800°W.

Population

In 1911, Caledonia had a population of 2,247: 1,151 male and 1,096 female residents. Population density was 30.8 people per square mile.
