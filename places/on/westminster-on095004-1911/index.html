Westminster, Ontario (1911 census)

Westminster was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 5,019. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.911°N, 81.225°W.

Population

In 1911, Westminster had a population of 5,019: 2,591 male and 2,428 female residents. Population density was 48.2 people per square mile.
