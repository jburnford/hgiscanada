Brooke, Ontario (1911 census)

Brooke was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,992. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115260945. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.849°N, 81.905°W.
