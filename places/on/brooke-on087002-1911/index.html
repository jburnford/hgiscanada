Brooke, Ontario (1911 census)

Brooke was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,992. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115260945. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.849°N, 81.905°W.

Population

In 1911, Brooke had a population of 2,992: 1,568 male and 1,424 female residents. Population density was 25.2 people per square mile.
