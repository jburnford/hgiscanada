Brantford, Ontario (1851 census)

Brantford was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 6,410. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.130°N, 80.269°W.

Population

In 1851, Brantford had a population of 6,410: 3,393 male and 3,017 female residents.
