Wainfleet, Ontario (1861 census)

Wainfleet was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,316. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q424870. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.935°N, 79.397°W.
