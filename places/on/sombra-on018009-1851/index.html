Sombra, Ontario (1851 census)

Sombra was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 738. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.683°N, 82.374°W.

Population

In 1851, Sombra had a population of 738: 395 male and 343 female residents.

Boundary continuity (non-identical overlaps)

Spatial polygon overlaps with adjacent census years where the boundary shifted enough that the SAME_AS chain didn't merge them. These show where the territory came from and went to even when it isn't tracked as the same persistent place.

Later boundary forms

  • In a later year, this CSD became part of Sombra and Indian Reserve, 1861 (68.1% share).
