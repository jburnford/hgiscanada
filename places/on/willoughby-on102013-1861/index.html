Willoughby, Ontario (1861 census)

Willoughby was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,539. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q8022201. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.006°N, 79.069°W.
