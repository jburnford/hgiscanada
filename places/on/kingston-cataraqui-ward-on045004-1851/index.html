Kingston, Cataraqui Ward, Ontario (1851 census)

Kingston, Cataraqui Ward was a township in Kingston, City County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,658. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.244°N, 76.481°W.

Population

In 1851, Kingston, Cataraqui Ward had a population of 1,658: 807 male and 851 female residents.
