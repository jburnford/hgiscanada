Bedford, Ontario (1911 census)

Bedford was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,421. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115263202. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.620°N, 76.537°W.
