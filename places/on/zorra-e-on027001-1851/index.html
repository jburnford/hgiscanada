Zorra E, Ontario (1851 census)

Zorra E was a township in Oxford County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,200. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q5329738. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.239°N, 80.791°W.
