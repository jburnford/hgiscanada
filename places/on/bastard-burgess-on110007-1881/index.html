Bastard & Burgess, Ontario (1881 census)

Bastard & Burgess was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 3,500. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.690°N, 76.139°W.

Population

In 1881, Bastard & Burgess had a population of 3,500: 1,720 male and 1,780 female residents. Population density was 28.2 people per square mile.
