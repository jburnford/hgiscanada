Orillia, Ontario (1911 census)

Orillia was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 4,508. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.654°N, 79.432°W.

Population

In 1911, Orillia had a population of 4,508: 2,411 male and 2,097 female residents. Population density was 37.4 people per square mile.
