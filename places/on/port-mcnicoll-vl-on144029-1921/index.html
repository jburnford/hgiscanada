Port McNicoll, VL, Ontario (1921 census)

Port McNicoll, VL was a village in Ontario, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 1,074. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7230787. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.749°N, 79.815°W.
