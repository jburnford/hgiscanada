Trenton, Village, Ontario (1921 census)

Trenton, Village was a village in Ontario, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 5,902. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q2739754. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.113°N, 77.561°W.
