Charleton & Dack, Ontario (1911 census)

Charleton & Dack was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 736. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.137°N, 79.821°W.

Population

In 1911, Charleton & Dack had a population of 736: 313 male and 272 female residents. Population density was 15.3 people per square mile.
