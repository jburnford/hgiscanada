Macpherson & Loudan, Ontario (1911 census)

Macpherson & Loudan was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 459. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.277°N, 80.218°W.

Population

In 1911, Macpherson & Loudan had a population of 459: 70 male and 47 female residents. Population density was 3.3 people per square mile.
