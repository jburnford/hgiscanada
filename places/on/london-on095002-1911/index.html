London, Ontario (1911 census)

London was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 9,785. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.075°N, 81.301°W.

Population

In 1911, London had a population of 9,785: 4,970 male and 4,815 female residents. Population density was 58.3 people per square mile.
