Mariposa, Ontario (1911 census)

Mariposa was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,867. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.304°N, 78.894°W.

Population

In 1911, Mariposa had a population of 3,867: 31 male and 25 female residents. Population density was 0.8 people per square mile.
