Whitchurch, Ontario (1851 census)

Whitchurch was a census subdivision in York County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 4,758. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.014°N, 79.357°W.

Population

In 1851, Whitchurch had a population of 4,758: 2,582 male and 2,176 female residents.
