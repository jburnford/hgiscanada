Saltfleet, Ontario (1851 census)

Saltfleet was a census subdivision in Wentworth County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,801. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7406103. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.203°N, 79.725°W.
