Waterloo S, Ontario (1911 census)

Waterloo S was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,467. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.453°N, 80.437°W.

Population

In 1911, Waterloo S had a population of 3,467: 1,799 male and 1,668 female residents. Population density was 53.5 people per square mile.
