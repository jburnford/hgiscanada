Drummond, Ontario (1911 census)

Drummond was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,764. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261318. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.991°N, 76.237°W.
