Wellesley, Ontario (1911 census)

Wellesley was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 4,757. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7981233. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.545°N, 80.728°W.

Population

In 1911, Wellesley had a population of 4,757: 2,440 male and 2,317 female residents. Population density was 46.0 people per square mile.
