Hardy, McConkey & Patterson, Ontario (1911 census)

Hardy, McConkey & Patterson was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 422. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.081°N, 79.892°W.

Population

In 1911, Hardy, McConkey & Patterson had a population of 422: 276 male and 241 female residents. Population density was 8.8 people per square mile.
