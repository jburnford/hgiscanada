Elzevir, Ontario (1861 census)

Elzevir was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,309. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.708°N, 77.387°W.

Population

In 1861, Elzevir had a population of 1,309: 742 male and 567 female residents.
