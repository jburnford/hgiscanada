Easthope N, Ontario (1861 census)

Easthope N was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 3,129. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.419°N, 80.849°W.

Population

In 1861, Easthope N had a population of 3,129: 1,711 male and 1,418 female residents.
