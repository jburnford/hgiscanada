Thorold, Ontario (1851 census)

Thorold was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,735. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.063°N, 79.235°W.

Population

In 1851, Thorold had a population of 2,735: 1,451 male and 1,284 female residents.
