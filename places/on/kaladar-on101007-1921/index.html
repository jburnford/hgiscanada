Kaladar, Ontario (1921 census)

Kaladar was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 908. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.671°N, 77.161°W.

Population

In 1921, Kaladar had a population of 908: 481 male and 427 female residents. Population density was 8.0 people per square mile.
