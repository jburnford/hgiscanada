Innisfil, Ontario (1911 census)

Innisfil was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,499. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3492080. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.304°N, 79.617°W.

Population

In 1911, Innisfil had a population of 3,499: 1,820 male and 1,679 female residents. Population density was 31.7 people per square mile.
