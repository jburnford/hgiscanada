Owen Sound, Town—Ville, Ontario (1861 census)

Owen Sound, Town—Ville was a town in Grey County, Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,216. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q1017735. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.578°N, 80.922°W.

Population

In 1861, Owen Sound, Town—Ville had a population of 2,216: 1,189 male and 1,027 female residents. Population density was 281.8 people per square mile.
