Dalhousie, Sherbrooke, North Lavant, Ontario (1871 census)

Dalhousie, Sherbrooke, North Lavant was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 2,295. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.038°N, 76.593°W.
