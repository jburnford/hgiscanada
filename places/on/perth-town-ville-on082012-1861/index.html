Perth, Town—Ville, Ontario (1861 census)

Perth, Town—Ville was a township in Lanark County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,465. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.899°N, 76.253°W.

Population

In 1861, Perth, Town—Ville had a population of 2,465: 1,241 male and 1,224 female residents.
