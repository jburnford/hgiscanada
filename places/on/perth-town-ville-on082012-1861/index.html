Perth, Town—Ville, Ontario (1861 census)

Perth, Town—Ville was a town in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,465. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q2428683. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.899°N, 76.253°W.
