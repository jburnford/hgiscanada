Westmeath, Ontario (1911 census)

Westmeath was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,246. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115263132. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.763°N, 76.884°W.
