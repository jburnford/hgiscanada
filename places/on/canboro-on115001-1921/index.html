Canboro, Ontario (1921 census)

Canboro was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 997. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.971°N, 79.657°W.

Population

In 1921, Canboro had a population of 997: 533 male and 464 female residents. Population density was 26.5 people per square mile.
