McKillop, Ontario (1891 census)

McKillop was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 3,086. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.599°N, 81.310°W.

Population

In 1891, McKillop had a population of 3,086: 1,515 male and 1,571 female residents.
