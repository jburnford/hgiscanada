Neebing Centre & S, Ontario (1891 census)

Neebing Centre & S was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,118. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.382°N, 89.348°W.
