Huron, Ontario (1911 census)

Huron was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,951. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261817. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.072°N, 81.617°W.
