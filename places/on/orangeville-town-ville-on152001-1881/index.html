Orangeville, Town—Ville, Ontario (1881 census)

Orangeville, Town—Ville was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,847. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.917°N, 80.102°W.
