Mono, Ontario (1911 census)

Mono was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,677. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q4859513. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.017°N, 80.062°W.
