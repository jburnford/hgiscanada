Uxbridge, Ontario (1861 census)

Uxbridge was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 3,933. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q386959. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.056°N, 79.162°W.
