Tyendinaga, Ontario (1881 census)

Tyendinaga was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 6,162. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7859812. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.274°N, 77.184°W.

Population

In 1881, Tyendinaga had a population of 6,162: 3,097 male and 3,065 female residents.
