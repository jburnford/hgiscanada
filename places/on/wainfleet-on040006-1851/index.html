Wainfleet., Ontario (1851 census)

Wainfleet. was a township in Welland County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,841. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.935°N, 79.397°W.

Population

In 1851, Wainfleet. had a population of 1,841: 989 male and 852 female residents.
