Hamilton, St. Mary's Ward—Quartier, Ontario (1851 census)

Hamilton, St. Mary's Ward—Quartier was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,978. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.267°N, 79.883°W.

Population

In 1851, Hamilton, St. Mary's Ward—Quartier had a population of 2,978: 1,508 male and 1,470 female residents.
