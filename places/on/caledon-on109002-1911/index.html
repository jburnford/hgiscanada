Caledon, Ontario (1911 census)

Caledon was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,773. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q424659. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.855°N, 79.996°W.

Population

In 1911, Caledon had a population of 3,773: 1,949 male and 1,824 female residents. Population density was 35.2 people per square mile.
