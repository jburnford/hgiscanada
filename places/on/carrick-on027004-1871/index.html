Carrick, Ontario (1871 census)

Carrick was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 5,005. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.042°N, 81.105°W.

Population

In 1871, Carrick had a population of 5,005: 2,593 male and 2,412 female residents.
