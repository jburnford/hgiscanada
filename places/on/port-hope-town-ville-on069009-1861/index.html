Port Hope, Town—Ville, Ontario (1861 census)

Port Hope, Town—Ville was a town in Durham County, Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 4,162. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q613870. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.954°N, 78.299°W.
