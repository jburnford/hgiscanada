Onondaga, Ontario (1911 census)

Onondaga was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,104. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.079°N, 80.115°W.

Population

In 1911, Onondaga had a population of 1,104: 589 male and 515 female residents. Population density was 30.6 people per square mile.
