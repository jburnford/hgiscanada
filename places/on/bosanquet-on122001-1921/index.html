Bosanquet, Ontario (1921 census)

Bosanquet was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 2,332. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.169°N, 81.897°W.

Population

In 1921, Bosanquet had a population of 2,332: 1,263 male and 1,069 female residents. Population density was 18.8 people per square mile.
