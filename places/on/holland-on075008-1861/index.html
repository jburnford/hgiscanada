Holland, Ontario (1861 census)

Holland was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,317. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261768. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.405°N, 80.782°W.
