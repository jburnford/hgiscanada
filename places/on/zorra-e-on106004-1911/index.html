Zorra E, Ontario (1911 census)

Zorra E was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,937. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q5329738. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.239°N, 80.791°W.
