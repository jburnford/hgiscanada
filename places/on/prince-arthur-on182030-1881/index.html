Prince Arthur, Ontario (1881 census)

Prince Arthur was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,275. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.416°N, 89.250°W.

Population

In 1881, Prince Arthur had a population of 1,275: 699 male and 576 female residents. Population density was 52.0 people per square mile.
