Tudor, Wollaston, Limerick, Cashel, Ontario (1871 census)

Tudor, Wollaston, Limerick, Cashel was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,055. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.860°N, 77.659°W.
