Brighton, Ontario (1851 census)

Brighton was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,725. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.113°N, 77.757°W.

Population

In 1851, Brighton had a population of 3,725: 1,952 male and 1,773 female residents.
