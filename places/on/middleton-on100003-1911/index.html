Middleton, Ontario (1911 census)

Middleton was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,335. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262318. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.823°N, 80.621°W.
