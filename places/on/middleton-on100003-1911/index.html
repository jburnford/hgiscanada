Middleton, Ontario (1911 census)

Middleton was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,335. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.823°N, 80.621°W.

Population

In 1911, Middleton had a population of 2,335: 1,222 male and 1,113 female residents. Population density was 30.1 people per square mile.
