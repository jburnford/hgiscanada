Dumfries S, Ontario (1861 census)

Dumfries S was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 3,916. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.233°N, 80.333°W.

Population

In 1861, Dumfries S had a population of 3,916: 2,059 male and 1,857 female residents.
