Kingston, C, Ontario (1911 census)

Kingston, C was a city in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 18,874. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q202973. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.236°N, 76.493°W.
