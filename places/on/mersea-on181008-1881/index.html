Mersea, Ontario (1881 census)

Mersea was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 3,552. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.083°N, 82.548°W.

Population

In 1881, Mersea had a population of 3,552: 1,928 male and 1,624 female residents.
