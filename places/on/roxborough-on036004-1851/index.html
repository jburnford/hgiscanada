Roxborough, Ontario (1851 census)

Roxborough was a township in Stormont County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,141. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.226°N, 74.935°W.

Population

In 1851, Roxborough had a population of 2,141: 1,129 male and 1,012 female residents.
