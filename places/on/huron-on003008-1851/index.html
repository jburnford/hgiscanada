Huron, Ontario (1851 census)

Huron was a census subdivision in Bruce County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 236. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261817. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.072°N, 81.617°W.

Population

In 1851, Huron had a population of 236: 135 male and 101 female residents. Population density was 2.4 people per square mile.
