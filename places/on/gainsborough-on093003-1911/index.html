Gainsborough, Ontario (1911 census)

Gainsborough was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,182. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.044°N, 79.487°W.

Population

In 1911, Gainsborough had a population of 2,182: 1,124 male and 1,058 female residents. Population density was 34.3 people per square mile.
