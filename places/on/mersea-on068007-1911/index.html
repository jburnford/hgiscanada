Mersea, Ontario (1911 census)

Mersea was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 4,081. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.083°N, 82.548°W.

Population

In 1911, Mersea had a population of 4,081: 2,150 male and 1,931 female residents. Population density was 37.2 people per square mile.
