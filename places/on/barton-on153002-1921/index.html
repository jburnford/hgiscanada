Barton, Ontario (1921 census)

Barton was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 10,165. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.222°N, 79.861°W.

Population

In 1921, Barton had a population of 10,165: 5,186 male and 4,979 female residents. Population density was 491.9 people per square mile.
