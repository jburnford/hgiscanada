Eldon, Ontario (1861 census)

Eldon was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,508. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q5354067. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.492°N, 78.981°W.
