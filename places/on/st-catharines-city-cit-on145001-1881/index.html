St. Catharines, City—Cité, Ontario (1881 census)

St. Catharines, City—Cité was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 9,631. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.161°N, 79.241°W.
