Portland, Ontario (1861 census)

Portland was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,836. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262612. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.449°N, 76.703°W.
