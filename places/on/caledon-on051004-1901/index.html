Caledon, Ontario (1901 census)

Caledon was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 4,345. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q424659. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.855°N, 79.996°W.
