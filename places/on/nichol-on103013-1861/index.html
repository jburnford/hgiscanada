Nichol, Ontario (1861 census)

Nichol was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,395. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262441. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.686°N, 80.388°W.
