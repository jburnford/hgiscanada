Cayuga S, Ontario (1851 census)

Cayuga S was a township in Haldimand County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,013. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.889°N, 79.759°W.

Population

In 1851, Cayuga S had a population of 2,013: 1,108 male and 905 female residents.
