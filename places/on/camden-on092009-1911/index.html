Camden, Ontario (1911 census)

Camden was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,848. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q5025760. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.402°N, 76.891°W.
