Barrie, T-V, Ontario (1911 census)

Barrie, T-V was a town in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 6,420. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q34183. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.393°N, 79.679°W.

Population

In 1911, Barrie, T-V had a population of 6,420: 3,016 male and 3,404 female residents. Population density was 1411.0 people per square mile.
