Glenelg, Ontario (1871 census)

Glenelg was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 4,060. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.243°N, 80.736°W.

Population

In 1871, Glenelg had a population of 4,060: 2,089 male and 1,971 female residents. Population density was 34.8 people per square mile.
