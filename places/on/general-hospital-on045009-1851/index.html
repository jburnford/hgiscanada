General Hospital, Ontario (1851 census)

General Hospital was a township in Kingston, City County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 32. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.223°N, 76.493°W.

Population

In 1851, General Hospital had a population of 32: 20 male and 12 female residents.
