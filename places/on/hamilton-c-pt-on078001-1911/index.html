Hamilton c pt, Ontario (1911 census)

Hamilton c pt was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 37,279. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.261°N, 79.882°W.

Population

In 1911, Hamilton c pt had a population of 37,279: 18,644 male and 18,635 female residents. Population density was 10530.8 people per square mile.
