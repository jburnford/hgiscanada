Burford, Ontario (1911 census)

Burford was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 4,133. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115260971. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.082°N, 80.489°W.

Population
