Burford, Ontario (1911 census)

Burford was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 4,133. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.082°N, 80.489°W.

Population

In 1911, Burford had a population of 4,133: 2,119 male and 2,014 female residents. Population density was 39.3 people per square mile.
