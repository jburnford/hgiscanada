Trenton, Village, Ontario (1861 census)

Trenton, Village was a township in Hastings County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,398. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.113°N, 77.561°W.

Population

In 1861, Trenton, Village had a population of 1,398: 732 male and 666 female residents.
