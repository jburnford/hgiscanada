Grimsby S, Ontario (1911 census)

Grimsby S was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,389. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.125°N, 79.599°W.

Population

In 1911, Grimsby S had a population of 1,389: 715 male and 674 female residents. Population density was 44.1 people per square mile.
