Nepean, Ontario (1851 census)

Nepean was a census subdivision in Carleton County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,800. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q6994612. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.303°N, 75.778°W.

Population

In 1851, Nepean had a population of 3,800: 1,966 male and 1,834 female residents.
