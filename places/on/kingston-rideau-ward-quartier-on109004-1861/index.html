Kingston, Rideau, Ward—Quartier, Ontario (1861 census)

Kingston, Rideau, Ward—Quartier was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,213. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.236°N, 76.502°W.

Population

In 1861, Kingston, Rideau, Ward—Quartier had a population of 2,213: 1,080 male and 1,133 female residents.
