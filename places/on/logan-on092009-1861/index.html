Logan, Ontario (1861 census)

Logan was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,259. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262070. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.535°N, 81.156°W.

Population

In 1861, Logan had a population of 2,259: 1,138 male and 1,121 female residents.
