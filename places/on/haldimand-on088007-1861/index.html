Haldimand, Ontario (1861 census)

Haldimand was a township in Northumberland County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 6,164. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261662. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.068°N, 78.031°W.

Population

In 1861, Haldimand had a population of 6,164: 3,217 male and 2,947 female residents.
