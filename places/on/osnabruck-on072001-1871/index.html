Osnabruck, Ontario (1871 census)

Osnabruck was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 5,791. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.051°N, 75.027°W.

Population

In 1871, Osnabruck had a population of 5,791: 2,927 male and 2,864 female residents. Population density was 60.4 people per square mile.
