Matilda, Ontario (1861 census)

Matilda was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 4,855. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.916°N, 75.348°W.

Population

In 1861, Matilda had a population of 4,855: 2,432 male and 2,423 female residents.
