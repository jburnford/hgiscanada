Woolwich, Ontario (1861 census)

Woolwich was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 5,256. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q4116337. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.582°N, 80.531°W.
