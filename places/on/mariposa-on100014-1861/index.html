Mariposa, Ontario (1861 census)

Mariposa was a township in Victoria County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 5,506. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.337°N, 78.909°W.

Population

In 1861, Mariposa had a population of 5,506: 2,886 male and 2,617 female residents.
