Arran, Ontario (1911 census)

Arran was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,091. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.463°N, 81.214°W.

Population

In 1911, Arran had a population of 2,091: 1,069 male and 1,022 female residents. Population density was 23.6 people per square mile.
