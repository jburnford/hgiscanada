Brantford E, Ontario (1871 census)

Brantford E was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 3,461. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.162°N, 80.208°W.

Population

In 1871, Brantford E had a population of 3,461: 1,773 male and 1,688 female residents. Population density was 73.9 people per square mile.
