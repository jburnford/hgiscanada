Bangor, McClure & Wicklow, Ontario (1901 census)

Bangor, McClure & Wicklow was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 1,062. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.311°N, 77.902°W.
