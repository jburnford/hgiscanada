Toronto, Ward—Quartier 5 (part), Ontario (1901 census)

Toronto, Ward—Quartier 5 (part) was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 4,338. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.668°N, 79.422°W.

Population

In 1901, Toronto, Ward—Quartier 5 (part) had a population of 4,338: 2,163 male and 2,175 female residents. Population density was 6809.7 people per square mile.
