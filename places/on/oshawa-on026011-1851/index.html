Oshawa, Ontario (1851 census)

Oshawa was a township in Ontario County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,142. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.898°N, 78.861°W.

Population

In 1851, Oshawa had a population of 1,142: 592 male and 550 female residents.
