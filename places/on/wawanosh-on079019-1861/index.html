Wawanosh, Ontario (1861 census)

Wawanosh was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 3,151. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.847°N, 81.475°W.

Population

In 1861, Wawanosh had a population of 3,151: 1,673 male and 1,478 female residents.
