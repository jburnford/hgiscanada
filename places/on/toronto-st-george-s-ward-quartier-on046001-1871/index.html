Toronto, St. George’s, Ward—Quartier, Ontario (1871 census)

Toronto, St. George’s, Ward—Quartier was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 3,534. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.640°N, 79.404°W.

Population

In 1871, Toronto, St. George’s, Ward—Quartier had a population of 3,534: 1,709 male and 1,825 female residents. Population density was 2757.7 people per square mile.
