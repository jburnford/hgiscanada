Winchester, Ontario (1911 census)

Winchester was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,384. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.120°N, 75.292°W.

Population

In 1911, Winchester had a population of 3,384: 1,766 male and 1,618 female residents. Population density was 37.0 people per square mile.
