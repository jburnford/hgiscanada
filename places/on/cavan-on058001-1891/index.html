Cavan, Ontario (1891 census)

Cavan was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 3,106. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.186°N, 78.467°W.

Population

In 1891, Cavan had a population of 3,106: 1,621 male and 1,485 female residents.
