Houghton, Ontario (1851 census)

Houghton was a census subdivision in Norfolk County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,509. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261790. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.656°N, 80.661°W.
