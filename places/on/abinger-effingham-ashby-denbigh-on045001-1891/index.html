Abinger, Effingham, Ashby & Denbigh, Ontario (1891 census)

Abinger, Effingham, Ashby & Denbigh was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 870. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.055°N, 77.303°W.
