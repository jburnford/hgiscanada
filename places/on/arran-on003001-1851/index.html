Arran, Ontario (1851 census)

Arran was a census subdivision in Bruce County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 149. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.463°N, 81.213°W.

Population

In 1851, Arran had a population of 149: 76 male and 73 female residents. Population density was 1.6 people per square mile.
