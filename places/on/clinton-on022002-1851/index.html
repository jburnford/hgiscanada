Clinton, Ontario (1851 census)

Clinton was a census subdivision in Lincoln County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,462. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.145°N, 79.452°W.

Population

In 1851, Clinton had a population of 2,462: 1,256 male and 1,206 female residents.
