Reformatory Prison, Ontario (1861 census)

Reformatory Prison was a township in Simcoe County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 146. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.763°N, 79.929°W.
