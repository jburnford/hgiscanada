Berlin, Town—Ville, Ontario (1911 census)

Berlin, Town—Ville was a town in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 15,196. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.458°N, 80.478°W.

Population

In 1911, Berlin, Town—Ville had a population of 15,196: 7,383 male and 7,813 female residents. Population density was 3101.2 people per square mile.
