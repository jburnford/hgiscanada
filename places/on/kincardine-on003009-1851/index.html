Kincardine, Ontario (1851 census)

Kincardine was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,149. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261919. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.187°N, 81.526°W.
