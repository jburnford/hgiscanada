Morris, Ontario (1921 census)

Morris was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 1,905. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.779°N, 81.309°W.

Population

In 1921, Morris had a population of 1,905: 959 male and 946 female residents. Population density was 20.5 people per square mile.
