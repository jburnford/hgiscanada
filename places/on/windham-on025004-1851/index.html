Windham, Ontario (1851 census)

Windham was a township in Norfolk County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,900. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.915°N, 80.425°W.

Population

In 1851, Windham had a population of 2,900: 1,508 male and 1,392 female residents.
