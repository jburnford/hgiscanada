Euphrasia, Ontario (1851 census)

Euphrasia was a census subdivision in Grey County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 603. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261421. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.434°N, 80.583°W.

Population

In 1851, Euphrasia had a population of 603: 313 male and 290 female residents. Population density was 4.9 people per square mile.
