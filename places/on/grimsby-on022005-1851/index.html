Grimsby, Ontario (1851 census)

Grimsby was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,448. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.149°N, 79.588°W.

Population

In 1851, Grimsby had a population of 2,448: 1,280 male and 1,168 female residents.
