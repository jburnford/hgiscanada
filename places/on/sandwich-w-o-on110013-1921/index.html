Sandwich W-O, Ontario (1921 census)

Sandwich W-O was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 4,598. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.231°N, 83.058°W.

Population

In 1921, Sandwich W-O had a population of 4,598: 2,459 male and 2,139 female residents. Population density was 126.1 people per square mile.
