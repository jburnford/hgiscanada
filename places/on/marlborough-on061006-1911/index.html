Marlborough, Ontario (1911 census)

Marlborough was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,339. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.066°N, 75.807°W.

Population

In 1911, Marlborough had a population of 1,339: 676 male and 663 female residents. Population density was 12.6 people per square mile.
