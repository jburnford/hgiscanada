Raleigh, Ontario (1861 census)

Raleigh was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 3,750. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.306°N, 82.188°W.

Population

In 1861, Raleigh had a population of 3,750: 1,965 male and 1,785 female residents.
