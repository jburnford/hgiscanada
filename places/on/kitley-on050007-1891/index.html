Kitley, Ontario (1891 census)

Kitley was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,336. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.765°N, 75.977°W.

Population

In 1891, Kitley had a population of 2,336: 1,160 male and 1,176 female residents.
