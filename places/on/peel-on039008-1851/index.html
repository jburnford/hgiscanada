Peel, Ontario (1851 census)

Peel was a township in Wellington County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,435. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.722°N, 80.607°W.

Population

In 1851, Peel had a population of 2,435: 1,347 male and 1,088 female residents.
