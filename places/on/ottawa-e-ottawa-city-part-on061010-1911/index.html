Ottawa E Ottawa city part, Ontario (1911 census)

Ottawa E Ottawa city part was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,504. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.413°N, 75.675°W.

Population

In 1911, Ottawa E Ottawa city part had a population of 2,504: 1,268 male and 1,236 female residents. Population density was 3210.2 people per square mile.
