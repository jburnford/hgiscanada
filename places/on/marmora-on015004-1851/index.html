Marmora, Ontario (1851 census)

Marmora was a township in Hastings County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 635. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.541°N, 77.681°W.

Population

In 1851, Marmora had a population of 635: 356 male and 279 female residents.
