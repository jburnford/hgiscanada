Woodstock, Town—Ville, Ontario (1921 census)

Woodstock, Town—Ville was a town in Ontario, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 9,935. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q1642364. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.123°N, 80.755°W.
