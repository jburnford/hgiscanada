Galt, Town—Ville, Ontario (1861 census)

Galt, Town—Ville was a town in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 3,069. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.359°N, 80.318°W.

Population

In 1861, Galt, Town—Ville had a population of 3,069: 1,504 male and 1,565 female residents.
