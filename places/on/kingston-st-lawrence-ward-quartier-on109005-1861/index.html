Kingston, St. Lawrence, Ward—Quartier, Ontario (1861 census)

Kingston, St. Lawrence, Ward—Quartier was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,035. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.231°N, 76.485°W.

In 1861, Kingston, St. Lawrence, Ward—Quartier had a population of 1,035: 518 male and 517 female residents.
