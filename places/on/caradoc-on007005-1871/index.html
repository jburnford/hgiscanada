Caradoc, Ontario (1871 census)

Caradoc was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 5,065. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.885°N, 81.526°W.

Population

In 1871, Caradoc had a population of 5,065: 2,593 male and 2,472 female residents.
