Guelph, Ontario (1911 census)

Guelph was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,641. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.558°N, 80.283°W.

Population

In 1911, Guelph had a population of 2,641: 1,465 male and 1,176 female residents. Population density was 45.7 people per square mile.
