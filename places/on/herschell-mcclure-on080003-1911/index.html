Herschell & McClure, Ontario (1911 census)

Herschell & McClure was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 913. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.201°N, 78.020°W.

Population

In 1911, Herschell & McClure had a population of 913: 340 male and 300 female residents. Population density was 7.0 people per square mile.
