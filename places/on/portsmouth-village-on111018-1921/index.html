Portsmouth, Village, Ontario (1921 census)

Portsmouth, Village was a village in Ontario, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 2,351. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.224°N, 76.518°W.

Population

In 1921, Portsmouth, Village had a population of 2,351: 1,570 male and 781 female residents. Population density was 3220.1 people per square mile.
