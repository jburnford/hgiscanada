Brower, Ontario (1911 census)

Brower was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 31. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.015°N, 80.877°W.

Population

In 1911, Brower had a population of 31: 61 male and 48 female residents. Population density was 1.4 people per square mile.
