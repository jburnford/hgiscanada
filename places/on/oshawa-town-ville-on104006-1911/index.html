Oshawa, Town—Ville, Ontario (1911 census)

Oshawa, Town—Ville was a town in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 7,436. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q211867. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.892°N, 78.856°W.
