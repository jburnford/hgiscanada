Marysburgh, Ontario (1851 census)

Marysburgh was a township in Prince Edward County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,512. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.972°N, 77.003°W.

Population

In 1851, Marysburgh had a population of 3,512: 1,857 male and 1,655 female residents.
