Moore, Ontario (1861 census)

Moore was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,873. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262361. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.837°N, 82.352°W.
