Trenton, Village, Ontario (1891 census)

Trenton, Village was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 4,363. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.113°N, 77.561°W.

Population

In 1891, Trenton, Village had a population of 4,363: 2,153 male and 2,210 female residents.
