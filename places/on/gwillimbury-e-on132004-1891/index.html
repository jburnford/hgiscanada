Gwillimbury E, Ontario (1891 census)

Gwillimbury E was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 3,844. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q483424. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.136°N, 79.410°W.

Population

In 1891, Gwillimbury E had a population of 3,844: 1,930 male and 1,914 female residents. Population density was 37.1 people per square mile.
