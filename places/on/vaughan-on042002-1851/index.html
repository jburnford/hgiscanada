Vaughan, Ontario (1851 census)

Vaughan was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 7,723. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q44013. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.838°N, 79.556°W.

Population

In 1851, Vaughan had a population of 7,723: 4,111 male and 3,612 female residents.
