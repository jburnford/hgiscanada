Ashburnham, Village, Ontario (1871 census)

Ashburnham, Village was a village in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,197. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.308°N, 78.304°W.

Population

In 1871, Ashburnham, Village had a population of 1,197: 607 male and 590 female residents. Population density was 1193.4 people per square mile.
