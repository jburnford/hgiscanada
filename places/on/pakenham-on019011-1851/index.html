Pakenham, Ontario (1851 census)

Pakenham was a census subdivision in Lanark County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,868. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262528. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.305°N, 76.360°W.

Population

In 1851, Pakenham had a population of 1,868: 977 male and 891 female residents. Population density was 17.9 people per square mile.
