Barrie, Town—Ville, Ontario (1871 census)

Barrie, Town—Ville was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 3,398. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.393°N, 79.679°W.
