Elora, Village, Ontario (1861 census)

Elora, Village was a township in Wellington County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,043. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.686°N, 80.424°W.
