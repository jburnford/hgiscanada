Woodstock, Town—Ville, Ontario (1911 census)

Woodstock, Town—Ville was a town in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 9,320. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q1642364. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.125°N, 80.753°W.
