Lobo, Ontario (1911 census)

Lobo was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,327. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262065. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.021°N, 81.476°W.
