Beverley, Ontario (1871 census)

Beverley was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 5,803. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q4899444. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.319°N, 80.145°W.

Population
