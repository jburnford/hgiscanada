Bosanquet, Ontario (1851 census)

Bosanquet was a township in Lambton County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,093. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.171°N, 81.901°W.
