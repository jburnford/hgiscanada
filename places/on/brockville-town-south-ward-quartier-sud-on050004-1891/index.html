Brockville Town, South Ward—Quartier Sud, Ontario (1891 census)

Brockville Town, South Ward—Quartier Sud was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,179. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.584°N, 75.698°W.
