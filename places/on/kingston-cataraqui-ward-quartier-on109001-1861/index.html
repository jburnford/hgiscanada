Kingston, Cataraqui, Ward—Quartier, Ontario (1861 census)

Kingston, Cataraqui, Ward—Quartier was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,812. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.244°N, 76.481°W.

Population

In 1861, Kingston, Cataraqui, Ward—Quartier had a population of 2,812: 1,377 male and 1,435 female residents.
