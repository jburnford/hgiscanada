Cobourg, Town—Ville, Ontario (1891 census)

Cobourg, Town—Ville was a town in Ontario, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 4,829. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q1007418. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.964°N, 78.173°W.
