Herschel, Monteagle, McClure, Wicklow, Bangor, Ontario (1871 census)

Herschel, Monteagle, McClure, Wicklow, Bangor was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 930. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.246°N, 77.904°W.
