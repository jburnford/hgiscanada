Walkerton, Town—Ville, Ontario (1891 census)

Walkerton, Town—Ville was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 3,061. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7962384. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.132°N, 81.149°W.

Population

In 1891, Walkerton, Town—Ville had a population of 3,061: 1,520 male and 1,541 female residents.
