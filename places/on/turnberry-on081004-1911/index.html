Turnberry, Ontario (1911 census)

Turnberry was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,897. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.897°N, 81.245°W.

Population

In 1911, Turnberry had a population of 1,897: 957 male and 940 female residents. Population density was 32.8 people per square mile.
