McGillivray, Ontario (1881 census)

McGillivray was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 4,178. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262232. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.206°N, 81.596°W.

Population

In 1881, McGillivray had a population of 4,178: 2,147 male and 2,031 female residents. Population density was 36.5 people per square mile.
