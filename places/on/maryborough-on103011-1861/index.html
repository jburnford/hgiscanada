Maryborough, Ontario (1861 census)

Maryborough was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 3,134. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262167. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.756°N, 80.746°W.
