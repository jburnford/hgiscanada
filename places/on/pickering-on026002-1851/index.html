Pickering, Ontario (1851 census)

Pickering was a census subdivision in Ontario County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 6,737. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q386626. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.901°N, 79.095°W.

Population

In 1851, Pickering had a population of 6,737: 3,566 male and 3,171 female residents.
