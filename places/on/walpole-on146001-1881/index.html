Walpole, Ontario (1881 census)

Walpole was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 5,854. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115263094. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.888°N, 80.055°W.
