Flamborough E, Ontario (1911 census)

Flamborough E was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,646. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.376°N, 79.952°W.

Population

In 1911, Flamborough E had a population of 2,646: 1,408 male and 1,238 female residents. Population density was 45.6 people per square mile.
