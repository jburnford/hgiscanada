Waterloo, Ontario (1851 census)

Waterloo was a township in Waterloo County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 7,698. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.453°N, 80.439°W.

Population

In 1851, Waterloo had a population of 7,698: 4,096 male and 3,602 female residents.
