Adolphustown, Ontario (1861 census)

Adolphustown was a township in Lennox and Addington County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 801. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.095°N, 77.022°W.

Population

In 1861, Adolphustown had a population of 801: 1,158 male and 1,099 female residents.
