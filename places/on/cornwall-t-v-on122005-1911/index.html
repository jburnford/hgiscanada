Cornwall, T-V, Ontario (1911 census)

Cornwall, T-V was a town in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 6,598. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q956690. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.023°N, 74.729°W.
