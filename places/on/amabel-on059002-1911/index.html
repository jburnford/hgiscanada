Amabel, Ontario (1911 census)

Amabel was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,795. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.638°N, 81.221°W.

Population

In 1911, Amabel had a population of 2,795: 1,459 male and 1,336 female residents. Population density was 19.1 people per square mile.
