Warwick, Ontario (1851 census)

Warwick was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,069. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115263106. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.008°N, 81.901°W.
