Dummer, Ontario (1911 census)

Dummer was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,715. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261330. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.476°N, 78.061°W.
