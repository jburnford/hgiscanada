Monteith, Spence & Christie, Ontario (1891 census)

Monteith, Spence & Christie was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,037. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.442°N, 79.695°W.

Population

In 1891, Monteith, Spence & Christie had a population of 1,037: 568 male and 469 female residents. Population density was 4.3 people per square mile.
