Etobicoke, Ontario (1891 census)

Etobicoke was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 4,557. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.672°N, 79.555°W.

Population

In 1891, Etobicoke had a population of 4,557: 2,487 male and 2,070 female residents.
