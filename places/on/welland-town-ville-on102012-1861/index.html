Welland, Town—Ville, Ontario (1861 census)

Welland, Town—Ville was a township in Welland County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 731. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q424773. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.993°N, 79.242°W.
