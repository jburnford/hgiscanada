Stanley and Bayfield, Ontario (1851 census)

Stanley and Bayfield was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,064. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.514°N, 81.613°W.

Population

In 1851, Stanley and Bayfield had a population of 2,064: 1,117 male and 947 female residents.
