New Edinburgh, Village, Ontario (1881 census)

New Edinburgh, Village was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 995. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.443°N, 75.686°W.

Population

In 1881, New Edinburgh, Village had a population of 995: 499 male and 496 female residents.
