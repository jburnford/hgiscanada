Wawanosh, Ontario (1851 census)

Wawanosh was a census subdivision in Huron County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 722. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.847°N, 81.475°W.

Population

In 1851, Wawanosh had a population of 722: 380 male and 342 female residents.
