Waterloo, Town—Ville, Ontario (1861 census)

Waterloo, Town—Ville was a town in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,273. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q639408. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.466°N, 80.532°W.
