Darlington, Ontario (1871 census)

Darlington was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 5,931. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.972°N, 78.750°W.

Population

In 1871, Darlington had a population of 5,931: 3,007 male and 2,924 female residents.
