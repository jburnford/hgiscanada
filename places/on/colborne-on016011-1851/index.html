Colborne, Ontario (1851 census)

Colborne was a census subdivision in Huron County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 921. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261141. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.764°N, 81.640°W.

Population

In 1851, Colborne had a population of 921: 487 male and 434 female residents. Population density was 15.2 people per square mile.
