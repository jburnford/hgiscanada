Enniskillen, Ontario (1851 census)

Enniskillen was a township in Lambton County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 238. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.845°N, 82.121°W.

Population

In 1851, Enniskillen had a population of 238: 137 male and 101 female residents.
