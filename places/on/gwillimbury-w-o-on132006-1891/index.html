Gwillimbury W-O, Ontario (1891 census)

Gwillimbury W-O was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,525. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.125°N, 79.599°W.

Population

In 1891, Gwillimbury W-O had a population of 2,525: 1,286 male and 1,239 female residents.
