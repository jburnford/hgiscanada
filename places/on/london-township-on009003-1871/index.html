London, Township, Ontario (1871 census)

London, Township was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 10,991. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.072°N, 81.299°W.

Population

In 1871, London, Township had a population of 10,991: 5,630 male and 5,361 female residents.
