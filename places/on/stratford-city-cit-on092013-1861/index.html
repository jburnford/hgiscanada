Stratford, City—Cité, Ontario (1861 census)

Stratford, City—Cité was a city in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,809. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q740756. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.370°N, 80.983°W.
