Elizabethtown, Ontario (1881 census)

Elizabethtown was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 4,905. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.656°N, 75.788°W.

In 1881, Elizabethtown had a population of 4,905: 2,480 male and 2,425 female residents.
