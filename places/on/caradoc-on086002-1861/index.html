Caradoc, Ontario (1861 census)

Caradoc was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 4,422. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261024. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.885°N, 81.526°W.
