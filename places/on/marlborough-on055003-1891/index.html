Marlborough, Ontario (1891 census)

Marlborough was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,703. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.066°N, 75.807°W.

Population

In 1891, Marlborough had a population of 1,703: 878 male and 825 female residents.
