Blenheim, Ontario (1861 census)

Blenheim was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 6,968. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115260874. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.249°N, 80.557°W.
