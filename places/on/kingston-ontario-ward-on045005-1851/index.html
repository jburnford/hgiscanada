Kingston, Ontario Ward, Ontario (1851 census)

Kingston, Ontario Ward was a township in Kingston, City County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,520. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.230°N, 76.485°W.

Population

In 1851, Kingston, Ontario Ward had a population of 1,520: 770 male and 750 female residents.
