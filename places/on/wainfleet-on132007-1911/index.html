Wainfleet, Ontario (1911 census)

Wainfleet was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,764. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q424870. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.935°N, 79.397°W.

Population

In 1911, Wainfleet had a population of 2,764: 1,452 male and 1,312 female residents. Population density was 32.4 people per square mile.
