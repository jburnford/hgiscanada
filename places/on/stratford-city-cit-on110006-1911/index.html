Stratford, City—Cité, Ontario (1911 census)

Stratford, City—Cité was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 12,946. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q740756. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.370°N, 80.982°W.

Population

In 1911, Stratford, City—Cité had a population of 12,946: 6,399 male and 6,547 female residents. Population density was 2922.3 people per square mile.
