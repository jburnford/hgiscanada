Esquesing, Ontario (1891 census)

Esquesing was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 4,435. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q16891177. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.619°N, 79.941°W.
