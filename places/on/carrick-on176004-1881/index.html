Carrick, Ontario (1881 census)

Carrick was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 5,909. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.042°N, 81.105°W.

Population

In 1881, Carrick had a population of 5,909: 3,027 male and 2,882 female residents.
