Kingston, Victoria Ward, Ontario (1851 census)

Kingston, Victoria Ward was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,519. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.226°N, 76.504°W.

Population

In 1851, Kingston, Victoria Ward had a population of 1,519: 720 male and 799 female residents.
