Elizabethtown, Ontario (1911 census)

Elizabethtown was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 4,673. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.656°N, 75.789°W.

Population

In 1911, Elizabethtown had a population of 4,673: 2,325 male and 2,348 female residents. Population density was 36.1 people per square mile.
