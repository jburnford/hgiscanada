Colborne, Ontario (1861 census)

Colborne was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,868. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261141. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.764°N, 81.640°W.
