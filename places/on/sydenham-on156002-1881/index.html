Sydenham, Ontario (1881 census)

Sydenham was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 4,293. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7659765. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.578°N, 80.810°W.

Population

In 1881, Sydenham had a population of 4,293: 2,203 male and 2,090 female residents. Population density was 34.2 people per square mile.
