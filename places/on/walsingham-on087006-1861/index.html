Walsingham, Ontario (1861 census)

Walsingham was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 4,855. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.677°N, 80.512°W.

Population

In 1861, Walsingham had a population of 4,855: 2,747 male and 2,108 female residents.
