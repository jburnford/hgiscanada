Camden, Ontario (1921 census)

Camden was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 2,133. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.584°N, 82.103°W.

Population

In 1921, Camden had a population of 2,133: 1,094 male and 1,039 female residents. Population density was 30.2 people per square mile.
