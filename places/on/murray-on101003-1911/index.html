Murray, Ontario (1911 census)

Murray was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,765. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.148°N, 77.654°W.

Population

In 1911, Murray had a population of 2,765: 1,431 male and 1,334 female residents. Population density was 34.8 people per square mile.
