Gainsborough, Ontario (1851 census)

Gainsborough was a township in Lincoln County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,538. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.044°N, 79.487°W.

Population

In 1851, Gainsborough had a population of 2,538: 1,358 male and 1,180 female residents.
