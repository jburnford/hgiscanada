Upper Canada College, Ontario (1851 census)

Upper Canada College was a census subdivision in Toronto, City County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 92. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.693°N, 79.404°W.

Population

In 1851, Upper Canada College had a population of 92: 54 male and 38 female residents.
