Walpole Island, Ontario (1891 census)

Walpole Island was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 885. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.542°N, 82.642°W.

Population

In 1891, Walpole Island had a population of 885: 450 male and 435 female residents.
