Colchester N, Ontario (1891 census)

Colchester N was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,720. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.133°N, 82.895°W.

Population

In 1891, Colchester N had a population of 1,720: 925 male and 795 female residents.
