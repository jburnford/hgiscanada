Thurlow, Ontario (1851 census)

Thurlow was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 4,469. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.250°N, 77.360°W.

Population

In 1851, Thurlow had a population of 4,469: 2,386 male and 2,083 female residents.
