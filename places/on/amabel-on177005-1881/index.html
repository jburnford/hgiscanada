Amabel, Ontario (1881 census)

Amabel was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 3,046. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.639°N, 81.219°W.

Population

In 1881, Amabel had a population of 3,046: 1,598 male and 1,448 female residents. Population density was 22.2 people per square mile.
