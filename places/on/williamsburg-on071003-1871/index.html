Williamsburg, Ontario (1871 census)

Williamsburg was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 4,724. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.987°N, 75.188°W.

Population

In 1871, Williamsburg had a population of 4,724: 2,408 male and 2,316 female residents.
