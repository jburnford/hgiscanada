Brooke, Ontario (1881 census)

Brooke was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 3,492. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115260945. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.849°N, 81.905°W.

Population

In 1881, Brooke had a population of 3,492: 1,831 male and 1,661 female residents. Population density was 27.6 people per square mile.
