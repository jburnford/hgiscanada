Osgoode, Ontario (1891 census)

Osgoode was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 4,858. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q4337550. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.208°N, 75.515°W.

Population

In 1891, Osgoode had a population of 4,858: 2,519 male and 2,339 female residents.
