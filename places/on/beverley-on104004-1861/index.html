Beverley, Ontario (1861 census)

Beverley was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 6,339. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q4899444. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.319°N, 80.145°W.
