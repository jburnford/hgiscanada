Wallace, Ontario (1911 census)

Wallace was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,508. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115263091. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.781°N, 80.918°W.
