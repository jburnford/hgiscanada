Ottawa, St. George ward-quartier, Ontario (1881 census)

Ottawa, St. George ward-quartier was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 4,527. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.426°N, 75.679°W.

Population

In 1881, Ottawa, St. George ward-quartier had a population of 4,527: 2,115 male and 2,412 female residents. Population density was 5965.9 people per square mile.
