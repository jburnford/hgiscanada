Tyendinaga, Ontario (1891 census)

Tyendinaga was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 5,135. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.270°N, 77.185°W.

Population

In 1891, Tyendinaga had a population of 5,135: 2,659 male and 2,476 female residents.
