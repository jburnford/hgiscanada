Gosfield S, Ontario (1911 census)

Gosfield S was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,412. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.061°N, 82.742°W.

Population

In 1911, Gosfield S had a population of 2,412: 1,240 male and 1,172 female residents. Population density was 57.5 people per square mile.
