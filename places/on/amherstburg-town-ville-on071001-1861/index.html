Amherstburg, Town—Ville, Ontario (1861 census)

Amherstburg, Town—Ville was a town in Essex County, Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,360. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q385335. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.099°N, 83.100°W.

Population

In 1861, Amherstburg, Town—Ville had a population of 2,360: 1,184 male and 1,176 female residents. Population density was 965.2 people per square mile.
