Pelham, Ontario (1911 census)

Pelham was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,815. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q448041. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.040°N, 79.336°W.

Population

In 1911, Pelham had a population of 2,815: 1,499 male and 1,316 female residents. Population density was 57.9 people per square mile.
