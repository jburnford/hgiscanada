Sombra & Walpole Is, Ontario (1911 census)

Sombra & Walpole Is was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 4,774. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.643°N, 82.416°W.
