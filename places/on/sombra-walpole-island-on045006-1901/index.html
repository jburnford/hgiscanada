Sombra & Walpole Island, Ontario (1901 census)

Sombra & Walpole Island was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 5,231. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.643°N, 82.416°W.

Population

In 1901, Sombra & Walpole Island had a population of 5,231: 2,745 male and 2,486 female residents.
