Beckwith, Ontario (1911 census)

Beckwith was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,402. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q55138151. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.086°N, 76.083°W.
