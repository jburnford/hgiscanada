Yarmouth, Ontario (1871 census)

Yarmouth was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 5,563. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.757°N, 81.124°W.

Population

In 1871, Yarmouth had a population of 5,563: 2,853 male and 2,710 female residents.
