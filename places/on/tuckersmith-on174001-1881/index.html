Tuckersmith, Ontario (1881 census)

Tuckersmith was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 3,550. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115263026. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.513°N, 81.456°W.

Population

In 1881, Tuckersmith had a population of 3,550: 1,808 male and 1,742 female residents. Population density was 50.7 people per square mile.
