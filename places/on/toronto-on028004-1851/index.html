Toronto, Ontario (1851 census)

Toronto was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 7,539. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7826543. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.604°N, 79.662°W.

Population

In 1851, Toronto had a population of 7,539: 3,997 male and 3,542 female residents.
