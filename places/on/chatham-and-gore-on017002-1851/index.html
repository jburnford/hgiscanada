Chatham and Gore, Ontario (1851 census)

Chatham and Gore was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,768. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.536°N, 82.233°W.

Population

In 1851, Chatham and Gore had a population of 1,768: 932 male and 836 female residents.
