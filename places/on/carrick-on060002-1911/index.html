Carrick, Ontario (1911 census)

Carrick was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 4,008. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.042°N, 81.105°W.

Population

In 1911, Carrick had a population of 4,008: 1,995 male and 2,013 female residents. Population density was 42.1 people per square mile.
