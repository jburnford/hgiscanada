Lunatic Asylum, Ontario (1851 census)

Lunatic Asylum was a township in Toronto, City County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 351. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.662°N, 79.337°W.

In 1851, Lunatic Asylum had a population of 351: 179 male and 172 female residents.
