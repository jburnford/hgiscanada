Sandwich, Town—Ville, Ontario (1911 census)

Sandwich, Town—Ville was a town in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,302. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.295°N, 83.069°W.

Population

In 1911, Sandwich, Town—Ville had a population of 2,302: 1,175 male and 1,127 female residents. Population density was 735.5 people per square mile.
