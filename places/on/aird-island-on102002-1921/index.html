Aird Island, Ontario (1921 census)

Aird Island was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 343. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.135°N, 82.422°W.

Population

In 1921, Aird Island had a population of 343: 248 male and 95 female residents.
