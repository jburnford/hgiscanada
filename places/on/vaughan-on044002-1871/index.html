Vaughan, Ontario (1871 census)

Vaughan was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 7,657. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q44013. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.838°N, 79.556°W.

Population

In 1871, Vaughan had a population of 7,657: 3,957 male and 3,700 female residents. Population density was 65.2 people per square mile.
