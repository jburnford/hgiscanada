Mosa, Ontario (1851 census)

Mosa was a census subdivision in Middlesex County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,075. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.715°N, 81.772°W.

Population

In 1851, Mosa had a population of 2,075: 1,100 male and 975 female residents.
