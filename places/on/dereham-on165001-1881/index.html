Dereham, Ontario (1881 census)

Dereham was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 4,486. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.928°N, 80.813°W.

Population

In 1881, Dereham had a population of 4,486: 2,322 male and 2,164 female residents.
