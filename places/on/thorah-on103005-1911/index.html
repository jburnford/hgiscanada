Thorah, Ontario (1911 census)

Thorah was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,366. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7796147. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.443°N, 79.148°W.
