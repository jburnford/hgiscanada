Trenton, Village, Ontario (1861 census)

Trenton, Village was a village in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 760. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.601°N, 76.764°W.

Population

In 1861, Trenton, Village had a population of 760: 446 male and 314 female residents.
