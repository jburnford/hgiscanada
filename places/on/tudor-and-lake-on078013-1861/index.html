Tudor and Lake, Ontario (1861 census)

Tudor and Lake was a township in Hastings County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 848. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.845°N, 77.676°W.

Population

In 1861, Tudor and Lake had a population of 848: 548 male and 300 female residents.
