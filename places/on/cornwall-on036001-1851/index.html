Cornwall, Ontario (1851 census)

Cornwall was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 4,707. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.085°N, 74.813°W.

Population

In 1851, Cornwall had a population of 4,707: 2,418 male and 2,289 female residents.
