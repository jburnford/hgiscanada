Osgoode, Ontario (1861 census)

Osgoode was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 4,332. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q4337550. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.208°N, 75.515°W.

Population

In 1861, Osgoode had a population of 4,332: 2,251 male and 2,081 female residents.
