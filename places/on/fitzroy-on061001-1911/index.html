Fitzroy, Ontario (1911 census)

Fitzroy was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,488. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q5455722. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.403°N, 76.207°W.
