Parry Island, Ontario (1901 census)

Parry Island was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 807. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.294°N, 80.147°W.

Population

In 1901, Parry Island had a population of 807: 430 male and 377 female residents.
