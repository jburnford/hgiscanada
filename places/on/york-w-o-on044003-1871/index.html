York, W-O, Ontario (1871 census)

York, W-O was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 5,618. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.720°N, 79.470°W.

Population

In 1871, York, W-O had a population of 5,618: 2,865 male and 2,753 female residents.
