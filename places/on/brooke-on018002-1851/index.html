Brooke, Ontario (1851 census)

Brooke was a township in Lambton County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 511. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.849°N, 81.905°W.

In 1851, Brooke had a population of 511: 269 male and 242 female residents.
