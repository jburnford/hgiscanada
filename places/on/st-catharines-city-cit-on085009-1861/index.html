St. Catharines, City—Cité, Ontario (1861 census)

St. Catharines, City—Cité was a city in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 6,284. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q126805. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.158°N, 79.250°W.
