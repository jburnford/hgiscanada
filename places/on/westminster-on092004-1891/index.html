Westminster, Ontario (1891 census)

Westminster was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 8,506. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.911°N, 81.225°W.

Population

In 1891, Westminster had a population of 8,506: 4,205 male and 4,301 female residents.
