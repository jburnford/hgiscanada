Moulton, Ontario (1911 census)

Moulton was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,818. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262388. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.919°N, 79.540°W.
