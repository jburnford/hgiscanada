Toronto, St. David's Ward—Quartier, Ontario (1881 census)

Toronto, St. David's Ward—Quartier was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 11,072. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.665°N, 79.363°W.
