Logan, Ontario (1851 census)

Logan was a census subdivision in Perth County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 698. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262070. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.535°N, 81.156°W.
