Ottawa, Dalhousie ward-quartier, Ontario (1911 census)

Ottawa, Dalhousie ward-quartier was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 12,647. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.406°N, 75.710°W.

Population

In 1911, Ottawa, Dalhousie ward-quartier had a population of 12,647: 6,357 male and 6,290 female residents. Population density was 18329.0 people per square mile.
