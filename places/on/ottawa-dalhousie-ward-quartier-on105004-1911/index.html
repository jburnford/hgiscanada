Ottawa, Dalhousie ward-quartier, Ontario (1911 census)

Ottawa, Dalhousie ward-quartier was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 12,647. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.406°N, 75.710°W.
