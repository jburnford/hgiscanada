Sophiasburg, Ontario (1891 census)

Sophiasburg was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,341. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.100°N, 77.193°W.

Population

In 1891, Sophiasburg had a population of 2,341: 1,189 male and 1,152 female residents.
