Carrick, Ontario (1861 census)

Carrick was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 3,163. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.042°N, 81.105°W.

Population

In 1861, Carrick had a population of 3,163: 1,676 male and 1,487 female residents.
