Bradford, Village, Ontario (1881 census)

Bradford, Village was a village in Ontario, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,176. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q4954551. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.116°N, 79.564°W.
