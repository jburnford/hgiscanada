Mountain, Ontario (1851 census)

Mountain was a township in Dundas County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,764. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.054°N, 75.458°W.

Population

In 1851, Mountain had a population of 2,764: 1,428 male and 1,336 female residents.
