Adelaide, Ontario (1871 census)

Adelaide was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 2,909. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.000°N, 81.695°W.

Population

In 1871, Adelaide had a population of 2,909: 1,541 male and 1,368 female residents. Population density was 38.4 people per square mile.
