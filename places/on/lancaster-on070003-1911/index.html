Lancaster, Ontario (1911 census)

Lancaster was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,842. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.239°N, 74.486°W.

Population

In 1911, Lancaster had a population of 3,842: 1,961 male and 1,881 female residents. Population density was 40.4 people per square mile.
