Matawin, Ontario (1881 census)

Matawin was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,465. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.530°N, 89.617°W.

Population

In 1881, Matawin had a population of 1,465: 1,085 male and 380 female residents. Population density was 182.3 people per square mile.
