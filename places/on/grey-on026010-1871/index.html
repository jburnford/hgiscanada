Grey, Ontario (1871 census)

Grey was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 3,855. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.720°N, 81.167°W.

Population

In 1871, Grey had a population of 3,855: 2,008 male and 1,847 female residents.
