Medonte, Ontario (1871 census)

Medonte was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 2,541. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.632°N, 79.667°W.

Population

In 1871, Medonte had a population of 2,541: 1,345 male and 1,196 female residents.
