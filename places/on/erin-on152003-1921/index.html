Erin, Ontario (1921 census)

Erin was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 2,797. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3492596. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.744°N, 80.115°W.
