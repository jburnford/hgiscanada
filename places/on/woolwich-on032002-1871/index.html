Woolwich, Ontario (1871 census)

Woolwich was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 5,569. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q4116337. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.582°N, 80.531°W.
