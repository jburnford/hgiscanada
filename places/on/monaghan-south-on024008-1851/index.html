Monaghan, South, Ontario (1851 census)

Monaghan, South was a township in Northumberland County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,051. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.167°N, 78.304°W.

Population

In 1851, Monaghan, South had a population of 1,051: 527 male and 524 female residents.
