Sherbrooke, Ontario (1851 census)

Sherbrooke was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 334. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.859°N, 79.526°W.

Population

In 1851, Sherbrooke had a population of 334: 178 male and 156 female residents.
