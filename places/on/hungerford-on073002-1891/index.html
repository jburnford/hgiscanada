Hungerford, Ontario (1891 census)

Hungerford was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 4,760. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261809. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.461°N, 77.245°W.
