St. Catharines, City—Cité, Ontario (1851 census)

St. Catharines, City—Cité was a city in Lincoln County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 4,368. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q126805. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.158°N, 79.250°W.

Population

In 1851, St. Catharines, City—Cité had a population of 4,368: 2,199 male and 2,169 female residents. Population density was 1800.3 people per square mile.
