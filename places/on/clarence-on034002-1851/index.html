Clarence, Ontario (1851 census)

Clarence was a census subdivision in Russell County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 508. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.476°N, 75.208°W.

Population

In 1851, Clarence had a population of 508: 270 male and 238 female residents. Population density was 4.3 people per square mile.
