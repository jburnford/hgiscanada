Aurora, Town—Ville, Ontario (1891 census)

Aurora, Town—Ville was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,743. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q266438. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.003°N, 79.481°W.

Population

In 1891, Aurora, Town—Ville had a population of 1,743: 854 male and 889 female residents.
