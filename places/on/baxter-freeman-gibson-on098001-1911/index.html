Baxter, Freeman & Gibson, Ontario (1911 census)

Baxter, Freeman & Gibson was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 745. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.999°N, 79.817°W.
