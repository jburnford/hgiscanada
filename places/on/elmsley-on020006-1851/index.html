Elmsley, Ontario (1851 census)

Elmsley was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,442. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.837°N, 76.082°W.

Population

In 1851, Elmsley had a population of 1,442: 740 male and 702 female residents.
