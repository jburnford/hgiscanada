Roxborough, Ontario (1911 census)

Roxborough was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 4,295. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.226°N, 74.935°W.

Population

In 1911, Roxborough had a population of 4,295: 2,171 male and 2,124 female residents. Population density was 37.5 people per square mile.
