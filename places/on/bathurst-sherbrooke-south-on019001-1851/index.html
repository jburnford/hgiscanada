Bathurst & Sherbrooke South, Ontario (1851 census)

Bathurst & Sherbrooke South was a township in Lanark County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,355. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.862°N, 76.440°W.

Population

In 1851, Bathurst & Sherbrooke South had a population of 3,355: 1,699 male and 1,656 female residents.
