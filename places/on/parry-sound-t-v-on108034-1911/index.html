Parry Sound, T-V, Ontario (1911 census)

Parry Sound, T-V was a town in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,429. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q2219667. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.346°N, 80.031°W.
