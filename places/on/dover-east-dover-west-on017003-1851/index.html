Dover East & Dover West, Ontario (1851 census)

Dover East & Dover West was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,723. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.438°N, 82.342°W.

Population

In 1851, Dover East & Dover West had a population of 1,723: 896 male and 827 female residents.
