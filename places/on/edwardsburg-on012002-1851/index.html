Edwardsburg, Ontario (1851 census)

Edwardsburg was a census subdivision in Grenville County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 4,779. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.839°N, 75.502°W.

Population

In 1851, Edwardsburg had a population of 4,779: 2,554 male and 2,225 female residents. Population density was 38.0 people per square mile.
