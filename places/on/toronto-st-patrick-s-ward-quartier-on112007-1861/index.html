Toronto, St. Patrick’s, Ward—Quartier, Ontario (1861 census)

Toronto, St. Patrick’s, Ward—Quartier was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 5,809. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.655°N, 79.411°W.

Population

In 1861, Toronto, St. Patrick’s, Ward—Quartier had a population of 5,809: 2,757 male and 3,052 female residents. Population density was 1988.8 people per square mile.
