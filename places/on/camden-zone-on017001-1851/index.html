Camden & Zone, Ontario (1851 census)

Camden & Zone was a township in Kent County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,434. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.605°N, 81.996°W.

Population

In 1851, Camden & Zone had a population of 1,434: 765 male and 669 female residents.
