Scott, Ontario (1861 census)

Scott was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,169. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7437405. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.182°N, 79.216°W.
