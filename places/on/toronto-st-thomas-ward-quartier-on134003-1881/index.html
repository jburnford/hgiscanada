Toronto, St. Thomas, Ward—Quartier, Ontario (1881 census)

Toronto, St. Thomas, Ward—Quartier was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 8,931. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.662°N, 79.372°W.
