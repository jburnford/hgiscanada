Oakville, Town—Ville, Ontario (1911 census)

Oakville, Town—Ville was a town in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,372. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.460°N, 79.670°W.

Population

In 1911, Oakville, Town—Ville had a population of 2,372: 1,219 male and 1,153 female residents. Population density was 2364.7 people per square mile.
