Kincardine, Ontario (1891 census)

Kincardine was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 3,618. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261919. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.186°N, 81.525°W.

Population

In 1891, Kincardine had a population of 3,618: 1,870 male and 1,748 female residents.
