Fenelon, Ontario (1891 census)

Fenelon was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,809. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.484°N, 78.789°W.

Population

In 1891, Fenelon had a population of 2,809: 1,450 male and 1,359 female residents.
