Fredericksburg N, Ontario (1911 census)

Fredericksburg N was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,502. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.204°N, 76.950°W.

In 1911, Fredericksburg N had a population of 1,502: 772 male and 730 female residents. Population density was 39.1 people per square mile.
