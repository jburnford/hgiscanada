Onondaga, Ontario (1871 census)

Onondaga was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,924. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.119°N, 80.099°W.

Population

In 1871, Onondaga had a population of 1,924: 983 male and 941 female residents.
