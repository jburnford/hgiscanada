Beckwith, Ontario (1861 census)

Beckwith was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,541. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q55138151. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.087°N, 76.084°W.
