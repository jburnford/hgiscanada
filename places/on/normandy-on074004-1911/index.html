Normandy, Ontario (1911 census)

Normandy was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,442. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.063°N, 80.895°W.

Population

In 1911, Normandy had a population of 3,442: 1,767 male and 1,675 female residents. Population density was 31.6 people per square mile.
