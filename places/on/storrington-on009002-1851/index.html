Storrington, Ontario (1851 census)

Storrington was a township in Frontenac County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,130. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.437°N, 76.395°W.

Population

In 1851, Storrington had a population of 2,130: 1,114 male and 1,016 female residents.
