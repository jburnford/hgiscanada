Esquesing, Ontario (1861 census)

Esquesing was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 6,076. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q16891177. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.619°N, 79.942°W.
