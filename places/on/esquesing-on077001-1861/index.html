Esquesing, Ontario (1861 census)

Esquesing was a township in Halton County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 6,076. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.619°N, 79.942°W.

Population

In 1861, Esquesing had a population of 6,076: 3,120 male and 2,956 female residents.
