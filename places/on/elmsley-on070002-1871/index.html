Elmsley, Ontario (1871 census)

Elmsley was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,241. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.837°N, 76.082°W.

Population

In 1871, Elmsley had a population of 1,241: 614 male and 627 female residents. Population density was 25.4 people per square mile.
