Dalhousie & Sherbrooke, North—Nord, Ontario (1891 census)

Dalhousie & Sherbrooke, North—Nord was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,142. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.987°N, 76.548°W.

Population

In 1891, Dalhousie & Sherbrooke, North—Nord had a population of 2,142: 1,066 male and 1,076 female residents. Population density was 16.1 people per square mile.
