Snowden, Ontario (1911 census)

Snowden was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 760. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.878°N, 78.601°W.

Population

In 1911, Snowden had a population of 760: 266 male and 252 female residents. Population density was 11.9 people per square mile.
