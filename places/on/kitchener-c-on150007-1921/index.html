Kitchener, C, Ontario (1921 census)

Kitchener, C was a city in Ontario, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 21,763. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.458°N, 80.478°W.

Population

In 1921, Kitchener, C had a population of 21,763: 10,602 male and 11,161 female residents. Population density was 21695.9 people per square mile.
