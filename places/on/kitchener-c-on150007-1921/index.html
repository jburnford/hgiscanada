Kitchener, C, Ontario (1921 census)

Kitchener, C was a city in Ontario, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 21,763. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q200166. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.458°N, 80.478°W.
