Uxbridge, Ontario (1911 census)

Uxbridge was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,471. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q386959. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.055°N, 79.162°W.

Population

In 1911, Uxbridge had a population of 2,471: 1,265 male and 1,206 female residents. Population density was 29.3 people per square mile.
