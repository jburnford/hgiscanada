Springfield, Village, Ontario (1881 census)

Springfield, Village was a village in Ontario, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 555. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.824°N, 80.930°W.

Population

In 1881, Springfield, Village had a population of 555: 296 male and 259 female residents. Population density was 546.6 people per square mile.
