Digby, Ontario (1861 census)

Digby was a township in Victoria County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 88. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.849°N, 78.965°W.

Population

In 1861, Digby had a population of 88: 66 male and 22 female residents.
