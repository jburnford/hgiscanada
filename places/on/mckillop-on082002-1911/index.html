McKillop, Ontario (1911 census)

McKillop was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,239. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q6801880. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.599°N, 81.310°W.

In 1911, McKillop had a population of 2,239: 1,164 male and 1,075 female residents. Population density was 26.9 people per square mile.
