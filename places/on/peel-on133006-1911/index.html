Peel, Ontario (1911 census)

Peel was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,413. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.722°N, 80.607°W.

In 1911, Peel had a population of 3,413: 1,787 male and 1,626 female residents. Population density was 29.7 people per square mile.
