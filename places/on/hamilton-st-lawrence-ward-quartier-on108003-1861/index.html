Hamilton, St. Lawrence Ward—Quartier, Ontario (1861 census)

Hamilton, St. Lawrence Ward—Quartier was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 4,243. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.263°N, 79.854°W.

Population

In 1861, Hamilton, St. Lawrence Ward—Quartier had a population of 4,243: 2,271 male and 1,972 female residents.
