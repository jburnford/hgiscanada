Nassagaweya, Ontario (1851 census)

Nassagaweya was a census subdivision in Halton County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,237. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.533°N, 80.036°W.

Population

In 1851, Nassagaweya had a population of 2,237: 1,196 male and 1,041 female residents.
