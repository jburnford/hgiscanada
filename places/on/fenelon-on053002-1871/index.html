Fenelon, Ontario (1871 census)

Fenelon was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 3,949. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q5443317. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.485°N, 78.789°W.

Population

In 1871, Fenelon had a population of 3,949: 2,070 male and 1,879 female residents.
