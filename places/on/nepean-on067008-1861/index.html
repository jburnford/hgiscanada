Nepean, Ontario (1861 census)

Nepean was a township in Carleton County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 4,410. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.302°N, 75.779°W.

Population

In 1861, Nepean had a population of 4,410: 2,296 male and 2,114 female residents.
