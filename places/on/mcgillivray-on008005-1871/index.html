McGillivray, Ontario (1871 census)

McGillivray was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 4,658. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262232. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.206°N, 81.596°W.
