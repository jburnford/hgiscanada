Southampton, Village, Ontario (1861 census)

Southampton, Village was a township in Bruce County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 609. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.495°N, 81.359°W.

Population

In 1861, Southampton, Village had a population of 609: 310 male and 299 female residents.
