Euphemia, Ontario (1911 census)

Euphemia was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,846. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.704°N, 81.921°W.

Population

In 1911, Euphemia had a population of 1,846: 949 male and 897 female residents. Population density was 29.6 people per square mile.
