Cumberland, Ontario (1851 census)

Cumberland was a census subdivision in Russell County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,659. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q4210774. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.425°N, 75.385°W.

Population

In 1851, Cumberland had a population of 1,659: 979 male and 680 female residents. Population density was 13.1 people per square mile.
