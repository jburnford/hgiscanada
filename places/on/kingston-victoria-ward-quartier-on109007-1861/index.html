Kingston, Victoria, Ward—Quartier, Ontario (1861 census)

Kingston, Victoria, Ward—Quartier was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,669. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.226°N, 76.501°W.
