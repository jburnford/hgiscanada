Oxford, Ontario (1851 census)

Oxford was a township in Grenville County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 4,496. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.953°N, 75.677°W.

Population

In 1851, Oxford had a population of 4,496: 2,326 male and 2,170 female residents.
