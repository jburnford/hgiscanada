Plympton, Ontario (1871 census)

Plympton was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 5,259. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262605. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.012°N, 82.107°W.

Population

In 1871, Plympton had a population of 5,259: 2,722 male and 2,537 female residents. Population density was 39.7 people per square mile.
