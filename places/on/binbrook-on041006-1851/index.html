Binbrook, Ontario (1851 census)

Binbrook was a census subdivision in Wentworth County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,737. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.119°N, 79.785°W.

Population

In 1851, Binbrook had a population of 1,737: 913 male and 824 female residents. Population density was 38.1 people per square mile.
