Carradoc, Ontario (1851 census)

Carradoc was a township in Middlesex County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,118. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.886°N, 81.527°W.

Population

In 1851, Carradoc had a population of 3,118: 1,646 male and 1,472 female residents.
