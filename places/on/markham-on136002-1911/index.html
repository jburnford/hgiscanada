Markham, Ontario (1911 census)

Markham was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 5,328. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.888°N, 79.317°W.

Population

In 1911, Markham had a population of 5,328: 2,736 male and 2,592 female residents. Population density was 48.0 people per square mile.
