Caledon, Ontario (1851 census)

Caledon was a census subdivision in Peel County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,707. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q424659. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.855°N, 79.996°W.

Population

In 1851, Caledon had a population of 3,707: 1,930 male and 1,777 female residents. Population density was 31.7 people per square mile.
