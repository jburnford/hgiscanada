Edwardsburg, Ontario (1881 census)

Edwardsburg was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 5,431. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.839°N, 75.502°W.

Population

In 1881, Edwardsburg had a population of 5,431: 2,713 male and 2,718 female residents.
