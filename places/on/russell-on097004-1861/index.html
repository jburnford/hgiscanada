Russell, Ontario (1861 census)

Russell was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,814. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q2981843. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.272°N, 75.314°W.
