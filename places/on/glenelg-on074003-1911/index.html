Glenelg, Ontario (1911 census)

Glenelg was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,209. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261586. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.243°N, 80.737°W.

Population

In 1911, Glenelg had a population of 2,209: 1,092 male and 1,117 female residents. Population density was 20.4 people per square mile.
