Camden, Ontario (1871 census)

Camden was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 4,095. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.583°N, 82.102°W.

Population

In 1871, Camden had a population of 4,095: 2,139 male and 1,956 female residents. Population density was 56.3 people per square mile.
