Hope, Ontario (1911 census)

Hope was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,115. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.016°N, 78.390°W.

Population

In 1911, Hope had a population of 3,115: 1,664 male and 1,451 female residents. Population density was 30.4 people per square mile.
