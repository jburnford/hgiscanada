Dorchester S, Ontario (1861 census)

Dorchester S was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,204. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.861°N, 80.984°W.

Population

In 1861, Dorchester S had a population of 2,204: 1,160 male and 1,044 female residents.
