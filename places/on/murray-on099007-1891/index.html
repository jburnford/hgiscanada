Murray, Ontario (1891 census)

Murray was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 3,303. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q136503203. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.148°N, 77.654°W.
