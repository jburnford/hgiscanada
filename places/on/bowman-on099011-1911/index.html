Bowman, Ontario (1911 census)

Bowman was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 221. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.494°N, 80.486°W.

Population

In 1911, Bowman had a population of 221: 255 male and 201 female residents. Population density was 12.7 people per square mile.
