Malahide, Ontario (1911 census)

Malahide was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,509. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262134. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.742°N, 80.944°W.
