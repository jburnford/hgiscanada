Blenheim, Ontario (1851 census)

Blenheim was a census subdivision in Oxford County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 4,995. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115260874. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.249°N, 80.557°W.

Population

In 1851, Blenheim had a population of 4,995: 2,660 male and 2,335 female residents.
