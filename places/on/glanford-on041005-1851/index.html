Glanford, Ontario (1851 census)

Glanford was a township in Wentworth County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,008. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.152°N, 79.892°W.

Population

In 1851, Glanford had a population of 2,008: 1,070 male and 938 female residents.
