Oxford, Ontario (1871 census)

Oxford was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 4,051. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.952°N, 75.677°W.

Population

In 1871, Oxford had a population of 4,051: 2,032 male and 2,019 female residents.
