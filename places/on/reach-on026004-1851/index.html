Reach, Ontario (1851 census)

Reach was a township in Ontario County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,897. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.115°N, 79.002°W.

Population

In 1851, Reach had a population of 3,897: 2,055 male and 1,842 female residents.
