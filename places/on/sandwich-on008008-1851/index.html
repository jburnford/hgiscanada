Sandwich, Ontario (1851 census)

Sandwich was a township in Essex County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 4,928. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.255°N, 82.983°W.

Population

In 1851, Sandwich had a population of 4,928: 2,563 male and 2,365 female residents.
