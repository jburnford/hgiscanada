Wiarton, Village, Ontario (1911 census)

Wiarton, Village was a village in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,266. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3567895. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.726°N, 81.153°W.
