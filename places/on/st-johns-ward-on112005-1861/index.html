St. Johns’ Ward, Ontario (1861 census)

St. Johns’ Ward was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 8,034. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.661°N, 79.387°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Johns’ Ward had a population of 8,034: 3,800 male and 4,234 female residents.
