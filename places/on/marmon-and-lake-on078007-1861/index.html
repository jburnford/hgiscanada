Marmon and Lake, Ontario (1861 census)

Marmon and Lake was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,499. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.581°N, 77.708°W.

Population

In 1861, Marmon and Lake had a population of 1,499: 924 male and 575 female residents.
