Innisfil, Ontario (1891 census)

Innisfil was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 5,110. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3492080. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.304°N, 79.617°W.

Population

In 1891, Innisfil had a population of 5,110: 2,633 male and 2,477 female residents. Population density was 36.5 people per square mile.
