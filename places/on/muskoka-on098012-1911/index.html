Muskoka, Ontario (1911 census)

Muskoka was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 944. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.955°N, 79.379°W.

Population

In 1911, Muskoka had a population of 944: 623 male and 575 female residents.
