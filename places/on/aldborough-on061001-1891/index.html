Aldborough, Ontario (1891 census)

Aldborough was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 5,299. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.586°N, 81.669°W.

Population

In 1891, Aldborough had a population of 5,299: 2,737 male and 2,562 female residents.
