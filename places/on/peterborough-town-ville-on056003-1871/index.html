Peterborough, Town—Ville, Ontario (1871 census)

Peterborough, Town—Ville was a town in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 4,611. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q776930. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.301°N, 78.331°W.

Population

In 1871, Peterborough, Town—Ville had a population of 4,611: 2,293 male and 2,318 female residents. Population density was 1171.9 people per square mile.
