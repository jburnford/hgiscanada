Howard, Ontario (1871 census)

Howard was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 4,512. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.455°N, 81.911°W.

In 1871, Howard had a population of 4,512: 2,379 male and 2,134 female residents.
