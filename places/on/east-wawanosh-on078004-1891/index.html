East Wawanosh, Ontario (1891 census)

East Wawanosh was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,078. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.827°N, 81.425°W.

In 1891, East Wawanosh had a population of 2,078: 1,078 male and 1,000 female residents.
