Plympton, Ontario (1891 census)

Plympton was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 3,929. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262605. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.012°N, 82.107°W.

Population

In 1891, Plympton had a population of 3,929: 2,038 male and 1,891 female residents.
