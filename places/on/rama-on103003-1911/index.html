Rama, Ontario (1911 census)

Rama was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,588. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.709°N, 79.273°W.

Population

In 1911, Rama had a population of 1,588: 886 male and 702 female residents. Population density was 22.3 people per square mile.
