Clarke, Ontario (1871 census)

Clarke was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 5,728. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.992°N, 78.563°W.

Population

In 1871, Clarke had a population of 5,728: 2,980 male and 2,748 female residents. Population density was 47.7 people per square mile.
