Yarmouth, Ontario (1851 census)

Yarmouth was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 5,288. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q102473224. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.757°N, 81.124°W.
