Ottawa, Rideau ward-quartier, Ontario (1911 census)

Ottawa, Rideau ward-quartier was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,943. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.446°N, 75.678°W.
