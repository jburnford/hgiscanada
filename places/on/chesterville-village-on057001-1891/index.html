Chesterville, Village, Ontario (1891 census)

Chesterville, Village was a village in Ontario, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 775. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q17519561. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.097°N, 75.227°W.

Population

In 1891, Chesterville, Village had a population of 775: 390 male and 385 female residents. Population density was 772.9 people per square mile.
