Goulburn, Ontario (1911 census)

Goulburn was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,358. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q4141332. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.191°N, 75.928°W.

Population

In 1911, Goulburn had a population of 2,358: 1,234 male and 1,124 female residents. Population density was 23.3 people per square mile.
