Toronto, St. John’s, Ward—Quartier, Ontario (1891 census)

Toronto, St. John’s, Ward—Quartier was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 13,765. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.661°N, 79.387°W.
