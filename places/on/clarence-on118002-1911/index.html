Clarence, Ontario (1911 census)

Clarence was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 5,463. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.476°N, 75.208°W.

Population

In 1911, Clarence had a population of 5,463: 2,787 male and 2,676 female residents. Population density was 48.3 people per square mile.
