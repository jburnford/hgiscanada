Egremont, Ontario (1911 census)

Egremont was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,060. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261377. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.073°N, 80.695°W.
