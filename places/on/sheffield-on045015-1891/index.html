Sheffield, Ontario (1891 census)

Sheffield was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,355. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.539°N, 77.010°W.

Population

In 1891, Sheffield had a population of 2,355: 1,204 male and 1,151 female residents.
