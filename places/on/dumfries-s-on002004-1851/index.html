Dumfries S, Ontario (1851 census)

Dumfries S was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 4,297. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.233°N, 80.333°W.

Population

In 1851, Dumfries S had a population of 4,297: 2,311 male and 1,986 female residents.
