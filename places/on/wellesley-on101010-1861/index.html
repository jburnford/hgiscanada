Wellesley, Ontario (1861 census)

Wellesley was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 5,888. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7981233. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.545°N, 80.728°W.
