Leeds, Ontario (1851 census)

Leeds was a township in Leeds County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,283. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.430°N, 76.179°W.

Population

In 1851, Leeds had a population of 2,283: 1,192 male and 1,091 female residents.
