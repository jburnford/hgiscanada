Ancaster, Ontario (1881 census)

Ancaster was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 4,726. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q488909. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.204°N, 80.027°W.
