Seymour, Ontario (1911 census)

Seymour was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,331. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7459234. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.328°N, 77.779°W.

Population

In 1911, Seymour had a population of 3,331: 1,905 male and 1,426 female residents. Population density was 29.2 people per square mile.
