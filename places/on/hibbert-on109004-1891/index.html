Hibbert, Ontario (1891 census)

Hibbert was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,636. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261744. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.452°N, 81.331°W.
