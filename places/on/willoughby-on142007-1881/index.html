Willoughby, Ontario (1881 census)

Willoughby was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,273. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q8022201. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.006°N, 79.069°W.

Population

In 1881, Willoughby had a population of 1,273: 638 male and 635 female residents. Population density was 38.8 people per square mile.
