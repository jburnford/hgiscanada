Aldborough, Ontario (1911 census)

Aldborough was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,278. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.586°N, 81.670°W.

Population

In 1911, Aldborough had a population of 3,278: 1,760 male and 1,518 female residents. Population density was 26.7 people per square mile.
