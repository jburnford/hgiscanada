Unorganised, Ontario (1911 census)

Unorganised was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,215. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.729°N, 89.773°W.

Population

In 1911, Unorganised had a population of 3,215: 219 male and 152 female residents. Population density was 11.0 people per square mile.
