Rawdon, Ontario (1911 census)

Rawdon was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,029. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262656. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.376°N, 77.600°W.

Population

In 1911, Rawdon had a population of 3,029: 131 male and 142 female residents. Population density was 3.4 people per square mile.
