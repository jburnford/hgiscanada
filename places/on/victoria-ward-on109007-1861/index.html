Victoria Ward, Ontario (1861 census)

Victoria Ward was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,669. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.226°N, 76.501°W.

Population

In 1861, Victoria Ward had a population of 1,669: 778 male and 891 female residents.
