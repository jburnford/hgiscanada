Gaol, Ontario (1851 census)

Gaol was a census subdivision in Kingston, City County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 21. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.219°N, 76.514°W.

Population

In 1851, Gaol had a population of 21. Population density was 550.9 people per square mile.
