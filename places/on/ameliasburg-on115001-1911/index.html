Ameliasburg, Ontario (1911 census)

Ameliasburg was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,496. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q4742320. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.077°N, 77.442°W.

Population

In 1911, Ameliasburg had a population of 2,496: 1,293 male and 1,203 female residents. Population density was 35.0 people per square mile.
