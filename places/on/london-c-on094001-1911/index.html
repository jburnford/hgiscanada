London, C, Ontario (1911 census)

London, C was a city in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 46,300. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q92561. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.987°N, 81.234°W.

Population

In 1911, London, C had a population of 46,300: 21,901 male and 24,399 female residents. Population density was 6962.4 people per square mile.
