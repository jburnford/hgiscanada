Armstrong, Beauchamp & Bryce, Ontario (1911 census)

Armstrong, Beauchamp & Bryce was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 664. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.711°N, 79.951°W.

Population

In 1911, Armstrong, Beauchamp & Bryce had a population of 664: 280 male and 203 female residents. Population density was 13.5 people per square mile.
