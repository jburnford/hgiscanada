Kingston, Ontario, Ward—Quartier, Ontario (1861 census)

Kingston, Ontario, Ward—Quartier was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,553. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.230°N, 76.485°W.

Population

In 1861, Kingston, Ontario, Ward—Quartier had a population of 1,553: 743 male and 810 female residents.
