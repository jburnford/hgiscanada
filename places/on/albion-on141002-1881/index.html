Albion, Ontario (1881 census)

Albion was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 3,872. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q4712560. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.901°N, 79.813°W.

Population

In 1881, Albion had a population of 3,872: 1,977 male and 1,895 female residents. Population density was 40.7 people per square mile.
