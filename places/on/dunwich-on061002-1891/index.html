Dunwich, Ontario (1891 census)

Dunwich was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 3,663. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261343. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.669°N, 81.493°W.

Population

In 1891, Dunwich had a population of 3,663: 1,915 male and 1,748 female residents.
