Macaulay, Ontario (1891 census)

Macaulay was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 760. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.106°N, 79.264°W.

Population

In 1891, Macaulay had a population of 760: 425 male and 335 female residents.
