Gwillimbury W-O, Ontario (1861 census)

Gwillimbury W-O was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 3,603. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.137°N, 79.611°W.

Population

In 1861, Gwillimbury W-O had a population of 3,603: 1,884 male and 1,719 female residents.
