Petrolia, Town—Ville, Ontario (1911 census)

Petrolia, Town—Ville was a town in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,518. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3089341. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.887°N, 82.141°W.
