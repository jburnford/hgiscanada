Ross, Ontario (1851 census)

Ross was a census subdivision in Renfrew County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 708. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262721. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.632°N, 76.781°W.

In 1851, Ross had a population of 708: 404 male and 304 female residents.
