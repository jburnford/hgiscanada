Lancaster, Ontario (1871 census)

Lancaster was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 4,415. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.238°N, 74.486°W.

Population

In 1871, Lancaster had a population of 4,415: 2,195 male and 2,220 female residents.
