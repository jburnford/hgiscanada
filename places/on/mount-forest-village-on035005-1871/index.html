Mount Forest, Village, Ontario (1871 census)

Mount Forest, Village was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,370. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.978°N, 80.734°W.

In 1871, Mount Forest, Village had a population of 1,370: 687 male and 683 female residents.
