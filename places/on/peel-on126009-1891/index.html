Peel, Ontario (1891 census)

Peel was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 4,253. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.722°N, 80.607°W.

Population

In 1891, Peel had a population of 4,253: 2,225 male and 2,028 female residents.
