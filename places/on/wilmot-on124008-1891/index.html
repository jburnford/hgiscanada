Wilmot, Ontario (1891 census)

Wilmot was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 5,487. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.397°N, 80.651°W.

Population

In 1891, Wilmot had a population of 5,487: 2,764 male and 2,723 female residents. Population density was 51.2 people per square mile.
