Holland, Ontario (1891 census)

Holland was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 3,602. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261768. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.405°N, 80.782°W.

Population

In 1891, Holland had a population of 3,602: 1,893 male and 1,709 female residents.
