Cambridge, Ontario (1911 census)

Cambridge was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,836. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.317°N, 75.132°W.

Population

In 1911, Cambridge had a population of 3,836: 2,009 male and 1,827 female residents. Population density was 40.2 people per square mile.
