Toronto, City, Ontario (1851 census)

Toronto, City was a city in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 30,041. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.654°N, 79.394°W.

Population

In 1851, Toronto, City had a population of 30,041: 14,777 male and 15,264 female residents.
