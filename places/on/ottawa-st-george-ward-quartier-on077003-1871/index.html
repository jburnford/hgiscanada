Ottawa, St. George ward-quartier, Ontario (1871 census)

Ottawa, St. George ward-quartier was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 3,474. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.426°N, 75.679°W.

Population

In 1871, Ottawa, St. George ward-quartier had a population of 3,474: 1,613 male and 1,861 female residents.
