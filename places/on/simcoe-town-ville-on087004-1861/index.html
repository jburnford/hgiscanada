Simcoe, Town—Ville, Ontario (1861 census)

Simcoe, Town—Ville was a town in Norfolk County, Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,858. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3054408. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.840°N, 80.300°W.
