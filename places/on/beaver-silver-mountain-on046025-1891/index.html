Beaver & Silver Mountain, Ontario (1891 census)

Beaver & Silver Mountain was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 685. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.203°N, 89.802°W.
