Grimsby, Ontario (1861 census)

Grimsby was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 3,005. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.149°N, 79.588°W.

Population

In 1861, Grimsby had a population of 3,005: 1,534 male and 1,471 female residents.
