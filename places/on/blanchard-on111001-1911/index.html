Blanchard, Ontario (1911 census)

Blanchard was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,227. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.273°N, 81.230°W.

Population

In 1911, Blanchard had a population of 2,227: 1,175 male and 1,052 female residents. Population density was 29.8 people per square mile.
