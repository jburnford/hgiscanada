York, Ontario (1911 census)

York was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 13,871. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.737°N, 79.426°W.

Population

In 1911, York had a population of 13,871: 7,405 male and 6,466 female residents. Population density was 136.3 people per square mile.
