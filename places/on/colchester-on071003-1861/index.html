Colchester, Ontario (1861 census)

Colchester was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,656. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.084°N, 82.900°W.

Population

In 1861, Colchester had a population of 2,656: 1,376 male and 1,280 female residents.
