Brockville, T-V, Ontario (1911 census)

Brockville, T-V was a town in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 9,374. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q34047. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.595°N, 75.687°W.

Population

In 1911, Brockville, T-V had a population of 9,374: 4,436 male and 4,938 female residents. Population density was 4758.4 people per square mile.
