Ops, Ontario (1851 census)

Ops was a census subdivision in Victoria County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,512. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262505. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.327°N, 78.716°W.

Population

In 1851, Ops had a population of 2,512: 1,279 male and 1,233 female residents. Population density was 24.0 people per square mile.
