Tuckersmith, Ontario (1861 census)

Tuckersmith was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 3,226. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115263026. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.513°N, 81.456°W.
