Waterloo, Town—Ville, Ontario (1911 census)

Waterloo, Town—Ville was a town in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 4,359. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q639408. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.451°N, 80.496°W.
