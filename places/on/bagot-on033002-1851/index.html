Bagot, Ontario (1851 census)

Bagot was a census subdivision in Renfrew County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 734. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.239°N, 76.769°W.

Population

In 1851, Bagot had a population of 734: 411 male and 323 female residents.
