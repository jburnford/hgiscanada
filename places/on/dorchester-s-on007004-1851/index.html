Dorchester S, Ontario (1851 census)

Dorchester S was a township in Elgin County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,477. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.861°N, 80.984°W.

Population

In 1851, Dorchester S had a population of 1,477: 767 male and 710 female residents.
