Monteagle, Ontario (1911 census)

Monteagle was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,161. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262353. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.183°N, 77.821°W.
