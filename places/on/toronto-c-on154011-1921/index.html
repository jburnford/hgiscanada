Toronto, C, Ontario (1921 census)

Toronto, C was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 521,893. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q172. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.671°N, 79.391°W.
