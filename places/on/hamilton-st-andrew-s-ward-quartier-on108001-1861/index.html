Hamilton, St. Andrew’s Ward—Quartier, Ontario (1861 census)

Hamilton, St. Andrew’s Ward—Quartier was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 3,363. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.266°N, 79.865°W.

Population

In 1861, Hamilton, St. Andrew’s Ward—Quartier had a population of 3,363: 1,748 male and 1,615 female residents.
