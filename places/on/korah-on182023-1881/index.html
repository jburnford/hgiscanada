Korah, Ontario (1881 census)

Korah was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 339. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.580°N, 84.408°W.

Population

In 1881, Korah had a population of 339: 170 male and 169 female residents.
