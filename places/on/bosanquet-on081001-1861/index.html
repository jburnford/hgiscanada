Bosanquet, Ontario (1861 census)

Bosanquet was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 3,097. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q4947111. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.171°N, 81.901°W.
