Brant, Ontario (1911 census)

Brant was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,532. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.192°N, 81.145°W.

Population

In 1911, Brant had a population of 3,532: 1,826 male and 1,706 female residents. Population density was 32.0 people per square mile.
