Flamborough W-O, Ontario (1851 census)

Flamborough W-O was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,533. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.323°N, 79.998°W.

Population

In 1851, Flamborough W-O had a population of 3,533: 1,894 male and 1,639 female residents.
