Kingston, Rideau, Ward—Quartier, Ontario (1891 census)

Kingston, Rideau, Ward—Quartier was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 4,464. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.236°N, 76.502°W.

Population

In 1891, Kingston, Rideau, Ward—Quartier had a population of 4,464: 2,127 male and 2,337 female residents.
