Grantham, Ontario (1851 census)

Grantham was a township in Lincoln County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,216. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.175°N, 79.216°W.

Population

In 1851, Grantham had a population of 3,216: 1,662 male and 1,554 female residents.
