Grantham, Ontario (1851 census)

Grantham was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,216. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q5596641. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.175°N, 79.216°W.
