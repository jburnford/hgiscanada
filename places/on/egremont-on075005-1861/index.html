Egremont, Ontario (1861 census)

Egremont was a township in Grey County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,934. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261377. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.073°N, 80.695°W.

Population

In 1861, Egremont had a population of 2,934: 1,522 male and 1,412 female residents.
