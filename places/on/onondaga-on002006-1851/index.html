Onondaga, Ontario (1851 census)

Onondaga was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,858. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.119°N, 80.099°W.

Population

In 1851, Onondaga had a population of 1,858: 1,031 male and 827 female residents.
