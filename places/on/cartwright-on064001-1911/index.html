Cartwright, Ontario (1911 census)

Cartwright was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,584. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.119°N, 78.802°W.

Population

In 1911, Cartwright had a population of 1,584: 803 male and 781 female residents. Population density was 21.6 people per square mile.
