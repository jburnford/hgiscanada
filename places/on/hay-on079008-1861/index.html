Hay, Ontario (1861 census)

Hay was a township in Huron County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 3,054. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.403°N, 81.608°W.

Population

In 1861, Hay had a population of 3,054: 1,581 male and 1,473 female residents.
