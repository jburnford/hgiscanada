Ottawa, Wellington, Ward—Quartier, Ontario (1901 census)

Ottawa, Wellington, Ward—Quartier was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 11,243. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.407°N, 75.697°W.
