Cornwall, Centre Ward—Quartier, Ontario (1881 census)

Cornwall, Centre Ward—Quartier was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,693. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.021°N, 74.725°W.
