Augusta, Ontario (1891 census)

Augusta was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 4,534. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q4820879. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.749°N, 75.646°W.

Population

In 1891, Augusta had a population of 4,534: 2,268 male and 2,266 female residents.
