Dalhousie, Ontario (1861 census)

Dalhousie was a township in Lanark County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,689. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.004°N, 76.524°W.

Population

In 1861, Dalhousie had a population of 1,689: 865 male and 824 female residents.
