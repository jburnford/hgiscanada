Mosa, Ontario (1911 census)

Mosa was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,878. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.716°N, 81.772°W.

Population

In 1911, Mosa had a population of 1,878: 977 male and 901 female residents. Population density was 23.9 people per square mile.
