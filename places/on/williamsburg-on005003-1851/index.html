Williamsburg, Ontario (1851 census)

Williamsburg was a census subdivision in Dundas County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 4,284. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.986°N, 75.188°W.

Population

In 1851, Williamsburg had a population of 4,284: 2,157 male and 2,127 female residents.
