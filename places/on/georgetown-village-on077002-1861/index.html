Georgetown, Village, Ontario (1861 census)

Georgetown, Village was a township in Halton County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,158. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3103735. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.648°N, 79.930°W.

Population

In 1861, Georgetown, Village had a population of 1,158: 593 male and 565 female residents.
