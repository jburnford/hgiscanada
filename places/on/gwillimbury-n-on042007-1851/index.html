Gwillimbury N, Ontario (1851 census)

Gwillimbury N was a township in York County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,176. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.302°N, 79.440°W.

Population

In 1851, Gwillimbury N had a population of 1,176: 598 male and 578 female residents.
