Grattan, Ontario (1911 census)

Grattan was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,977. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261611. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.447°N, 77.051°W.
