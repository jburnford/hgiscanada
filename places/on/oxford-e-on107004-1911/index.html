Oxford E, Ontario (1911 census)

Oxford E was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,042. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.089°N, 80.666°W.

In 1911, Oxford E had a population of 2,042: 1,076 male and 966 female residents. Population density was 33.5 people per square mile.
