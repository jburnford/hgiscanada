Toronto, Ontario (1921 census)

Toronto was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 6,981. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.605°N, 79.662°W.

Population

In 1921, Toronto had a population of 6,981: 3,626 male and 3,355 female residents. Population density was 62.9 people per square mile.
