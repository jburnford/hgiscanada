Ops, Ontario (1861 census)

Ops was a census subdivision in Victoria County, Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,872. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262505. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.325°N, 78.715°W.

Population

In 1861, Ops had a population of 2,872: 1,493 male and 1,379 female residents. Population density was 28.4 people per square mile.
