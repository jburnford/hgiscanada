Mayo, Ontario (1891 census)

Mayo was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 518. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262186. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.085°N, 77.594°W.

In 1891, Mayo had a population of 518: 292 male and 226 female residents.
