Mornington, Ontario (1911 census)

Mornington was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,900. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262370. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.599°N, 80.871°W.

Population
