Collingwood, Town—Ville, Ontario (1861 census)

Collingwood, Town—Ville was a town in Simcoe County, Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,408. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3501912. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.497°N, 80.216°W.

Population

In 1861, Collingwood, Town—Ville had a population of 1,408: 785 male and 623 female residents. Population density was 473.3 people per square mile.
