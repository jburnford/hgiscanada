Brunel & Stephenson, Ontario (1891 census)

Brunel & Stephenson was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,728. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.242°N, 79.246°W.

Population

In 1891, Brunel & Stephenson had a population of 1,728: 921 male and 807 female residents.
