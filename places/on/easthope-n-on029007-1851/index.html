Easthope N, Ontario (1851 census)

Easthope N was a township in Perth County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,341. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.419°N, 80.850°W.

Population

In 1851, Easthope N had a population of 2,341: 1,224 male and 1,117 female residents.
