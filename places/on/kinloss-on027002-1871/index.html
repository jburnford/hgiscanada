Kinloss, Ontario (1871 census)

Kinloss was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 3,430. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261926. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.011°N, 81.452°W.
