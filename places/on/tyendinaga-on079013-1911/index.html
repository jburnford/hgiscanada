Tyendinaga, Ontario (1911 census)

Tyendinaga was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 4,023. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.270°N, 77.185°W.

Population

In 1911, Tyendinaga had a population of 4,023: 2,143 male and 1,880 female residents. Population density was 26.8 people per square mile.
