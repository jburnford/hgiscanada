Madoc, Ontario (1901 census)

Madoc was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 2,790. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q11765886. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.584°N, 77.509°W.
