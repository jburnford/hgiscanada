Nottawasaga, Ontario (1851 census)

Nottawasaga was a township in Simcoe County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,887. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.386°N, 80.166°W.

Population

In 1851, Nottawasaga had a population of 1,887: 1,030 male and 857 female residents.
