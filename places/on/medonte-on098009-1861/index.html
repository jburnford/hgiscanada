Medonte, Ontario (1861 census)

Medonte was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,638. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262292. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.632°N, 79.667°W.
