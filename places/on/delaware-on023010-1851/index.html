Delaware, Ontario (1851 census)

Delaware was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,861. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261257. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.875°N, 81.376°W.
