Delaware, Ontario (1851 census)

Delaware was a census subdivision in Middlesex County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,861. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261257. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.875°N, 81.376°W.

Population

In 1851, Delaware had a population of 1,861: 985 male and 876 female residents. Population density was 36.5 people per square mile.
