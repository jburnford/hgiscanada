McKillop, Ontario (1871 census)

McKillop was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 3,808. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.600°N, 81.309°W.

Population

In 1871, McKillop had a population of 3,808: 1,933 male and 1,875 female residents.
