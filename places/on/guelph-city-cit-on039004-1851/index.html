Guelph, City—Cité, Ontario (1851 census)

Guelph, City—Cité was a city in Wellington County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,860. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q504114. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.547°N, 80.250°W.
