Fredericksburg N, Ontario (1891 census)

Fredericksburg N was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,659. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.204°N, 76.950°W.

Population

In 1891, Fredericksburg N had a population of 1,659: 856 male and 803 female residents.
