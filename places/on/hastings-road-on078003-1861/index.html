Hastings Road, Ontario (1861 census)

Hastings Road was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 676. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.570°N, 77.602°W.

Population

In 1861, Hastings Road had a population of 676: 448 male and 228 female residents.
