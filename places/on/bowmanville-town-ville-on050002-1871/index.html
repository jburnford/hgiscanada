Bowmanville, Town—Ville, Ontario (1871 census)

Bowmanville, Town—Ville was a town in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 3,034. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.915°N, 78.682°W.

Population

In 1871, Bowmanville, Town—Ville had a population of 3,034: 1,475 male and 1,559 female residents. Population density was 465.8 people per square mile.
