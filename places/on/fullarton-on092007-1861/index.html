Fullarton, Ontario (1861 census)

Fullarton was a township in Perth County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,890. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.394°N, 81.201°W.

Population

In 1861, Fullarton had a population of 2,890: 1,494 male and 1,396 female residents.
