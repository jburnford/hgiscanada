Alfred, Ontario (1851 census)

Alfred was a township in Prescott County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 584. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q2834929. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.567°N, 74.883°W.
