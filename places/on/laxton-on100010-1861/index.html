Laxton, Ontario (1861 census)

Laxton was a township in Victoria County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 407. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.707°N, 78.895°W.

Population

In 1861, Laxton had a population of 407: 294 male and 113 female residents.
