Thorah, Ontario (1851 census)

Thorah was a census subdivision in Ontario County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,146. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7796147. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.443°N, 79.148°W.

Population

In 1851, Thorah had a population of 1,146: 583 male and 563 female residents. Population density was 12.1 people per square mile.
