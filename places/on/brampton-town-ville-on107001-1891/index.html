Brampton, Town—Ville, Ontario (1891 census)

Brampton, Town—Ville was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 3,252. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.686°N, 79.765°W.
