Colchester, Ontario (1851 census)

Colchester was a township in Essex County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,870. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.084°N, 82.900°W.

Population

In 1851, Colchester had a population of 1,870: 965 male and 905 female residents.
