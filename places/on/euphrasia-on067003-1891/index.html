Euphrasia, Ontario (1891 census)

Euphrasia was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 3,567. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261421. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.434°N, 80.583°W.
