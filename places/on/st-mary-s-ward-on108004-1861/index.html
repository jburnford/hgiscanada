St. Mary’s Ward, Ontario (1861 census)

St. Mary’s Ward was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 5,056. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.267°N, 79.883°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Mary’s Ward had a population of 5,056: 2,506 male and 2,550 female residents.
