Culross, Ontario (1861 census)

Culross was a township in Bruce County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,266. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.013°N, 81.296°W.

Population

In 1861, Culross had a population of 2,266: 1,227 male and 1,039 female residents.
