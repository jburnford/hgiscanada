Crowland, Ontario (1881 census)

Crowland was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,318. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.998°N, 79.182°W.

Population

In 1881, Crowland had a population of 1,318: 655 male and 663 female residents.
