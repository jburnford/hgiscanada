Billings, Ontario (1911 census)

Billings was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 535. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.859°N, 82.206°W.

Population

In 1911, Billings had a population of 535: 288 male and 247 female residents. Population density was 23.3 people per square mile.
