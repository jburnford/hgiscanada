Pelham, Ontario (1851 census)

Pelham was a township in Welland County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,400. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.040°N, 79.336°W.

Population

In 1851, Pelham had a population of 2,400: 1,248 male and 1,152 female residents.
