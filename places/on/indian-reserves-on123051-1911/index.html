Indian Reserves, Ontario (1911 census)

Indian Reserves was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 4,279. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.892°N, 91.916°W.

Population

In 1911, Indian Reserves had a population of 4,279: 314 male and 134 female residents. Population density was 2.2 people per square mile.
