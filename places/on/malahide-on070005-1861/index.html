Malahide, Ontario (1861 census)

Malahide was a township in Elgin County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 5,320. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.743°N, 80.944°W.

Population

In 1861, Malahide had a population of 5,320: 2,750 male and 2,570 female residents.
