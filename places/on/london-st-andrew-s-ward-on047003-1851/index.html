London, St. Andrew’s Ward, Ontario (1851 census)

London, St. Andrew’s Ward was a township in London County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,083. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.981°N, 81.234°W.
