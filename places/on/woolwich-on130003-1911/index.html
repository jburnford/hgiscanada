Woolwich, Ontario (1911 census)

Woolwich was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 4,051. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q4116337. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.582°N, 80.530°W.
