Ottawa, Central ward-quartier, Ontario (1911 census)

Ottawa, Central ward-quartier was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 10,343. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.416°N, 75.690°W.

Population

In 1911, Ottawa, Central ward-quartier had a population of 10,343: 4,694 male and 5,649 female residents. Population density was 9850.5 people per square mile.
