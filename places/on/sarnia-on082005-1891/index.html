Sarnia, Ontario (1891 census)

Sarnia was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,937. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262762. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.965°N, 82.323°W.
