Camden, Ontario (1911 census)

Camden was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,454. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.584°N, 82.103°W.

Population

In 1911, Camden had a population of 2,454: 1,278 male and 1,176 female residents. Population density was 41.5 people per square mile.
