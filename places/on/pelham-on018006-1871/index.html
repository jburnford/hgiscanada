Pelham, Ontario (1871 census)

Pelham was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 2,515. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q448041. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.040°N, 79.336°W.
