Wallbridge, Ontario (1911 census)

Wallbridge was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,627. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.739°N, 80.457°W.

Population

In 1911, Wallbridge had a population of 1,627: 145 male and 134 female residents. Population density was 3.5 people per square mile.
