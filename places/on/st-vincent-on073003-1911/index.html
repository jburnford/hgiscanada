St. Vincent, Ontario (1911 census)

St. Vincent was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,708. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262893. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.603°N, 80.648°W.
