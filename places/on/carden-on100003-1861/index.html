Carden, Ontario (1861 census)

Carden was a township in Victoria County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 626. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.638°N, 79.048°W.

Population

In 1861, Carden had a population of 626: 405 male and 221 female residents.
