Westminster, Ontario (1861 census)

Westminster was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 6,285. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7989101. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.912°N, 81.225°W.
