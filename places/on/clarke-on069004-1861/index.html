Clarke, Ontario (1861 census)

Clarke was a census subdivision in Durham County, Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 6,575. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.992°N, 78.563°W.

Population

In 1861, Clarke had a population of 6,575: 3,388 male and 3,187 female residents.
