Downie, Ontario (1921 census)

Downie was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 2,457. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.328°N, 81.060°W.

Population

In 1921, Downie had a population of 2,457: 1,313 male and 1,144 female residents. Population density was 29.1 people per square mile.
