Bytown, Centre Ward, Ontario (1851 census)

Bytown, Centre Ward was a census subdivision in Bytown County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,078. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.418°N, 75.696°W.

Population

In 1851, Bytown, Centre Ward had a population of 3,078: 1,591 male and 1,487 female residents.
