Nichol, Ontario (1911 census)

Nichol was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,520. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.686°N, 80.388°W.

Population

In 1911, Nichol had a population of 1,520: 786 male and 734 female residents. Population density was 35.5 people per square mile.
