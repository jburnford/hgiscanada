Palmerston, Ontario (1861 census)

Palmerston was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 127. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.956°N, 76.764°W.

Population

In 1861, Palmerston had a population of 127: 63 male and 64 female residents.
