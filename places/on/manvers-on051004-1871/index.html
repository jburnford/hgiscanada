Manvers, Ontario (1871 census)

Manvers was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 4,114. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.165°N, 78.644°W.

Population

In 1871, Manvers had a population of 4,114: 2,107 male and 2,007 female residents.
