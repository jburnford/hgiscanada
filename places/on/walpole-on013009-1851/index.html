Walpole, Ontario (1851 census)

Walpole was a census subdivision in Haldimand County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,583. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115263094. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.888°N, 80.055°W.

Population

In 1851, Walpole had a population of 3,583: 2,014 male and 1,569 female residents. Population density was 30.4 people per square mile.
