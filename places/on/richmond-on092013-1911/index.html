Richmond, Ontario (1911 census)

Richmond was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,242. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.308°N, 77.034°W.

Population

In 1911, Richmond had a population of 2,242: 1,127 male and 1,115 female residents. Population density was 28.6 people per square mile.
