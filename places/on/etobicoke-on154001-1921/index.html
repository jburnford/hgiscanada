Etobicoke, Ontario (1921 census)

Etobicoke was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 10,445. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.675°N, 79.557°W.

Population

In 1921, Etobicoke had a population of 10,445: 5,536 male and 4,909 female residents.
