Etobicoke, Ontario (1921 census)

Etobicoke was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 10,445. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q1020159. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.675°N, 79.557°W.
