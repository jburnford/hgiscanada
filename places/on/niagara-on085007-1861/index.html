Niagara, Ontario (1861 census)

Niagara was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,400. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.199°N, 79.108°W.

Population

In 1861, Niagara had a population of 2,400: 1,241 male and 1,159 female residents.
