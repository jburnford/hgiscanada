Gainsborough, Ontario (1921 census)

Gainsborough was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 2,117. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.044°N, 79.487°W.

Population

In 1921, Gainsborough had a population of 2,117: 1,079 male and 1,038 female residents. Population density was 29.9 people per square mile.
