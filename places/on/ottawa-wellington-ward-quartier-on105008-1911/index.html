Ottawa, Wellington, Ward—Quartier, Ontario (1911 census)

Ottawa, Wellington, Ward—Quartier was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 12,980. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.411°N, 75.700°W.

Population

In 1911, Ottawa, Wellington, Ward—Quartier had a population of 12,980: 6,039 male and 6,941 female residents. Population density was 13244.9 people per square mile.
