Yonge and Escott, Rear, Ontario (1891 census)

Yonge and Escott, Rear was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,413. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.614°N, 75.951°W.
