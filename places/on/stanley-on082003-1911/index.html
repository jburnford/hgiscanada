Stanley, Ontario (1911 census)

Stanley was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,830. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262896. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.514°N, 81.612°W.
