Toronto, Ontario (1891 census)

Toronto was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 5,528. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.604°N, 79.662°W.

Population

In 1891, Toronto had a population of 5,528: 2,855 male and 2,673 female residents.
