Caledon, Ontario (1861 census)

Caledon was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 4,588. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q424659. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.855°N, 79.996°W.

Population

In 1861, Caledon had a population of 4,588: 2,434 male and 2,154 female residents.
