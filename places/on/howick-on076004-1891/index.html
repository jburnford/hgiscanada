Howick, Ontario (1891 census)

Howick was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 4,439. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q5921913. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.891°N, 81.068°W.

Population

In 1891, Howick had a population of 4,439: 2,262 male and 2,177 female residents.
