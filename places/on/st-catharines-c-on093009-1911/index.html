St. Catharines c, Ontario (1911 census)

St. Catharines c was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 12,484. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.157°N, 79.237°W.

Population

In 1911, St. Catharines c had a population of 12,484: 6,182 male and 6,302 female residents. Population density was 3259.5 people per square mile.
