Hinchinbrook, Ontario (1851 census)

Hinchinbrook was a census subdivision in Frontenac County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 364. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.601°N, 76.764°W.

Population

In 1851, Hinchinbrook had a population of 364: 225 male and 139 female residents. Population density was 2.9 people per square mile.
