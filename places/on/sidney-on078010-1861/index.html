Sidney, Ontario (1861 census)

Sidney was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 5,082. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.208°N, 77.521°W.

Population

In 1861, Sidney had a population of 5,082: 2,601 male and 2,481 female residents.
