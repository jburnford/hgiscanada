Waterloo, Town—Ville, Ontario (1891 census)

Waterloo, Town—Ville was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,941. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.451°N, 80.496°W.
