Medonte, Ontario (1911 census)

Medonte was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,361. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.631°N, 79.668°W.

Population

In 1911, Medonte had a population of 3,361: 1,743 male and 1,618 female residents. Population density was 29.6 people per square mile.
