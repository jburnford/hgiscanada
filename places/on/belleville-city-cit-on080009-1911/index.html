Belleville, City—Cité, Ontario (1911 census)

Belleville, City—Cité was a city in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 9,876. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q34227. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.165°N, 77.378°W.

In 1911, Belleville, City—Cité had a population of 9,876: 2,258 male and 2,077 female residents. Population density was 39.5 people per square mile.
