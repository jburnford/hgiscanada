Medonte, Ontario (1901 census)

Medonte was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 4,451. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.632°N, 79.667°W.

Population

In 1901, Medonte had a population of 4,451: 2,327 male and 2,124 female residents. Population density was 38.0 people per square mile.
