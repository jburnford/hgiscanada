Cavan, Ontario (1881 census)

Cavan was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 3,479. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.186°N, 78.467°W.

Population

In 1881, Cavan had a population of 3,479: 1,796 male and 1,683 female residents. Population density was 32.5 people per square mile.
