Kingston, Frontenac, Ward—Quartier, Ontario (1891 census)

Kingston, Frontenac, Ward—Quartier was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 4,368. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.244°N, 76.490°W.

Population

In 1891, Kingston, Frontenac, Ward—Quartier had a population of 4,368: 2,083 male and 2,285 female residents.
