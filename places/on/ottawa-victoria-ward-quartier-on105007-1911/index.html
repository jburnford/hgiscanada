Ottawa, Victoria, Ward—Quartier, Ontario (1911 census)

Ottawa, Victoria, Ward—Quartier was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,145. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.417°N, 75.713°W.

Population

In 1911, Ottawa, Victoria, Ward—Quartier had a population of 3,145: 1,725 male and 1,420 female residents. Population density was 5155.7 people per square mile.
