Bentinck, Ontario (1911 census)

Bentinck was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,980. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q4890427. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.220°N, 80.941°W.
