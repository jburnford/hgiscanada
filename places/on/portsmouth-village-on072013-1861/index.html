Portsmouth, Village, Ontario (1861 census)

Portsmouth, Village was a township in Frontenac County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 892. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.224°N, 76.518°W.
