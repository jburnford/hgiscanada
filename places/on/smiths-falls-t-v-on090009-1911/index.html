Smiths Falls, T-V, Ontario (1911 census)

Smiths Falls, T-V was a town in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 6,370. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q1938153. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.908°N, 76.021°W.
