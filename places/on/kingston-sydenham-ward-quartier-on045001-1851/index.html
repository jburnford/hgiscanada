Kingston, Sydenham, Ward—Quartier, Ontario (1851 census)

Kingston, Sydenham, Ward—Quartier was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,929. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.226°N, 76.487°W.

In 1851, Kingston, Sydenham, Ward—Quartier had a population of 1,929: 940 male and 989 female residents.
