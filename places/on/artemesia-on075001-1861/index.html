Artemesia, Ontario (1861 census)

Artemesia was a census subdivision in Grey County, Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,575. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.271°N, 80.542°W.

Population

In 1861, Artemesia had a population of 2,575: 1,347 male and 1,228 female residents.
