Ashfield, Ontario (1911 census)

Ashfield was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,630. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115260729. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.931°N, 81.649°W.
