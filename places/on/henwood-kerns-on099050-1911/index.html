Henwood & Kerns, Ontario (1911 census)

Henwood & Kerns was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 596. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.624°N, 79.889°W.

Population

In 1911, Henwood & Kerns had a population of 596: 112 male and 65 female residents. Population density was 4.9 people per square mile.
