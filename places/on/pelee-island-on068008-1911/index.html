Pelee Island, Ontario (1911 census)

Pelee Island was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 830. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7161269. The administrative centroid was at approximately 41.776°N, 82.658°W.
