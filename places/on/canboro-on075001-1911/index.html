Canboro, Ontario (1911 census)

Canboro was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,021. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.971°N, 79.657°W.

Population

In 1911, Canboro had a population of 1,021: 530 male and 491 female residents. Population density was 28.8 people per square mile.
