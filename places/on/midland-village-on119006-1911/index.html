Midland, Village, Ontario (1911 census)

Midland, Village was a village in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 4,663. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q1931996. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.750°N, 79.903°W.
