Yarmouth, Ontario (1911 census)

Yarmouth was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 5,217. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q102473224. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.758°N, 81.124°W.

Population

In 1911, Yarmouth had a population of 5,217: 2,741 male and 2,476 female residents. Population density was 46.1 people per square mile.
