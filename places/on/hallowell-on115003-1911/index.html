Hallowell, Ontario (1911 census)

Hallowell was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,834. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261670. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.990°N, 77.225°W.
