Thorold, Ontario (1871 census)

Thorold was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 2,501. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q448120. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.064°N, 79.235°W.
