Brighton, Ontario (1881 census)

Brighton was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 3,470. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.119°N, 77.758°W.

Population

In 1881, Brighton had a population of 3,470: 1,792 male and 1,678 female residents.
