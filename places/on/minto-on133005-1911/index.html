Minto, Ontario (1911 census)

Minto was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,665. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q2184124. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.911°N, 80.870°W.
