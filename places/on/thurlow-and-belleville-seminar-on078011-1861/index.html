Thurlow and Belleville Seminar, Ontario (1861 census)

Thurlow and Belleville Seminar was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 4,864. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.250°N, 77.360°W.

Population

In 1861, Thurlow and Belleville Seminar had a population of 4,864: 2,523 male and 2,341 female residents.
