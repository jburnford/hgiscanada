Camden, Ontario (1851 census)

Camden was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 6,975. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q5025760. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.401°N, 76.891°W.
