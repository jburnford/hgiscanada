Woodstock, Town—Ville, Ontario (1861 census)

Woodstock, Town—Ville was a town in Oxford County, Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 3,353. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q1642364. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.130°N, 80.753°W.

Population

In 1861, Woodstock, Town—Ville had a population of 3,353: 1,748 male and 1,605 female residents.
