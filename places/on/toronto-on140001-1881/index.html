Toronto, Ontario (1881 census)

Toronto was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 5,873. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.604°N, 79.662°W.

Population

In 1881, Toronto had a population of 5,873: 3,007 male and 2,866 female residents.
