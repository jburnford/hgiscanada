Blezard, McKim (including Sudbury & Broder), Ontario (1891 census)

Blezard, McKim (including Sudbury & Broder) was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,354. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.582°N, 81.008°W.

Population

In 1891, Blezard, McKim (including Sudbury & Broder) had a population of 2,354: 1,369 male and 985 female residents.
