Iroquois Falls, T-V, Ontario (1921 census)

Iroquois Falls, T-V was a town in Ontario, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 1,178. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3154557. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.765°N, 80.694°W.

In 1921, Iroquois Falls, T-V had a population of 1,178: 785 male and 393 female residents. Population density was 1174.4 people per square mile.
