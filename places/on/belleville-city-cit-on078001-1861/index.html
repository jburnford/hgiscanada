Belleville, City—Cité, Ontario (1861 census)

Belleville, City—Cité was a city in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 6,277. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q34227. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.167°N, 77.379°W.
