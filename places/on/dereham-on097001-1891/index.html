Dereham, Ontario (1891 census)

Dereham was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 4,025. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.928°N, 80.813°W.

Population

In 1891, Dereham had a population of 4,025: 2,086 male and 1,939 female residents. Population density was 35.4 people per square mile.
