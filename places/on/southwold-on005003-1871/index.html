Southwold, Ontario (1871 census)

Southwold was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 5,559. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7571481. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.746°N, 81.315°W.

Population

In 1871, Southwold had a population of 5,559: 2,869 male and 2,690 female residents. Population density was 43.6 people per square mile.
