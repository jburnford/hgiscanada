Pilkington, Ontario (1911 census)

Pilkington was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,255. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262592. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.647°N, 80.443°W.
