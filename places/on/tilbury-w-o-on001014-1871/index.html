Tilbury W-O, Ontario (1871 census)

Tilbury W-O was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 2,392. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.241°N, 82.517°W.

Population

In 1871, Tilbury W-O had a population of 2,392: 1,211 male and 1,181 female residents. Population density was 27.8 people per square mile.
