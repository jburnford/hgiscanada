(Source: 1921 Census of Canada, V1T16.)

Ethnic origin (1921). This community's record includes 147 persons of French origin, 79 persons of Finnish origin, 29 persons of British origin (English), 19 persons of British origin (Irish), 6 persons of British origin (Scotch / Scottish), 1 persons of Austrian origin, 1 persons of Scandinavian origin. 30 persons recorded under the official census category "Indian"; corresponds to what is now described as Indigenous (First Nations; in northern enumerations also Inuit) origin.
