Fenelon, Ontario (1911 census)

Fenelon was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,304. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q5443317. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.484°N, 78.789°W.
