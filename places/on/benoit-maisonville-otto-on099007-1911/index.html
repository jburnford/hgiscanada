Benoit, Maisonville & Otto, Ontario (1911 census)

Benoit, Maisonville & Otto was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 223. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.204°N, 80.168°W.
