Zorra W-O, Ontario (1851 census)

Zorra W-O was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,302. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3299874. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.190°N, 80.918°W.
