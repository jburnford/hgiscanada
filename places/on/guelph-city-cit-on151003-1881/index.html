Guelph, City—Cité, Ontario (1881 census)

Guelph, City—Cité was a city in Ontario, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 9,890. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q504114. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.547°N, 80.250°W.
