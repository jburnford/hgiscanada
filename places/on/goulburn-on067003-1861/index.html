Goulburn, Ontario (1861 census)

Goulburn was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,914. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q4141332. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.191°N, 75.928°W.

Population

In 1861, Goulburn had a population of 2,914: 1,447 male and 1,467 female residents.
