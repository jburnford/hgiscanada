Wolford, Ontario (1851 census)

Wolford was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,259. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.854°N, 75.828°W.

Population

In 1851, Wolford had a population of 3,259: 1,639 male and 1,620 female residents.
