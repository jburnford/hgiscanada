Humberstone, Ontario (1911 census)

Humberstone was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,417. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.918°N, 79.195°W.

Population

In 1911, Humberstone had a population of 3,417: 1,860 male and 1,557 female residents. Population density was 65.3 people per square mile.
