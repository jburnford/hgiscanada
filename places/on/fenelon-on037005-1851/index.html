Fenelon, Ontario (1851 census)

Fenelon was a township in Victoria County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 590. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.485°N, 78.789°W.

Population

In 1851, Fenelon had a population of 590: 308 male and 282 female residents.
