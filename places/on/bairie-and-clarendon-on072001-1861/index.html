Bairie and Clarendon, Ontario (1861 census)

Bairie and Clarendon was a census subdivision in Frontenac County, Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 463. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.889°N, 77.015°W.

Population

In 1861, Bairie and Clarendon had a population of 463: 299 male and 164 female residents.
