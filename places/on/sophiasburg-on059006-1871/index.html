Sophiasburg, Ontario (1871 census)

Sophiasburg was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 2,702. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7562942. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.094°N, 77.190°W.
