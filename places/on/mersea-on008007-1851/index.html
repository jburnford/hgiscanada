Mersea, Ontario (1851 census)

Mersea was a township in Essex County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,193. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.082°N, 82.549°W.

Population

In 1851, Mersea had a population of 1,193: 628 male and 565 female residents.
