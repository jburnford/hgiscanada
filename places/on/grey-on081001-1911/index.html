Grey, Ontario (1911 census)

Grey was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,850. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.720°N, 81.166°W.

Population

In 1911, Grey had a population of 2,850: 1,467 male and 1,383 female residents. Population density was 27.9 people per square mile.
