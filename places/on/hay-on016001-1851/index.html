Hay, Ontario (1851 census)

Hay was a census subdivision in Huron County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 985. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115261716. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.403°N, 81.608°W.
