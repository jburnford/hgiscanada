Hamilton, St. Patrick’s Ward—Quartier, Ontario (1851 census)

Hamilton, St. Patrick’s Ward—Quartier was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,128. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.249°N, 79.860°W.

Population

In 1851, Hamilton, St. Patrick’s Ward—Quartier had a population of 3,128: 1,504 male and 1,624 female residents.
