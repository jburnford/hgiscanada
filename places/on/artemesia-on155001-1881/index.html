Artemesia, Ontario (1881 census)

Artemesia was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 4,576. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.271°N, 80.541°W.

Population

In 1881, Artemesia had a population of 4,576: 2,340 male and 2,236 female residents. Population density was 39.4 people per square mile.
