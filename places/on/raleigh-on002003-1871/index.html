Raleigh, Ontario (1871 census)

Raleigh was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 4,081. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.306°N, 82.188°W.

Population

In 1871, Raleigh had a population of 4,081: 2,134 male and 1,947 female residents. Population density was 32.9 people per square mile.
