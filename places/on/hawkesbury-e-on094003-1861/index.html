Hawkesbury E, Ontario (1861 census)

Hawkesbury E was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 4,226. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3128766. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.507°N, 74.488°W.
