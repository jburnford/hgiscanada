Eastnor, Ontario (1911 census)

Eastnor was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,642. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.947°N, 81.274°W.

Population

In 1911, Eastnor had a population of 1,642: 884 male and 758 female residents. Population density was 18.1 people per square mile.
