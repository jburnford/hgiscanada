Chapleau District, Ontario (1911 census)

Chapleau District was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,967. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.800°N, 83.440°W.

Population

In 1911, Chapleau District had a population of 1,967: 432 male and 348 female residents. Population density was 10.8 people per square mile.
