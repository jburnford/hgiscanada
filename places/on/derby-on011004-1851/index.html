Derby, Ontario (1851 census)

Derby was a township in Grey County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 471. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.513°N, 81.017°W.

Population

In 1851, Derby had a population of 471: 250 male and 221 female residents.
