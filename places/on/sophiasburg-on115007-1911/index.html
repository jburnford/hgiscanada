Sophiasburg, Ontario (1911 census)

Sophiasburg was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,943. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7562942. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.100°N, 77.193°W.
