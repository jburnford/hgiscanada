Howick, Ontario (1871 census)

Howick was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 5,417. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q5921913. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.891°N, 81.069°W.

In 1871, Howick had a population of 5,417: 2,861 male and 2,556 female residents.
