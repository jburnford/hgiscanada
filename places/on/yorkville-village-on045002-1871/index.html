Yorkville, Village, Ontario (1871 census)

Yorkville, Village was a village in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 2,203. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.676°N, 79.390°W.

Population

In 1871, Yorkville, Village had a population of 2,203: 1,040 male and 1,163 female residents.
