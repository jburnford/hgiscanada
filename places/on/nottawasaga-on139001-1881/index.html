Nottawasaga, Ontario (1881 census)

Nottawasaga was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 6,971. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262471. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.383°N, 80.165°W.
