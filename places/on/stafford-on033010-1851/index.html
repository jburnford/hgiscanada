Stafford, Ontario (1851 census)

Stafford was a township in Renfrew County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 281. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.728°N, 77.065°W.

Population

In 1851, Stafford had a population of 281: 155 male and 126 female residents.
