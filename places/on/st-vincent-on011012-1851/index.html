St. Vincent, Ontario (1851 census)

St. Vincent was a census subdivision in Grey County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,601. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262893. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.603°N, 80.647°W.

Population

In 1851, St. Vincent had a population of 1,601: 731 male and 870 female residents. Population density was 14.4 people per square mile.
