Other parts, Ontario (1911 census)

Other parts was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 540. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.934°N, 79.605°W.

Population

In 1911, Other parts had a population of 540: 324 male and 229 female residents. Population density was 15.5 people per square mile.
