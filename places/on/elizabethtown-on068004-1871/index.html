Elizabethtown, Ontario (1871 census)

Elizabethtown was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 5,373. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.656°N, 75.788°W.

Population

In 1871, Elizabethtown had a population of 5,373: 2,713 male and 2,660 female residents. Population density was 37.7 people per square mile.
