Adolphustown, Ontario (1871 census)

Adolphustown was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 756. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.095°N, 77.022°W.

Population

In 1871, Adolphustown had a population of 756: 387 male and 369 female residents. Population density was 36.3 people per square mile.
