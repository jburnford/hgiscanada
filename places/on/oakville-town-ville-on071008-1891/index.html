Oakville, Town—Ville, Ontario (1891 census)

Oakville, Town—Ville was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,823. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.460°N, 79.670°W.
