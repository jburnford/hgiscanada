Brantford E, Ontario (1911 census)

Brantford E was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 4,524. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.170°N, 80.223°W.

Population

In 1911, Brantford E had a population of 4,524: 2,367 male and 2,157 female residents. Population density was 82.8 people per square mile.
