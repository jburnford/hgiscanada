Melancthon, Ontario (1861 census)

Melancthon was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,359. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q6811207. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.149°N, 80.295°W.
