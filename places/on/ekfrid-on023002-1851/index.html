Ekfrid, Ontario (1851 census)

Ekfrid was a census subdivision in Middlesex County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,791. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.783°N, 81.629°W.

Population

In 1851, Ekfrid had a population of 1,791: 929 male and 862 female residents. Population density was 19.3 people per square mile.
