Clinton, Village, Ontario (1861 census)

Clinton, Village was a township in Huron County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,000. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.618°N, 81.541°W.

Population

In 1861, Clinton, Village had a population of 1,000: 506 male and 494 female residents.
