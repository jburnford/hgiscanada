Sherbrooke, North, Ontario (1861 census)

Sherbrooke, North was a census subdivision in Lanark County, Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 377. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.935°N, 76.616°W.

Population

In 1861, Sherbrooke, North had a population of 377: 212 male and 165 female residents.
