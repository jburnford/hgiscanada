Sydney, Ontario (1901 census)

Sydney was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 4,438. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.208°N, 77.521°W.

Population

In 1901, Sydney had a population of 4,438: 2,301 male and 2,137 female residents. Population density was 36.9 people per square mile.
