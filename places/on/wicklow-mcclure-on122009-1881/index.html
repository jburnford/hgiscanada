Wicklow, McClure, Ontario (1881 census)

Wicklow, McClure was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 456. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.291°N, 77.980°W.

Population

In 1881, Wicklow, McClure had a population of 456: 251 male and 205 female residents.
