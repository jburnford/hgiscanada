Owen Sound, Town—Ville, Ontario (1871 census)

Owen Sound, Town—Ville was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 3,369. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.578°N, 80.922°W.
