Kenyon, Ontario (1911 census)

Kenyon was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 4,330. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.306°N, 74.763°W.

Population

In 1911, Kenyon had a population of 4,330: 2,163 male and 2,167 female residents. Population density was 32.4 people per square mile.
