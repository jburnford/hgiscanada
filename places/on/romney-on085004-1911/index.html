Romney, Ontario (1911 census)

Romney was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,815. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.154°N, 82.387°W.

Population

In 1911, Romney had a population of 1,815: 2,337 male and 2,063 female residents. Population density was 38.0 people per square mile.
