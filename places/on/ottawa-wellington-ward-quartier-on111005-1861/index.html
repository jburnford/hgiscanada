Ottawa, Wellington, Ward—Quartier, Ontario (1861 census)

Ottawa, Wellington, Ward—Quartier was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,343. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.414°N, 75.701°W.

Population

In 1861, Ottawa, Wellington, Ward—Quartier had a population of 2,343: 1,186 male and 1,157 female residents.
