Melancthon, Ontario (1891 census)

Melancthon was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 3,822. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q6811207. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.148°N, 80.291°W.
