Lindsay, Town—Ville, Ontario (1891 census)

Lindsay, Town—Ville was a town in Ontario, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 6,081. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3241437. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.357°N, 78.749°W.
