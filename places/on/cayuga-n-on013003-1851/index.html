Cayuga N, Ontario (1851 census)

Cayuga N was a census subdivision in Haldimand County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 824. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.944°N, 79.818°W.

Population

In 1851, Cayuga N had a population of 824: 436 male and 388 female residents. Population density was 13.5 people per square mile.
