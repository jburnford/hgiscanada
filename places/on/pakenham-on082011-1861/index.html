Pakenham, Ontario (1861 census)

Pakenham was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,442. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262528. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.305°N, 76.360°W.
