Hawkesbury W-O, Ontario (1851 census)

Hawkesbury W-O was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,665. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.542°N, 74.647°W.

In 1851, Hawkesbury W-O had a population of 2,665: 1,397 male and 1,268 female residents.
