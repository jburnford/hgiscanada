Walsingham, Ontario (1851 census)

Walsingham was a census subdivision in Norfolk County, Ontario, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,090. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.687°N, 80.539°W.

Population

In 1851, Walsingham had a population of 3,090: 1,734 male and 1,356 female residents. Population density was 22.5 people per square mile.
