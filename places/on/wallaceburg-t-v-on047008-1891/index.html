Wallaceburg, T-V, Ontario (1891 census)

Wallaceburg, T-V was a town in Ontario, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,726. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7962941. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.605°N, 82.373°W.

Population

In 1891, Wallaceburg, T-V had a population of 2,726: 1,402 male and 1,324 female residents. Population density was 2717.6 people per square mile.
