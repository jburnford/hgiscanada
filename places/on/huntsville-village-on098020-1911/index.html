Huntsville, Village, Ontario (1911 census)

Huntsville, Village was a village in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,358. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.330°N, 79.226°W.

Population

In 1911, Huntsville, Village had a population of 2,358: 331 male and 302 female residents. Population density was 8.7 people per square mile.
