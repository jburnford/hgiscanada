London, St. George’s Ward, Ontario (1851 census)

London, St. George’s Ward was a township in London County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,212. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.979°N, 81.247°W.

Population

In 1851, London, St. George’s Ward had a population of 1,212: 612 male and 600 female residents.
