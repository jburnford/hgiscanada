Belmont & Methuen, Ontario (1911 census)

Belmont & Methuen was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,931. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.578°N, 77.900°W.

Population

In 1911, Belmont & Methuen had a population of 1,931: 963 male and 861 female residents. Population density was 17.0 people per square mile.
