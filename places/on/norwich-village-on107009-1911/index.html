Norwich, Village, Ontario (1911 census)

Norwich, Village was a village in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,112. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q4115370. The administrative centroid was at approximately 42.992°N, 80.595°W.
