Yonge and Escott, Rear, Ontario (1871 census)

Yonge and Escott, Rear was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,791. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.614°N, 75.951°W.
