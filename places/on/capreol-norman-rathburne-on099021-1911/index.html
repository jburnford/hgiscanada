Capreol, Norman & Rathburne, Ontario (1911 census)

Capreol, Norman & Rathburne was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 645. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.734°N, 80.789°W.
