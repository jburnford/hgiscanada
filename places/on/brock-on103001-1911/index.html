Brock, Ontario (1911 census)

Brock was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,330. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q387094. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.288°N, 79.074°W.

Population

In 1911, Brock had a population of 3,330: 1,694 male and 1,636 female residents. Population density was 29.4 people per square mile.
