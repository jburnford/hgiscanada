Hamilton, Ontario (1851 census)

Hamilton was a township in Northumberland County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 5,008. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.049°N, 78.213°W.

Population

In 1851, Hamilton had a population of 5,008: 2,610 male and 2,398 female residents.
