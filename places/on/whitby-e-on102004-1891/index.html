Whitby E, Ontario (1891 census)

Whitby E was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 3,080. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.955°N, 78.881°W.

Population

In 1891, Whitby E had a population of 3,080: 1,615 male and 1,465 female residents. Population density was 57.3 people per square mile.
