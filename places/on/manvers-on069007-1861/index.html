Manvers, Ontario (1861 census)

Manvers was a census subdivision in Durham County, Ontario, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 4,205. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.165°N, 78.644°W.

Population

In 1861, Manvers had a population of 4,205: 2,260 male and 1,945 female residents.
