Nelson, Ontario (1911 census)

Nelson was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,910. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.407°N, 79.858°W.

Population

In 1911, Nelson had a population of 2,910: 1,572 male and 1,338 female residents. Population density was 37.9 people per square mile.
