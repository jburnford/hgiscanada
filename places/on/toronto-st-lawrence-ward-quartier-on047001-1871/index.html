Toronto, St. Lawrence Ward—Quartier, Ontario (1871 census)

Toronto, St. Lawrence Ward—Quartier was a census subdivision in Ontario, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 3,861. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.651°N, 79.362°W.
