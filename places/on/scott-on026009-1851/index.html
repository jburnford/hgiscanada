Scott, Ontario (1851 census)

Scott was a township in Ontario County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,028. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7437405. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.182°N, 79.216°W.

Population

In 1851, Scott had a population of 1,028: 554 male and 474 female residents.
