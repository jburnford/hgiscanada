Douro, Ontario (1851 census)

Douro was a township in Peterboro County, Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,676. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.405°N, 78.213°W.

Population

In 1851, Douro had a population of 1,676: 877 male and 799 female residents.
