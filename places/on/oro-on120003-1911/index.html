Oro, Ontario (1911 census)

Oro was a township in Ontario (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,485. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q115262509. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.498°N, 79.549°W.

Population

In 1911, Oro had a population of 3,485: 1,818 male and 1,667 female residents. Population density was 29.2 people per square mile.
