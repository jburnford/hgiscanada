431. St. Louis, Saskatchewan (1921 census)

431. St. Louis was a census subdivision in Saskatchewan, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 2,810. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3462775. The administrative centroid was at approximately 52.828°N, 105.790°W.
