499. Mervin, Saskatchewan (1921 census)

499. Mervin was a census subdivision in Saskatchewan, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 1,588. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q6820851. The administrative centroid was at approximately 53.404°N, 108.874°W.
