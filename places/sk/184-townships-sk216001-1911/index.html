184 townships, Saskatchewan (1911 census)

184 townships was a township in Saskatchewan (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 32,262. The administrative centroid was at approximately 51.958°N, 106.655°W.

Population

In 1911, 184 townships had a population of 32,262: 23 male and 13 female residents. Population density was 2.9 people per square mile.
