183. Fertile Belt, Saskatchewan (1921 census)

183. Fertile Belt was a census subdivision in Saskatchewan, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 2,851. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q5445569. The administrative centroid was at approximately 50.694°N, 102.216°W.
