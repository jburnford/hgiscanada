Saskatoon, C, Saskatchewan (1911 census)

Saskatoon, C was a township in Saskatchewan (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 12,004. The administrative centroid was at approximately 52.121°N, 106.666°W.
