376. Eagle Creek, Saskatchewan (1921 census)

376. Eagle Creek was a census subdivision in Saskatchewan, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 2,017. The administrative centroid was at approximately 52.280°N, 107.531°W.
