161. Moosejaw, Saskatchewan (1921 census)

161. Moosejaw was a census subdivision in Saskatchewan, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 2,009. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3020588. The administrative centroid was at approximately 50.442°N, 105.511°W.

Population

In 1921, 161. Moosejaw had a population of 2,009: 1,169 male and 840 female residents. Population density was 6.3 people per square mile.
