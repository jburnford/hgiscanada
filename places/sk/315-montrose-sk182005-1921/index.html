315. Montrose, Saskatchewan (1921 census)

315. Montrose was a census subdivision in Saskatchewan, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 1,542. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q6906492. The administrative centroid was at approximately 51.765°N, 107.042°W.
