Battleford, T-V, Saskatchewan (1911 census)

Battleford, T-V was a township in Saskatchewan (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,335. The administrative centroid was at approximately 52.728°N, 108.306°W.

Population

In 1911, Battleford, T-V had a population of 1,335: 89 male and 63 female residents. Population density was 4.1 people per square mile.
