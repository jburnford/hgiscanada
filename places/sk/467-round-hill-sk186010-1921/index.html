467. Round Hill, Saskatchewan (1921 census)

467. Round Hill was a census subdivision in Saskatchewan, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 1,236. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7371043. The administrative centroid was at approximately 53.059°N, 107.970°W.
