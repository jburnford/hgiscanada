423 townships, Saskatchewan (1911 census)

423 townships was a township in Saskatchewan (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 35,558. The administrative centroid was at approximately 52.553°N, 108.764°W.

Population

In 1911, 423 townships had a population of 35,558: 61 male and 31 female residents. Population density was 2.5 people per square mile.
