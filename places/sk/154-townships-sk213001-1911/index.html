154 townships, Saskatchewan (1911 census)

154 townships was a township in Saskatchewan (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 24,913. The administrative centroid was at approximately 50.278°N, 102.936°W.

Population

In 1911, 154 townships had a population of 24,913: 101 male and 86 female residents. Population density was 5.0 people per square mile.
