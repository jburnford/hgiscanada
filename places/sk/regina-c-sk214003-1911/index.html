Regina, C, Saskatchewan (1911 census)

Regina, C was a township in Saskatchewan (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 30,213. The administrative centroid was at approximately 50.446°N, 104.619°W.

Population

In 1911, Regina, C had a population of 30,213: 94 male and 75 female residents. Population density was 3.7 people per square mile.
