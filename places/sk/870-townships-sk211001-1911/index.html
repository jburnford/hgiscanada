870 townships, Saskatchewan (1911 census)

870 townships was a census subdivision in Saskatchewan, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 67,326. The administrative centroid was at approximately 50.203°N, 107.698°W.

Population

In 1911, 870 townships had a population of 67,326. Population density was 2.2 people per square mile.
