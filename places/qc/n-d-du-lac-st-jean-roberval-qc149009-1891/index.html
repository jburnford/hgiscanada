N.-D. du Lac St. Jean & Roberval, Quebec (1891 census)

N.-D. du Lac St. Jean & Roberval was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,040. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.490°N, 72.257°W.

Population

In 1891, N.-D. du Lac St. Jean & Roberval had a population of 1,040: 560 male and 480 female residents.
