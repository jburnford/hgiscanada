Aylmer, Village, Quebec (1861 census)

Aylmer, Village was a village in Outaouais County, Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,586. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.406°N, 75.853°W.

Population

In 1861, Aylmer, Village had a population of 1,586. Population density was 393.2 people per square mile.
