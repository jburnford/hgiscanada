Bécancour, Quebec (1871 census)

Bécancour was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 2,629. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.332°N, 72.389°W.

Population

In 1871, Bécancour had a population of 2,629: 1,318 male and 1,311 female residents.
