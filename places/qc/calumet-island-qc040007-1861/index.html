Calumet Island, Quebec (1861 census)

Calumet Island was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,050. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3205666. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.739°N, 76.685°W.

Population

In 1861, Calumet Island had a population of 1,050: 576 male and 474 female residents.
