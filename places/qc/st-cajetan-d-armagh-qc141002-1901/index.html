St. Cajetan d'Armagh, Quebec (1901 census)

St. Cajetan d'Armagh was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 1,741. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.758°N, 70.578°W.

Population

In 1901, St. Cajetan d'Armagh had a population of 1,741: 883 male and 858 female residents. Population density was 21.3 people per square mile.
