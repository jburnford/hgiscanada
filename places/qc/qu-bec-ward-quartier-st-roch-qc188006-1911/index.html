Québec, ward-quartier St. Roch, Quebec (1911 census)

Québec, ward-quartier St. Roch was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 9,910. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.818°N, 71.229°W.

Population
