St. Jacques, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Jacques was a township in Leinster County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 4,759. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.957°N, 73.547°W.

Population

In 1851, St. Jacques had a population of 4,759: 2,367 male and 2,392 female residents.
