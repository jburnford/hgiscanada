Chester, West, Quebec (1861 census)

Chester, West was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,876. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.980°N, 71.804°W.

Population

In 1861, Chester, West had a population of 1,876: 978 male and 898 female residents.
