St. Antoine, Ile aux Grues, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Antoine, Ile aux Grues was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 630. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.102°N, 70.519°W.
