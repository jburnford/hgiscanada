Beaumont, Quebec (1871 census)

Beaumont was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,142. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.818°N, 70.986°W.

Population

In 1871, Beaumont had a population of 1,142: 553 male and 589 female residents. Population density was 66.0 people per square mile.
