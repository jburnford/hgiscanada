Ste. Foye, Quebec (1851 census)

Ste. Foye was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,173. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.777°N, 71.294°W.

Population

In 1851, Ste. Foye had a population of 2,173: 1,012 male and 1,161 female residents.
