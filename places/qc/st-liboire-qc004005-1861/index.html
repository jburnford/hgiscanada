St. Liboire, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Liboire was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 960. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.652°N, 72.766°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Liboire had a population of 960: 504 male and 456 female residents.
