Stanfold, Quebec (1911 census)

Stanfold was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,272. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.176°N, 71.889°W.

Population

In 1911, Stanfold had a population of 1,272: 660 male and 612 female residents. Population density was 20.4 people per square mile.
