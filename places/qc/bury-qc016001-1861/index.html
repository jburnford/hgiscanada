Bury, Quebec (1861 census)

Bury was a township in Compton County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 989. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.514°N, 71.461°W.

In 1861, Bury had a population of 989: 764 male and 769 female residents.
