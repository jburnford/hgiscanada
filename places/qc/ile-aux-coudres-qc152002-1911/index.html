Ile aux Coudres, Quebec (1911 census)

Ile aux Coudres was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 980. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.399°N, 70.385°W.

Population

In 1911, Ile aux Coudres had a population of 980: 490 male and 490 female residents. Population density was 76.6 people per square mile.
