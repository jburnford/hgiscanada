St. Luc, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Luc was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,088. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7400713. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.367°N, 73.316°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Luc had a population of 1,088: 557 male and 531 female residents.
