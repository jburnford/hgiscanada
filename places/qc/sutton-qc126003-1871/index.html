Sutton, Quebec (1871 census)

Sutton was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 3,143. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.086°N, 72.588°W.

Population

In 1871, Sutton had a population of 3,143: 1,635 male and 1,508 female residents.
