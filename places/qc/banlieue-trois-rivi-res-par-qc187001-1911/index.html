Banlieue (Trois-Rivières, par.), Quebec (1911 census)

Banlieue (Trois-Rivières, par.) was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,724. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.797°N, 71.248°W.

Population

In 1911, Banlieue (Trois-Rivières, par.) had a population of 2,724: 1,260 male and 1,464 female residents. Population density was 3392.3 people per square mile.
