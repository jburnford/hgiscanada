St. André, Quebec (1881 census)

St. André was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,729. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.662°N, 69.689°W.

Population

In 1881, St. André had a population of 1,729: 864 male and 865 female residents.
