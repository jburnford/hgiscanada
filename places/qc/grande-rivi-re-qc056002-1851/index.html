Grande Rivière, Quebec (1851 census)

Grande Rivière was a census subdivision in Gaspé County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 689. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q142969. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.433°N, 64.528°W.

Population

In 1851, Grande Rivière had a population of 689: 366 male and 323 female residents.
