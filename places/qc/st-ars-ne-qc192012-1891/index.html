St. Arsène, Quebec (1891 census)

St. Arsène was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,213. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.914°N, 69.407°W.

Population

In 1891, St. Arsène had a population of 1,213: 613 male and 600 female residents.
