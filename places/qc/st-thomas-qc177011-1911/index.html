St. Thomas, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Thomas was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,689. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.945°N, 70.539°W.

Population

In 1911, St. Thomas had a population of 2,689: 1,323 male and 1,366 female residents. Population density was 51.4 people per square mile.
