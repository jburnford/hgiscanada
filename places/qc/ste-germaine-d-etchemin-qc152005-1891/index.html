Ste. Germaine d'Etchemin, Quebec (1891 census)

Ste. Germaine d'Etchemin was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,278. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.417°N, 70.494°W.

Population

In 1891, Ste. Germaine d'Etchemin had a population of 1,278: 661 male and 617 female residents.
