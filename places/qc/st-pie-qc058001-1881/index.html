St. Pie, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Pie was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 3,037. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.513°N, 72.897°W.

Population

In 1881, St. Pie had a population of 3,037: 1,515 male and 1,522 female residents. Population density was 69.2 people per square mile.
