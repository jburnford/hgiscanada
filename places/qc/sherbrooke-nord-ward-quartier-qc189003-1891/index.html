Sherbrooke, Nord, Ward—Quartier, Quebec (1891 census)

Sherbrooke, Nord, Ward—Quartier was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,350. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.406°N, 71.907°W.

Population

In 1891, Sherbrooke, Nord, Ward—Quartier had a population of 2,350: 1,161 male and 1,189 female residents.
