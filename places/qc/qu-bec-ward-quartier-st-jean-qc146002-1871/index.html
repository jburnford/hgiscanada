Québec, ward-quartier St. Jean, Quebec (1871 census)

Québec, ward-quartier St. Jean was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 7,913. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.811°N, 71.222°W.

Population

In 1871, Québec, ward-quartier St. Jean had a population of 7,913: 3,641 male and 4,272 female residents. Population density was 56638.6 people per square mile.
