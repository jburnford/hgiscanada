La Visitation, Champlain, Quebec (1851 census)

La Visitation, Champlain was a census subdivision in Champlain County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,923. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.461°N, 72.402°W.

Population

In 1851, La Visitation, Champlain had a population of 1,923: 984 male and 939 female residents.
