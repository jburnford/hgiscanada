St. Jean Port Joli, Quebec (1871 census)

St. Jean Port Joli was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 2,436. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.196°N, 70.249°W.

Population

In 1871, St. Jean Port Joli had a population of 2,436: 1,211 male and 1,225 female residents. Population density was 88.5 people per square mile.
