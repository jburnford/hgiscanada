Québec, Banlieue Centre, Quebec (1881 census)

Québec, Banlieue Centre was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 926. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.797°N, 71.248°W.

Population

In 1881, Québec, Banlieue Centre had a population of 926: 439 male and 487 female residents. Population density was 999.9 people per square mile.
