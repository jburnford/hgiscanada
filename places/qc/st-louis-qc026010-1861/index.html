St. Louis, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Louis was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,697. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.557°N, 69.832°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Louis had a population of 1,697: 840 male and 857 female residents.
