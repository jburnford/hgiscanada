Montmagny, Village, Quebec (1871 census)

Montmagny, Village was a village in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,512. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.982°N, 70.560°W.

Population

In 1871, Montmagny, Village had a population of 1,512: 708 male and 804 female residents. Population density was 1484.2 people per square mile.
