La Baie du Febvre, Quebec (1851 census)

La Baie du Febvre was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,086. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112911889. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.127°N, 72.690°W.

In 1851, La Baie du Febvre had a population of 3,086: 1,546 male and 1,540 female residents.
