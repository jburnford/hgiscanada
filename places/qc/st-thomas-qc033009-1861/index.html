St. Thomas, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Thomas was a census subdivision in Missisquoi County, Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 811. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.052°N, 73.293°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Thomas had a population of 811: 398 male and 413 female residents. Population density was 45.3 people per square mile.
