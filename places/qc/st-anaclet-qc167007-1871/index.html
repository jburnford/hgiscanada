St. Anaclet, Quebec (1871 census)

St. Anaclet was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,173. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q106644325. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.465°N, 68.409°W.
