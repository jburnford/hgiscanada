St. Janvier, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Janvier was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,569. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112912323. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.712°N, 73.927°W.
