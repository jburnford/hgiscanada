Berthier, Quebec (1851 census)

Berthier was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,109. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q2899316. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.918°N, 70.723°W.
