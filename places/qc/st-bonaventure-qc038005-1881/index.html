St. Bonaventure, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Bonaventure was a township in Bonaventure County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,367. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.118°N, 65.491°W.

Population

In 1881, St. Bonaventure had a population of 1,367: 674 male and 693 female residents.
