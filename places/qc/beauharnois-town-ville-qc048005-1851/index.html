Beauharnois, Town—Ville, Quebec (1851 census)

Beauharnois, Town—Ville was a township in Beauharnois County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 874. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.313°N, 73.879°W.

Population

In 1851, Beauharnois, Town—Ville had a population of 874: 451 male and 423 female residents.
