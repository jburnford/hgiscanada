Verchères, Quebec (1851 census)

Verchères was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,063. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.747°N, 73.309°W.

Population

In 1851, Verchères had a population of 3,063: 1,548 male and 1,515 female residents.
