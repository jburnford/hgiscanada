Melbourne & Brompton Gore, Quebec (1901 census)

Melbourne & Brompton Gore was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 1,626. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.565°N, 72.182°W.
