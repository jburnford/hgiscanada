Low, Quebec (1911 census)

Low was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,292. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.831°N, 76.037°W.

Population

In 1911, Low had a population of 1,292: 667 male and 625 female residents. Population density was 11.5 people per square mile.
