St. Hyacinthe, City—Cité, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Hyacinthe, City—Cité was a city in St. Hyacinthe County, Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 3,695. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q141873. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.629°N, 72.954°W.
