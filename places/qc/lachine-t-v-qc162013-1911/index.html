Lachine t-v, Quebec (1911 census)

Lachine t-v was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 10,699. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.440°N, 73.683°W.

Population

In 1911, Lachine t-v had a population of 10,699: 5,361 male and 5,338 female residents. Population density was 5288.7 people per square mile.
