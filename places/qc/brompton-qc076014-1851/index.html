Brompton, Quebec (1851 census)

Brompton was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 749. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.505°N, 72.049°W.

Population

In 1851, Brompton had a population of 749: 439 male and 310 female residents.
