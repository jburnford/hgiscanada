Montréal, ward-quartier St. Laurent, Quebec (1911 census)

Montréal, ward-quartier St. Laurent was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 25,039. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.509°N, 73.570°W.

Population

In 1911, Montréal, ward-quartier St. Laurent had a population of 25,039: 12,491 male and 12,548 female residents. Population density was 53048.7 people per square mile.
