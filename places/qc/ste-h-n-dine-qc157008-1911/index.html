Ste. Hénédine, Quebec (1911 census)

Ste. Hénédine was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,113. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.552°N, 70.986°W.

Population

In 1911, Ste. Hénédine had a population of 1,113: 555 male and 558 female residents. Population density was 66.4 people per square mile.
