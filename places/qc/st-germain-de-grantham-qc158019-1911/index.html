St. Germain de Grantham, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Germain de Grantham was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,181. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.844°N, 72.616°W.
