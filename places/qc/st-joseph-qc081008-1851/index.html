St. Joseph, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Joseph was a census subdivision in Vaudreuil County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,338. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.340°N, 74.089°W.

Population

In 1851, St. Joseph had a population of 2,338: 1,156 male and 1,182 female residents.
