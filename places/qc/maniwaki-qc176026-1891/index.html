Maniwaki, Quebec (1891 census)

Maniwaki was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 966. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.364°N, 76.063°W.

Population

In 1891, Maniwaki had a population of 966: 511 male and 455 female residents.
