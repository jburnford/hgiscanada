St. Ambroise, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Ambroise was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,575. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.883°N, 71.391°W.

Population

In 1851, St. Ambroise had a population of 2,575: 1,329 male and 1,246 female residents.
