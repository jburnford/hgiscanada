St. George de Clarenceville, Quebec (1911 census)

St. George de Clarenceville was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 908. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.060°N, 73.202°W.
