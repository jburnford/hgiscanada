Farnham, Quebec (1881 census)

Farnham was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,305. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.269°N, 72.960°W.

Population

In 1881, Farnham had a population of 1,305: 677 male and 628 female residents. Population density was 34.7 people per square mile.
