Mécatina, Quebec (1881 census)

Mécatina was a census subdivision in Chicoutimi & Saguenay County, Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 410. The administrative centroid was at approximately 50.756°N, 59.272°W.

Population

In 1881, Mécatina had a population of 410: 221 male and 189 female residents. Population density was 1.2 people per square mile.
