Québec, St. Louis, Ward—Quartier, Quebec (1891 census)

Québec, St. Louis, Ward—Quartier was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,835. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.810°N, 71.208°W.

Population

In 1891, Québec, St. Louis, Ward—Quartier had a population of 2,835: 1,190 male and 1,645 female residents. Population density was 15171.7 people per square mile.
