Ste. Julienne, Quebec (1851 census)

Ste. Julienne was a census subdivision in Leinster County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 765. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3464313. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.974°N, 73.729°W.
