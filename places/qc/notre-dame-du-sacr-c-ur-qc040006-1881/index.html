Notre Dame du Sacré Cœur, Quebec (1881 census)

Notre Dame du Sacré Cœur was a township in Rimouski County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 750. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.401°N, 68.580°W.
