St. Roch, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Roch was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,200. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.848°N, 73.619°W.

Population

In 1911, St. Roch had a population of 2,200: 1,080 male and 1,120 female residents. Population density was 54.3 people per square mile.
