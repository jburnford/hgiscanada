Notre Dame de Québec, Quebec (1861 census)

Notre Dame de Québec was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,181. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.797°N, 71.244°W.

Population

In 1861, Notre Dame de Québec had a population of 1,181: 571 male and 610 female residents.
