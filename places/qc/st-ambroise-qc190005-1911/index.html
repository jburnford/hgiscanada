St. Ambroise, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Ambroise was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,020. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.860°N, 71.369°W.

Population

In 1911, St. Ambroise had a population of 2,020: 1,005 male and 1,015 female residents. Population density was 34.7 people per square mile.
