Bigelow, Quebec (1861 census)

Bigelow was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 148. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.019°N, 75.655°W.

Population

In 1861, Bigelow had a population of 148: 663 male and 246 female residents.
