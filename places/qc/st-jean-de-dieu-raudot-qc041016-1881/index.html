St. Jean de Dieu & Raudot, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Jean de Dieu & Raudot was a census subdivision in Temiscouata County, Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,290. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.973°N, 68.957°W.

Population

In 1881, St. Jean de Dieu & Raudot had a population of 1,290: 639 male and 651 female residents. Population density was 10.7 people per square mile.
