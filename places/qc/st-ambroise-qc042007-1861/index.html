St. Ambroise, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Ambroise was a township in Québec County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 3,152. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.874°N, 71.411°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Ambroise had a population of 3,152: 1,646 male and 1,506 female residents.
