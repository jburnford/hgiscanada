Kensington, Quebec (1911 census)

Kensington was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 562. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.371°N, 75.831°W.

Population

In 1911, Kensington had a population of 562: 312 male and 250 female residents. Population density was 5.7 people per square mile.
