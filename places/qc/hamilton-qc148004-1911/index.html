Hamilton, Quebec (1911 census)

Hamilton was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,667. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.118°N, 65.491°W.

Population

In 1911, Hamilton had a population of 3,667: 1,856 male and 1,811 female residents. Population density was 37.0 people per square mile.
