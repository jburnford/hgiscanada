Upton, Quebec (1851 census)

Upton was a census subdivision in Drummond County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,184. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.837°N, 72.710°W.

Population

In 1851, Upton had a population of 2,184: 1,125 male and 1,059 female residents. Population density was 20.2 people per square mile.
