St. Irénée, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Irénée was a township in Charlevoix County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,082. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.582°N, 70.239°W.
