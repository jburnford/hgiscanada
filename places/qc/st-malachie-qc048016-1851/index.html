St. Malachie, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Malachie was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,268. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.115°N, 73.964°W.

Population

In 1851, St. Malachie had a population of 3,268: 1,713 male and 1,555 female residents.
