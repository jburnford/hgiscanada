Charlesbourg, Quebec (1861 census)

Charlesbourg was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,447. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.899°N, 71.306°W.

Population

In 1861, Charlesbourg had a population of 2,447: 1,236 male and 1,211 female residents.
