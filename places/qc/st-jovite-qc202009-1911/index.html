St. Jovite, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Jovite was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,742. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.169°N, 74.581°W.

Population

In 1911, St. Jovite had a population of 1,742: 914 male and 828 female residents. Population density was 17.4 people per square mile.
