Trois Pistoles, Quebec (1891 census)

Trois Pistoles was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,495. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.110°N, 69.145°W.

In 1891, Trois Pistoles had a population of 2,495: 1,214 male and 1,281 female residents.
