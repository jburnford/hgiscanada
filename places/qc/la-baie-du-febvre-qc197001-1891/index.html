La Baie du Febvre, Quebec (1891 census)

La Baie du Febvre was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,055. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.132°N, 72.690°W.
