St. Barnabé, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Barnabé was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,501. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.416°N, 72.879°W.

Population

In 1911, St. Barnabé had a population of 2,501: 1,277 male and 1,224 female residents. Population density was 59.7 people per square mile.
