Leeds, Quebec (1861 census)

Leeds was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,550. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.251°N, 71.306°W.

Population

In 1861, Leeds had a population of 2,550: 1,290 male and 1,260 female residents.
