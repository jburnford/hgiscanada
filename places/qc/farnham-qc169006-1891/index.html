Farnham, Quebec (1891 census)

Farnham was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,520. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.269°N, 72.960°W.

In 1891, Farnham had a population of 1,520: 812 male and 708 female residents.
