Yamachiche, Quebec (1911 census)

Yamachiche was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,845. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.309°N, 72.829°W.

Population

In 1911, Yamachiche had a population of 1,845: 945 male and 900 female residents. Population density was 56.5 people per square mile.
