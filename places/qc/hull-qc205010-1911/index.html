Hull, Quebec (1911 census)

Hull was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,626. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.516°N, 75.820°W.

Population

In 1911, Hull had a population of 3,626: 1,880 male and 1,746 female residents. Population density was 28.2 people per square mile.
