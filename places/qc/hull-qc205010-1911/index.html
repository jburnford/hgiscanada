Hull, Quebec (1911 census)

Hull was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,626. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q2995979. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.516°N, 75.820°W.
