Montreal, St. Mary’s Ward—Quartier Ste. Marie, Quebec (1881 census)

Montreal, St. Mary’s Ward—Quartier Ste. Marie was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 22,733. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.527°N, 73.555°W.

Population

In 1881, Montreal, St. Mary’s Ward—Quartier Ste. Marie had a population of 22,733: 10,881 male and 11,852 female residents. Population density was 19635.1 people per square mile.
