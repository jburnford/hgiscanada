Barnston, Quebec (1911 census)

Barnston was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,342. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q28770225. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.082°N, 71.905°W.
