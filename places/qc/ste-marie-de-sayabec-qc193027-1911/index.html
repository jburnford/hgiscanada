Ste. Marie de Sayabec, Quebec (1911 census)

Ste. Marie de Sayabec was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,208. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.583°N, 67.688°W.

Population

In 1911, Ste. Marie de Sayabec had a population of 2,208: 1,165 male and 1,043 female residents. Population density was 39.8 people per square mile.
