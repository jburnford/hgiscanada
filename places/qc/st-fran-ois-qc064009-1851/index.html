St. François, Quebec (1851 census)

St. François was a census subdivision in Montmorency County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 521. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3462178. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.000°N, 70.836°W.
