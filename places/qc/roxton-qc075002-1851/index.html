Roxton, Quebec (1851 census)

Roxton was a census subdivision in Shefford County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,226. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q63244565. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.530°N, 72.551°W.

Population

In 1851, Roxton had a population of 1,226: 651 male and 575 female residents.
