Québec, C, Quebec (1851–1921)

Québec, C was a city in Quebec, recorded in 2 censuses between 1851 and 1921. This place is grounded to Wikidata Q2145, so it can be queried as a single entity even when its boundaries or census name varied across years. Population grew substantially across the period (from 42,052 in 1851 to 95,193 in 1921).
