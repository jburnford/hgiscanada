St. Valérien, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Valérien was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,806. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.567°N, 72.706°W.

Population

In 1881, St. Valérien had a population of 1,806: 937 male and 869 female residents. Population density was 42.1 people per square mile.
