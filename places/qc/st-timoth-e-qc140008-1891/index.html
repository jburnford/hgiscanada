St. Timothée, Quebec (1891 census)

St. Timothée was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,104. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.271°N, 74.027°W.

Population

In 1891, St. Timothée had a population of 2,104: 1,077 male and 1,027 female residents.
