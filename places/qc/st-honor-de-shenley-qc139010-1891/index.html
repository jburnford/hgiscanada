St. Honoré de Shenley, Quebec (1891 census)

St. Honoré de Shenley was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,578. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.967°N, 70.812°W.

Population

In 1891, St. Honoré de Shenley had a population of 1,578: 798 male and 780 female residents.
