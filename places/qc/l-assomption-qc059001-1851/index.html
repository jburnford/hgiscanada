L’Assomption, Quebec (1851 census)

L’Assomption was a census subdivision in Leinster County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,110. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.856°N, 73.448°W.

Population

In 1851, L’Assomption had a population of 3,110: 1,565 male and 1,545 female residents.
