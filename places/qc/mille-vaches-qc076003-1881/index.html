Mille Vaches, Quebec (1881 census)

Mille Vaches was a census subdivision in Chicoutimi & Saguenay County, Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,115. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.740°N, 69.123°W.

Population

In 1881, Mille Vaches had a population of 1,115: 587 male and 528 female residents. Population density was 2.6 people per square mile.
