Les Eboulements, Quebec (1861 census)

Les Eboulements was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,235. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3236497. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.511°N, 70.332°W.
