Montréal, ward-quartier St. Jacques, Quebec (1911 census)

Montréal, ward-quartier St. Jacques was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 15,470. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.515°N, 73.554°W.

Population

In 1911, Montréal, ward-quartier St. Jacques had a population of 15,470: 7,530 male and 7,940 female residents.
