Sault St. Louis, Quebec (1911 census)

Sault St. Louis was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,500. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.385°N, 73.672°W.

Population

In 1911, Sault St. Louis had a population of 2,500: 1,271 male and 1,229 female residents. Population density was 120.2 people per square mile.
