Cap St. Ignace, Quebec (1911 census)

Cap St. Ignace was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,648. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q2936964. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.975°N, 70.371°W.

In 1911, Cap St. Ignace had a population of 3,648: 1,818 male and 1,830 female residents. Population density was 58.0 people per square mile.
