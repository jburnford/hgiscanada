St. Pierre de Broughton, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Pierre de Broughton was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,671. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.175°N, 71.264°W.

In 1881, St. Pierre de Broughton had a population of 1,671: 839 male and 832 female residents. Population density was 69.6 people per square mile.
