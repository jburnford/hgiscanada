Ixworth, Quebec (1851 census)

Ixworth was a township in Kamouraska County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 587. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.297°N, 69.922°W.

Population

In 1851, Ixworth had a population of 587: 295 male and 292 female residents.
