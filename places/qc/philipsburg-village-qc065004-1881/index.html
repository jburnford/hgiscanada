Philipsburg, Village, Quebec (1881 census)

Philipsburg, Village was a village in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 259. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.043°N, 73.075°W.

Population

In 1881, Philipsburg, Village had a population of 259: 132 male and 127 female residents. Population density was 213.8 people per square mile.
