Compton, Quebec (1901 census)

Compton was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 1,983. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.227°N, 71.823°W.

Population

In 1901, Compton had a population of 1,983: 1,019 male and 964 female residents. Population density was 25.0 people per square mile.
