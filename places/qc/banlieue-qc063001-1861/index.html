Banlieue, Quebec (1861 census)

Banlieue was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 289. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.312°N, 72.588°W.

Population

In 1861, Banlieue had a population of 289: 142 male and 147 female residents.
