Buckland, Quebec (1881 census)

Buckland was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,403. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.612°N, 70.578°W.

Population

In 1881, Buckland had a population of 1,403: 732 male and 671 female residents. Population density was 37.9 people per square mile.
