Laval, Quebec (1871 census)

Laval was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 763. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.057°N, 71.218°W.

Population

In 1871, Laval had a population of 763: 384 male and 379 female residents.
