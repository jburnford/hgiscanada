Neuville, Quebec (1911 census)

Neuville was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,247. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.721°N, 71.624°W.

Population

In 1911, Neuville had a population of 1,247: 634 male and 613 female residents. Population density was 40.8 people per square mile.
