Montréal, ward-quartier Laurier, Quebec (1911 census)

Montréal, ward-quartier Laurier was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 37,000. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.527°N, 73.603°W.

Population

In 1911, Montréal, ward-quartier Laurier had a population of 37,000: 18,373 male and 18,627 female residents. Population density was 33805.1 people per square mile.
