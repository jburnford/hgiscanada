Laprairie, Quebec (1861 census)

Laprairie was a census subdivision in Laprairie County, Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,234. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.419°N, 73.459°W.

Population

In 1861, Laprairie had a population of 2,234: 1,181 male and 1,053 female residents. Population density was 56.5 people per square mile.
