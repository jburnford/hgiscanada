Kénogami, Quebec (1881 census)

Kénogami was a census subdivision in Chicoutimi & Saguenay County, Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 262. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.412°N, 71.438°W.

Population

In 1881, Kénogami had a population of 262: 139 male and 123 female residents. Population density was 2.6 people per square mile.
