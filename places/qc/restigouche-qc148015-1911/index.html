Restigouche, Quebec (1911 census)

Restigouche was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 713. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.064°N, 66.917°W.

Population

In 1911, Restigouche had a population of 713: 402 male and 311 female residents. Population density was 6.1 people per square mile.
