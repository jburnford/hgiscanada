Dunham, Quebec (1891 census)

Dunham was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,466. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.150°N, 72.786°W.

Population

In 1891, Dunham had a population of 2,466: 1,268 male and 1,198 female residents.
