St. Barnabé, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Barnabé was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,316. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112911918. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.737°N, 72.936°W.
