Nouvelle & Shoolbred, Quebec (1911 census)

Nouvelle & Shoolbred was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,226. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.165°N, 66.405°W.
