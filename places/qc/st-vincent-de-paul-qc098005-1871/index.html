St. Vincent de Paul, Quebec (1871 census)

St. Vincent de Paul was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 2,320. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112912862. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.627°N, 73.665°W.

In 1871, St. Vincent de Paul had a population of 2,320: 1,145 male and 1,175 female residents. Population density was 101.2 people per square mile.
