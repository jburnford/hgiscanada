St. Jean Baptiste, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Jean Baptiste was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,246. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.509°N, 73.118°W.

Population

In 1911, St. Jean Baptiste had a population of 1,246: 640 male and 606 female residents. Population density was 45.4 people per square mile.
