Danville, Village, Quebec (1911 census)

Danville, Village was a village in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,331. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q142481. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.784°N, 72.017°W.

Population
