Dunham, Quebec (1881 census)

Dunham was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 3,702. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.152°N, 72.785°W.

Population

In 1881, Dunham had a population of 3,702: 1,926 male and 1,776 female residents. Population density was 38.6 people per square mile.
