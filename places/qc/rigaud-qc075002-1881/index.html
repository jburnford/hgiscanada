Rigaud, Quebec (1881 census)

Rigaud was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,826. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.470°N, 74.336°W.

Population

In 1881, Rigaud had a population of 2,826: 1,464 male and 1,362 female residents.
