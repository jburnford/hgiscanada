St. Lambert de Lauzon, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Lambert de Lauzon was a census subdivision in Dorchester County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 902. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.601°N, 71.202°W.

Population

In 1851, St. Lambert de Lauzon had a population of 902: 449 male and 453 female residents. Population density was 32.9 people per square mile.
