Ste. Rosalie, Quebec (1861 census)

Ste. Rosalie was a census subdivision in Bagot County, Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,064. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112913101. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.651°N, 72.879°W.

In 1861, Ste. Rosalie had a population of 2,064: 1,043 male and 1,021 female residents.
