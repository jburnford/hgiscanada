Mont Carmel, Quebec (1911 census)

Mont Carmel was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,282. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.405°N, 69.796°W.

Population

In 1911, Mont Carmel had a population of 1,282: 667 male and 615 female residents. Population density was 31.4 people per square mile.
