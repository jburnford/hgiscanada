Québec, St. Pierre, Ward—Quartier, Quebec (1891 census)

Québec, St. Pierre, Ward—Quartier was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,699. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.818°N, 71.207°W.

Population

In 1891, Québec, St. Pierre, Ward—Quartier had a population of 2,699: 1,272 male and 1,427 female residents.
