St. Gilles, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Gilles was a township in Lotbinière County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,203. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.497°N, 71.369°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Gilles had a population of 1,203: 630 male and 573 female residents.
