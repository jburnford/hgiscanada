Ste. Brigitte des Saults, Quebec (1871 census)

Ste. Brigitte des Saults was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 548. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.032°N, 72.487°W.

Population

In 1871, Ste. Brigitte des Saults had a population of 548: 277 male and 271 female residents. Population density was 18.6 people per square mile.
