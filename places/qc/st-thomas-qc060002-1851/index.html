St. Thomas, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Thomas was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,994. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112912821. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.945°N, 70.539°W.
