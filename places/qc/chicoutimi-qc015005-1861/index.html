Chicoutimi, Quebec (1861 census)

Chicoutimi was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 3,177. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.397°N, 71.061°W.

Population

In 1861, Chicoutimi had a population of 3,177: 1,622 male and 1,555 female residents.
