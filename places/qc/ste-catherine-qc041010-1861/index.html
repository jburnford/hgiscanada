Ste. Catherine, Quebec (1861 census)

Ste. Catherine was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,670. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q141565. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.872°N, 71.615°W.
