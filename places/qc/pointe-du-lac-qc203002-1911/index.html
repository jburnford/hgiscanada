Pointe du Lac, Quebec (1911 census)

Pointe du Lac was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,316. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3393527. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.316°N, 72.704°W.
