St. Alexandre, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Alexandre was a census subdivision in Kamouraska County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 913. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.660°N, 69.581°W.

Population

In 1851, St. Alexandre had a population of 913: 440 male and 473 female residents. Population density was 19.2 people per square mile.
