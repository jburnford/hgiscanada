Ste. Marguerite, Quebec (1861 census)

Ste. Marguerite was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,824. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3464341. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.508°N, 70.928°W.
