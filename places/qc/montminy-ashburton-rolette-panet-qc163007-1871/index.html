Montminy, Ashburton, Rolette, Panet, Quebec (1871 census)

Montminy, Ashburton, Rolette, Panet was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 917. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.705°N, 70.261°W.

Population

In 1871, Montminy, Ashburton, Rolette, Panet had a population of 917: 491 male and 426 female residents.
