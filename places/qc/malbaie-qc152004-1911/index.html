Malbaie, Quebec (1911 census)

Malbaie was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,034. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.714°N, 70.170°W.

Population

In 1911, Malbaie had a population of 3,034: 1,675 male and 1,359 female residents. Population density was 30.8 people per square mile.
