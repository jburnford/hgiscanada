St. Elzéar, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Elzéar was a census subdivision in Dorchester County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,365. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3462113. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.385°N, 71.067°W.

Population

In 1851, St. Elzéar had a population of 2,365: 1,189 male and 1,176 female residents.
