St. Constant, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Constant was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,722. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112912031. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.347°N, 73.580°W.
