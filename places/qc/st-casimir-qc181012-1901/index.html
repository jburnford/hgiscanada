St. Casimir, Quebec (1901 census)

St. Casimir was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 2,456. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.669°N, 72.152°W.

Population

In 1901, St. Casimir had a population of 2,456: 1,237 male and 1,219 female residents. Population density was 91.8 people per square mile.
