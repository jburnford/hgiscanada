Louiseville (Rivière du Loup), Quebec (1911 census)

Louiseville (Rivière du Loup) was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,305. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.256°N, 72.944°W.

Population

In 1911, Louiseville (Rivière du Loup) had a population of 1,305: 678 male and 627 female residents. Population density was 57.8 people per square mile.
