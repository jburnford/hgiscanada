L'Islet, Quebec (1881 census)

L'Islet was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,974. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.104°N, 70.357°W.

Population

In 1881, L'Islet had a population of 2,974: 1,486 male and 1,488 female residents. Population density was 118.7 people per square mile.
