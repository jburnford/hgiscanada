Lavaltrie, Quebec (1851 census)

Lavaltrie was a township in Berthier County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,367. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.905°N, 73.325°W.

Population

In 1851, Lavaltrie had a population of 1,367: 666 male and 701 female residents.
