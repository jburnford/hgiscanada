Robertson, Quebec (1911 census)

Robertson was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 807. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.506°N, 75.616°W.

Population

In 1911, Robertson had a population of 807: 439 male and 368 female residents. Population density was 6.8 people per square mile.
