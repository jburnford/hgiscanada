Franklin, Quebec (1861 census)

Franklin was a census subdivision in Huntingdon County, Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,551. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3082928. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.034°N, 73.923°W.

Population

In 1861, Franklin had a population of 1,551: 821 male and 730 female residents.
