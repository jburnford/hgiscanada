St. Grégoire, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Grégoire was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,183. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112912260. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.329°N, 73.149°W.

In 1911, St. Grégoire had a population of 1,183: 571 male and 612 female residents. Population density was 42.8 people per square mile.
