St. Liboire, Quebec (1891 census)

St. Liboire was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,353. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.652°N, 72.766°W.

Population

In 1891, St. Liboire had a population of 2,353: 1,173 male and 1,180 female residents. Population density was 82.1 people per square mile.
