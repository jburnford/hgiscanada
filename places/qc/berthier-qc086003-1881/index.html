Berthier, Quebec (1881 census)

Berthier was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,222. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.089°N, 73.215°W.

Population

In 1881, Berthier had a population of 2,222: 1,070 male and 1,152 female residents.
