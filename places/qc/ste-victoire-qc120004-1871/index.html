Ste. Victoire, Quebec (1871 census)

Ste. Victoire was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,620. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.942°N, 73.067°W.

In 1871, Ste. Victoire had a population of 1,620: 798 male and 822 female residents.
