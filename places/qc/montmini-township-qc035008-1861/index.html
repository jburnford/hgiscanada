Montmini, Township, Quebec (1861 census)

Montmini, Township was a township in Montmagny County, Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 600. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.805°N, 70.374°W.

In 1861, Montmini, Township had a population of 600: 345 male and 255 female residents.
