Pointe du Lac, Quebec (1881 census)

Pointe du Lac was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,607. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.316°N, 72.704°W.

Population

In 1881, Pointe du Lac had a population of 1,607: 809 male and 798 female residents.
