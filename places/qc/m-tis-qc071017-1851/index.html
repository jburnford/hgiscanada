Métis, Quebec (1851 census)

Métis was a township in Rimouski County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 656. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.525°N, 67.673°W.

Population

In 1851, Métis had a population of 656: 343 male and 313 female residents.
