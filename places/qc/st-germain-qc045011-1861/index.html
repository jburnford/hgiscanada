St. Germain, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Germain was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 3,550. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112912230. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.419°N, 68.518°W.
