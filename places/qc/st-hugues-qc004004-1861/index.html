St. Hugues, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Hugues was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,568. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.795°N, 72.820°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Hugues had a population of 2,568: 1,322 male and 1,246 female residents.
