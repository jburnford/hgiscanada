Lochaber, Gore, Quebec (1851 census)

Lochaber, Gore was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 225. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.669°N, 75.149°W.

Population

In 1851, Lochaber, Gore had a population of 225: 114 male and 111 female residents.
