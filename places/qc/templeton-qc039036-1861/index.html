Templeton, Quebec (1861 census)

Templeton was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,646. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.582°N, 75.634°W.

Population

In 1861, Templeton had a population of 2,646: 1,540 male and 1,106 female residents.
