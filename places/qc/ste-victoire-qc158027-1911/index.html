Ste. Victoire, Quebec (1911 census)

Ste. Victoire was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,241. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.065°N, 71.978°W.

Population

In 1911, Ste. Victoire had a population of 1,241: 629 male and 612 female residents. Population density was 41.9 people per square mile.
