Cap de la Magdeleine, Quebec (1911 census)

Cap de la Magdeleine was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,101. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.401°N, 72.503°W.

Population

In 1911, Cap de la Magdeleine had a population of 2,101: 1,080 male and 1,021 female residents. Population density was 106.0 people per square mile.
