St. Clément, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Clément was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,516. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.290°N, 73.887°W.

Population

In 1911, St. Clément had a population of 1,516: 797 male and 719 female residents. Population density was 50.9 people per square mile.
