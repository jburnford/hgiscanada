St. Antonin, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Antonin was a census subdivision in Temiscouata County, Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,234. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112911899. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.741°N, 69.478°W.
