St. Léon, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Léon was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,593. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3462823. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.339°N, 72.970°W.
