Thetford S., Quebec (1911 census)

Thetford S. was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 527. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.092°N, 71.281°W.

Population

In 1911, Thetford S. had a population of 527: 266 male and 261 female residents. Population density was 5.6 people per square mile.
