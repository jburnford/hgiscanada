St. Armand E, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Armand E was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,557. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.046°N, 72.812°W.

Population

In 1851, St. Armand E had a population of 1,557: 785 male and 772 female residents.
