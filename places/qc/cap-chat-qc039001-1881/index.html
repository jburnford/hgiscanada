Cap Chat, Quebec (1881 census)

Cap Chat was a census subdivision in Gaspé County, Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,427. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.072°N, 66.639°W.

Population

In 1881, Cap Chat had a population of 1,427: 760 male and 667 female residents. Population density was 50.3 people per square mile.
