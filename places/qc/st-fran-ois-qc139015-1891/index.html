St. François, Quebec (1891 census)

St. François was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 4,022. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.221°N, 70.760°W.

Population

In 1891, St. François had a population of 4,022: 2,116 male and 1,906 female residents.
