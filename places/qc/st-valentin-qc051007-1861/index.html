St. Valentin, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Valentin was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,915. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112912847. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.152°N, 73.313°W.

In 1861, St. Valentin had a population of 2,915: 1,508 male and 1,407 female residents.
