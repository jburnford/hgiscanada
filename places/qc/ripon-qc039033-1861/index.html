Ripon, Quebec (1861 census)

Ripon was a census subdivision in Outaouais County, Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 609. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.795°N, 75.147°W.

Population

In 1861, Ripon had a population of 609. Population density was 9.7 people per square mile.
