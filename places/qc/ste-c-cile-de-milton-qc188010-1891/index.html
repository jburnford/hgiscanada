Ste. Cécile de Milton, Quebec (1891 census)

Ste. Cécile de Milton was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,496. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.484°N, 72.759°W.

Population

In 1891, Ste. Cécile de Milton had a population of 1,496: 720 male and 776 female residents.
