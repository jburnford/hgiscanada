Marlow, Quebec (1851 census)

Marlow was a census subdivision in Dorchester County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 23. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.838°N, 70.536°W.

Population

In 1851, Marlow had a population of 23: 12 male and 11 female residents.
