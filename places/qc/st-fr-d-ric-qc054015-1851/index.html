St. Frédéric, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Frédéric was a census subdivision in Dorchester County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 934. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.303°N, 70.973°W.

Population

In 1851, St. Frédéric had a population of 934: 450 male and 484 female residents. Population density was 37.7 people per square mile.
