Somerset, Quebec (1851 census)

Somerset was a township in Mégantic County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,292. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.279°N, 71.724°W.

Population

In 1851, Somerset had a population of 2,292: 1,188 male and 1,104 female residents.
