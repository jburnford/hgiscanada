Ste. Marie, Quebec (1911 census)

Ste. Marie was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,555. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.442°N, 71.004°W.

Population

In 1911, Ste. Marie had a population of 2,555: 1,233 male and 1,322 female residents. Population density was 66.8 people per square mile.
