Québec, ward-quartier St. Valier, Quebec (1911 census)

Québec, ward-quartier St. Valier was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 11,300. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.812°N, 71.243°W.

Population

In 1911, Québec, ward-quartier St. Valier had a population of 11,300: 5,272 male and 6,028 female residents. Population density was 33137.8 people per square mile.
