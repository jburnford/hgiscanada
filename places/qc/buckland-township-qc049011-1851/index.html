Buckland, Township, Quebec (1851 census)

Buckland, Township was a township in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 137. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.584°N, 70.628°W.

Population

In 1851, Buckland, Township had a population of 137: 66 male and 71 female residents.
