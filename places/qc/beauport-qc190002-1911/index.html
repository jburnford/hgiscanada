Beauport, Quebec (1911 census)

Beauport was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,546. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.905°N, 71.218°W.

Population

In 1911, Beauport had a population of 3,546: 1,775 male and 1,771 female residents. Population density was 157.2 people per square mile.
