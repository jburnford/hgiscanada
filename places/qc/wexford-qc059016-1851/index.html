Wexford, Quebec (1851 census)

Wexford was a census subdivision in Leinster County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 145. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.040°N, 74.040°W.

Population

In 1851, Wexford had a population of 145: 75 male and 70 female residents.
