Marchand, Quebec (1891 census)

Marchand was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 414. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.384°N, 74.821°W.

Population

In 1891, Marchand had a population of 414: 228 male and 186 female residents. Population density was 3.2 people per square mile.
