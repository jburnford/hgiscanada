St. Roch, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Roch was a township in Québec County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 6,936. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.819°N, 71.262°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Roch had a population of 6,936: 3,581 male and 3,355 female residents.
