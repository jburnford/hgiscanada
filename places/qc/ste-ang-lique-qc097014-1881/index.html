Ste. Angélique, Quebec (1881 census)

Ste. Angélique was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,217. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.640°N, 75.044°W.

Population

In 1881, Ste. Angélique had a population of 2,217: 1,154 male and 1,063 female residents.
