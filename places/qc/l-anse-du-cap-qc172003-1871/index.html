L’Anse du Cap, Quebec (1871 census)

L’Anse du Cap was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,533. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.459°N, 64.407°W.

Population

In 1871, L’Anse du Cap had a population of 1,533: 829 male and 704 female residents. Population density was 29.3 people per square mile.
