Mont Joli, Village, Quebec (1911 census)

Mont Joli, Village was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,141. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.577°N, 68.190°W.
