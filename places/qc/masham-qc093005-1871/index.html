Masham, Quebec (1871 census)

Masham was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,904. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.683°N, 76.039°W.

Population

In 1871, Masham had a population of 1,904: 1,004 male and 900 female residents.
