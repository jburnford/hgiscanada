Stanbridge, Quebec (1881 census)

Stanbridge was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 5,348. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q25395180. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.146°N, 72.973°W.
