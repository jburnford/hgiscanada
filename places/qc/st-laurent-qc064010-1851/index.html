St. Laurent, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Laurent was a census subdivision in Montmorency County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 877. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.878°N, 71.022°W.

Population

In 1851, St. Laurent had a population of 877: 432 male and 445 female residents. Population density was 62.6 people per square mile.
