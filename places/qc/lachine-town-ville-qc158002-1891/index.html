Lachine, Town—Ville, Quebec (1891 census)

Lachine, Town—Ville was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 3,761. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.440°N, 73.683°W.

Population

In 1891, Lachine, Town—Ville had a population of 3,761: 1,824 male and 1,937 female residents.
