St. Armand E, Quebec (1871 census)

St. Armand E was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,340. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.046°N, 72.811°W.

Population

In 1871, St. Armand E had a population of 1,340: 663 male and 677 female residents. Population density was 27.4 people per square mile.
