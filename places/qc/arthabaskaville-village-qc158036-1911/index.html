Arthabaskaville, Village, Quebec (1911 census)

Arthabaskaville, Village was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,458. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.044°N, 71.920°W.

Population

In 1911, Arthabaskaville, Village had a population of 1,458: 769 male and 689 female residents. Population density was 468.8 people per square mile.
