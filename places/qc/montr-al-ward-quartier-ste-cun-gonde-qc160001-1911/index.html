Montréal, ward-quartier Ste. Cunégonde, Quebec (1911 census)

Montréal, ward-quartier Ste. Cunégonde was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 11,174. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.484°N, 73.575°W.

Population

In 1911, Montréal, ward-quartier Ste. Cunégonde had a population of 11,174: 5,667 male and 5,507 female residents. Population density was 57302.6 people per square mile.
