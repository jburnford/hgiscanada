Litchfield, Quebec (1911 census)

Litchfield was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,382. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.788°N, 76.596°W.

Population

In 1911, Litchfield had a population of 1,382: 706 male and 676 female residents. Population density was 17.1 people per square mile.
