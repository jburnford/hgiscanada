Longueuil, Quebec (1891 census)

Longueuil was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,194. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.528°N, 73.473°W.

In 1891, Longueuil had a population of 1,194: 581 male and 613 female residents.
