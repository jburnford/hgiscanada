St. Narcisse, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Narcisse was a township in Champlain County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 979. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.573°N, 72.513°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Narcisse had a population of 979: 505 male and 474 female residents.
