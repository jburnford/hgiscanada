Kingsey, Quebec (1911 census)

Kingsey was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,331. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q63243328. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.797°N, 72.186°W.

Population

In 1911, Kingsey had a population of 1,331: 674 male and 657 female residents. Population density was 14.8 people per square mile.
