Ste. Anne de la Pérade, Quebec (1911 census)

Ste. Anne de la Pérade was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,513. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.599°N, 72.204°W.
