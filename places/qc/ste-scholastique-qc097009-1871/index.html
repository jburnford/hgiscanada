Ste. Scholastique, Quebec (1871 census)

Ste. Scholastique was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 2,811. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.655°N, 74.085°W.

Population

In 1871, Ste. Scholastique had a population of 2,811: 1,441 male and 1,370 female residents. Population density was 66.6 people per square mile.
