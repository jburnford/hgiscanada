St. Lambert, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Lambert was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,344. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.504°N, 73.508°W.

In 1911, St. Lambert had a population of 3,344: 1,849 male and 1,495 female residents. Population density was 3070.7 people per square mile.
