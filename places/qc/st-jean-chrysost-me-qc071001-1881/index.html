St. Jean Chrysostôme, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Jean Chrysostôme was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 4,078. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.131°N, 73.745°W.

Population

In 1881, St. Jean Chrysostôme had a population of 4,078: 2,048 male and 2,030 female residents. Population density was 47.1 people per square mile.
