Allumettes, Quebec (1851 census)

Allumettes was a census subdivision in Outaouais County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,025. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.869°N, 77.048°W.

Population

In 1851, Allumettes had a population of 1,025: 566 male and 459 female residents.
