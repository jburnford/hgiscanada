St. Peters’ Ward, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Peters’ Ward was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 3,435. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.816°N, 71.208°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Peters’ Ward had a population of 3,435: 1,735 male and 1,700 female residents.
