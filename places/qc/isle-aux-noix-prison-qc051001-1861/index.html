Isle-aux-Noix (Prison), Quebec (1861 census)

Isle-aux-Noix (Prison) was a township in St. Johns County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 128. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.123°N, 73.265°W.

Population

In 1861, Isle-aux-Noix (Prison) had a population of 128: 1,911 male and 1,906 female residents.
