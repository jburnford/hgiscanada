Trois-Rivières, Notre-Dame, Ward—Quartier, Quebec (1891 census)

Trois-Rivières, Notre-Dame, Ward—Quartier was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,630. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.356°N, 72.549°W.

Population

In 1891, Trois-Rivières, Notre-Dame, Ward—Quartier had a population of 2,630: 1,227 male and 1,403 female residents.
