Saguenay and Rivière Ste. Marguerite, Quebec (1861 census)

Saguenay and Rivière Ste. Marguerite was a township in Saguenay County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 200. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.159°N, 69.876°W.

In 1861, Saguenay and Rivière Ste. Marguerite had a population of 200: 357 male and 301 female residents.
