Thetford, Quebec (1861 census)

Thetford was a census subdivision in Megantic County, Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 282. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.126°N, 71.229°W.

Population

In 1861, Thetford had a population of 282: 142 male and 140 female residents.
