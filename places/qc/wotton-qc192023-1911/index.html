Wotton, Quebec (1911 census)

Wotton was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,914. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q956507. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.752°N, 71.799°W.
