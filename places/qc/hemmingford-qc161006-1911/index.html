Hemmingford, Quebec (1911 census)

Hemmingford was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,429. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.060°N, 73.605°W.

Population

In 1911, Hemmingford had a population of 1,429: 718 male and 711 female residents. Population density was 23.9 people per square mile.
