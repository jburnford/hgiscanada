Cap de la Madeleine, Quebec (1851 census)

Cap de la Madeleine was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,123. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.651°N, 72.801°W.

Population

In 1851, Cap de la Madeleine had a population of 1,123: 556 male and 567 female residents.
