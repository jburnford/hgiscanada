Yamachiche, Quebec (1891 census)

Yamachiche was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,025. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.309°N, 72.829°W.

Population

In 1891, Yamachiche had a population of 2,025: 1,035 male and 990 female residents. Population density was 48.3 people per square mile.
