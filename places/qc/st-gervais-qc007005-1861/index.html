St. Gervais, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Gervais was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,717. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3462315. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.706°N, 70.842°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Gervais had a population of 2,717: 1,347 male and 1,370 female residents.
