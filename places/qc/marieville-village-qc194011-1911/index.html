Marieville, Village, Quebec (1911 census)

Marieville, Village was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,587. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.430°N, 73.166°W.

Population

In 1911, Marieville, Village had a population of 1,587: 752 male and 835 female residents. Population density was 3005.7 people per square mile.
