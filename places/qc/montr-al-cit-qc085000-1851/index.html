Montréal, cité, Quebec (1851 census)

Montréal, cité was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 57,715. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.506°N, 73.565°W.

Population

In 1851, Montréal, cité had a population of 57,715: 27,734 male and 29,981 female residents.
