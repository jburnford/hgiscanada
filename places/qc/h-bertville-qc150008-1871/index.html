Hébertville, Quebec (1871 census)

Hébertville was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 3,177. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.430°N, 71.680°W.

Population

In 1871, Hébertville had a population of 3,177: 1,661 male and 1,516 female residents. Population density was 6.9 people per square mile.
