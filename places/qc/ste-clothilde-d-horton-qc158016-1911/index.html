Ste. Clothilde d'Horton, Quebec (1911 census)

Ste. Clothilde d'Horton was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,398. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.973°N, 72.233°W.
