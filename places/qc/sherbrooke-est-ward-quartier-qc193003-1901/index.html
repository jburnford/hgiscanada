Sherbrooke, Est, Ward—Quartier, Quebec (1901 census)

Sherbrooke, Est, Ward—Quartier was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 2,774. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.404°N, 71.878°W.
