Roberval & Charlevoix, Quebec (1901 census)

Roberval & Charlevoix was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 1,345. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.490°N, 72.257°W.

Population

In 1901, Roberval & Charlevoix had a population of 1,345: 728 male and 617 female residents. Population density was 8.9 people per square mile.
