Wotton, Quebec (1901 census)

Wotton was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 1,903. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.752°N, 71.799°W.

In 1901, Wotton had a population of 1,903: 958 male and 945 female residents. Population density was 33.3 people per square mile.
