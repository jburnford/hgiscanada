Ste. Angélique, Quebec (1911 census)

Ste. Angélique was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 779. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112912898. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.648°N, 75.023°W.
