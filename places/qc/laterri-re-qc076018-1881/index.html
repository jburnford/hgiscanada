Laterrière, Quebec (1881 census)

Laterrière was a census subdivision in Chicoutimi & Saguenay County, Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,320. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.289°N, 71.159°W.
