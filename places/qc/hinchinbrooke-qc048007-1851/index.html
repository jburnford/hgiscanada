Hinchinbrooke, Quebec (1851 census)

Hinchinbrooke was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,657. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q30322644. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.042°N, 74.086°W.
