Pointe du Lac, Quebec (1891 census)

Pointe du Lac was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,366. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3393527. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.316°N, 72.704°W.

In 1891, Pointe du Lac had a population of 1,366: 672 male and 694 female residents.
