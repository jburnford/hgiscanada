Ste. Marthe, Quebec (1891 census)

Ste. Marthe was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,610. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.399°N, 74.300°W.

Population

In 1891, Ste. Marthe had a population of 1,610: 824 male and 786 female residents.
