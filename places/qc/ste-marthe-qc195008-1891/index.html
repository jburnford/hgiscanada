Ste. Marthe, Quebec (1891 census)

Ste. Marthe was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,610. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3464374. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.399°N, 74.300°W.
