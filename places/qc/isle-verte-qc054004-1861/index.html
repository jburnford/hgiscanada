Isle Verte, Quebec (1861 census)

Isle Verte was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 3,302. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.001°N, 69.319°W.

Population

In 1861, Isle Verte had a population of 3,302: 1,752 male and 1,550 female residents.
