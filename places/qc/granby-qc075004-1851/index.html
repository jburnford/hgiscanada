Granby, Quebec (1851 census)

Granby was a census subdivision in Shefford County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,392. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3113418. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.382°N, 72.753°W.

Population

In 1851, Granby had a population of 2,392: 1,221 male and 1,171 female residents.
