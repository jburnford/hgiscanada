Kingsey, Quebec (1861 census)

Kingsey was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,403. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q63243328. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.827°N, 72.169°W.
