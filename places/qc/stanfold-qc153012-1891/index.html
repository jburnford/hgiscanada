Stanfold, Quebec (1891 census)

Stanfold was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,084. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.198°N, 71.885°W.

Population

In 1891, Stanfold had a population of 2,084: 1,083 male and 1,001 female residents.
