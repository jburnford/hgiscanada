Kensington, Quebec (1871 census)

Kensington was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 835. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.524°N, 75.928°W.

Population

In 1871, Kensington had a population of 835: 458 male and 377 female residents. Population density was 1.7 people per square mile.
