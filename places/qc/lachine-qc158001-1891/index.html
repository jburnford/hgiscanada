Lachine, Quebec (1891 census)

Lachine was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,058. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.440°N, 73.676°W.

In 1891, Lachine had a population of 1,058: 569 male and 489 female residents.
