L’Anse du Cap, Quebec (1881 census)

L’Anse du Cap was a census subdivision in Gaspé County, Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,490. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.459°N, 64.407°W.

Population

In 1881, L’Anse du Cap had a population of 1,490: 782 male and 708 female residents. Population density was 28.5 people per square mile.
