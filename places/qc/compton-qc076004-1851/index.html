Compton, Quebec (1851 census)

Compton was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,718. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.233°N, 71.828°W.

Population

In 1851, Compton had a population of 2,718: 1,364 male and 1,354 female residents.
