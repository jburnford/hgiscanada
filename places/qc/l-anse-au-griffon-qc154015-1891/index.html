L’Anse au Griffon, Quebec (1891 census)

L’Anse au Griffon was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,030. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.918°N, 64.295°W.
