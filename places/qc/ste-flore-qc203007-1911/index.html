Ste. Flore, Quebec (1911 census)

Ste. Flore was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,541. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112912993. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.620°N, 72.771°W.
