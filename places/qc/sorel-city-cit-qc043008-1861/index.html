Sorel, City—Cité, Quebec (1861 census)

Sorel, City—Cité was a city in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 4,778. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q29386656. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.033°N, 73.105°W.
