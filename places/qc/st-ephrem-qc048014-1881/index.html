St. Ephrem, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Ephrem was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,256. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.072°N, 70.969°W.

Population

In 1881, St. Ephrem had a population of 2,256: 1,175 male and 1,081 female residents. Population density was 37.7 people per square mile.
