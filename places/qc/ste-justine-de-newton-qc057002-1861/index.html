Ste. Justine de Newton, Quebec (1861 census)

Ste. Justine de Newton was a census subdivision in Vaudreuil County, Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,029. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.359°N, 74.382°W.

In 1861, Ste. Justine de Newton had a population of 1,029. Population density was 30.4 people per square mile.
