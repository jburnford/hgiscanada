Berthier, Quebec (1861 census)

Berthier was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,581. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3464281. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.088°N, 73.209°W.
