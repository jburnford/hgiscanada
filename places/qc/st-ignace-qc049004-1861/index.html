St. Ignace, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Ignace was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,116. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q138277576. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.311°N, 74.194°W.
