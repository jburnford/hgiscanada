St. Hypolite, Quebec (1871 census)

St. Hypolite was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 967. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.920°N, 74.019°W.

In 1871, St. Hypolite had a population of 967: 488 male and 479 female residents.
