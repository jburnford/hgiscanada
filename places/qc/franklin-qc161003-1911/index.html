Franklin, Quebec (1911 census)

Franklin was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,009. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.034°N, 73.923°W.

Population

In 1911, Franklin had a population of 1,009: 506 male and 503 female residents. Population density was 36.5 people per square mile.
