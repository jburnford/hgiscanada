Ste. Cécile, Quebec (1911 census)

Ste. Cécile was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,574. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.243°N, 74.119°W.

Population

In 1911, Ste. Cécile had a population of 1,574: 822 male and 752 female residents. Population density was 81.2 people per square mile.
