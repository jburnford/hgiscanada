Westbury, Quebec (1901 census)

Westbury was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 1,976. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.480°N, 71.656°W.

Population

In 1901, Westbury had a population of 1,976: 1,064 male and 912 female residents. Population density was 70.7 people per square mile.
