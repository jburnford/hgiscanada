Ste. Rose, Village, Quebec (1871 census)

Ste. Rose, Village was a village in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 736. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.613°N, 73.790°W.

Population

In 1871, Ste. Rose, Village had a population of 736: 339 male and 397 female residents. Population density was 845.8 people per square mile.
