St. Onésime, Ixworth & Chapais, Quebec (1871 census)

St. Onésime, Ixworth & Chapais was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 770. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.236°N, 69.741°W.
