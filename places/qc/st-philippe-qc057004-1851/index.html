St. Philippe, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Philippe was a township in Huntingdon County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,480. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.338°N, 73.477°W.

Population

In 1851, St. Philippe had a population of 2,480: 1,224 male and 1,256 female residents.
