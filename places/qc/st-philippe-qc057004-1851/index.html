St. Philippe, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Philippe was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,480. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3463156. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.338°N, 73.477°W.

In 1851, St. Philippe had a population of 2,480: 1,224 male and 1,256 female residents.
