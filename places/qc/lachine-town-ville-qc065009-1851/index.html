Lachine, Town—Ville, Quebec (1851 census)

Lachine, Town—Ville was a township in Montréal County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,089. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.433°N, 73.675°W.

Population

In 1851, Lachine, Town—Ville had a population of 1,089: 562 male and 527 female residents.
