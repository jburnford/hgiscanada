St. Anicet, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Anicet was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,566. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3461782. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.117°N, 74.308°W.

Population

In 1851, St. Anicet had a population of 2,566: 1,298 male and 1,268 female residents.
