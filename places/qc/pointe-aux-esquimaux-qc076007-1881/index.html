Pointe aux Esquimaux, Quebec (1881 census)

Pointe aux Esquimaux was a township in Chicoutimi & Saguenay County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,775. The administrative centroid was at approximately 50.327°N, 63.605°W.
