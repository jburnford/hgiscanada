St. Cyrille, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Cyrille was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 652. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.029°N, 70.237°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Cyrille had a population of 652: 347 male and 305 female residents.
