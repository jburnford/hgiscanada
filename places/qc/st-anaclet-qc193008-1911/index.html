St. Anaclet, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Anaclet was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,518. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.452°N, 68.385°W.

Population

In 1911, St. Anaclet had a population of 1,518: 776 male and 742 female residents. Population density was 29.8 people per square mile.
