St. Augustin, Quebec (1871 census)

St. Augustin was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,880. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.755°N, 71.489°W.

Population

In 1871, St. Augustin had a population of 1,880: 912 male and 968 female residents. Population density was 53.6 people per square mile.
