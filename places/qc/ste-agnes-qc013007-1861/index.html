Ste. Agnes, Quebec (1861 census)

Ste. Agnes was a township in Charlevoix County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,324. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.655°N, 70.306°W.

Population

In 1861, Ste. Agnes had a population of 1,324: 656 male and 668 female residents.
