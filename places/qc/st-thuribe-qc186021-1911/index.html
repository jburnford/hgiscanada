St. Thuribe, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Thuribe was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 642. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.721°N, 72.190°W.

Population

In 1911, St. Thuribe had a population of 642: 302 male and 340 female residents. Population density was 53.5 people per square mile.
