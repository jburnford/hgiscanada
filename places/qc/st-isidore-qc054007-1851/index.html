St. Isidore, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Isidore was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,163. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3462458. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.579°N, 71.098°W.

Population

In 1851, St. Isidore had a population of 2,163: 1,089 male and 1,074 female residents.
