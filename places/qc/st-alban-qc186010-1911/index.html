St. Alban, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Alban was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,451. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112911830. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.768°N, 72.110°W.

Population

In 1911, St. Alban had a population of 1,451: 736 male and 715 female residents. Population density was 23.6 people per square mile.
