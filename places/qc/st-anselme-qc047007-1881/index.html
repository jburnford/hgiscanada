St. Anselme, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Anselme was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,206. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.632°N, 70.967°W.

In 1881, St. Anselme had a population of 2,206: 1,123 male and 1,083 female residents.
