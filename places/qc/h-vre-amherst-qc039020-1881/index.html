Hâvre Amherst, Quebec (1881 census)

Hâvre Amherst was a census subdivision in Gaspé County, Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,410. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.253°N, 61.928°W.

Population

In 1881, Hâvre Amherst had a population of 1,410: 705 male and 705 female residents. Population density was 41.9 people per square mile.
