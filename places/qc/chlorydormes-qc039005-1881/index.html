Chlorydormes, Quebec (1881 census)

Chlorydormes was a census subdivision in Gaspé County, Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 488. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.151°N, 64.913°W.

Population

In 1881, Chlorydormes had a population of 488: 252 male and 236 female residents. Population density was 5.5 people per square mile.
