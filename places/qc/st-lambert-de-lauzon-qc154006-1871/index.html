St. Lambert de Lauzon, Quebec (1871 census)

St. Lambert de Lauzon was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,563. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.584°N, 71.228°W.

Population

In 1871, St. Lambert de Lauzon had a population of 1,563: 796 male and 767 female residents. Population density was 35.6 people per square mile.
