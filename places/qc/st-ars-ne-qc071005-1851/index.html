St. Arsène, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Arsène was a township in Rimouski County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,958. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.907°N, 69.410°W.

Population

In 1851, St. Arsène had a population of 1,958: 955 male and 1,003 female residents.
