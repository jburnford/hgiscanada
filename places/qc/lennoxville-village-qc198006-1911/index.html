Lennoxville, Village, Quebec (1911 census)

Lennoxville, Village was a village in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,211. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q672089. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.367°N, 71.857°W.

Population

In 1911, Lennoxville, Village had a population of 1,211: 596 male and 615 female residents. Population density was 272.4 people per square mile.
