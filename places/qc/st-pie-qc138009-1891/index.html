St. Pie, Quebec (1891 census)

St. Pie was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 3,342. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.513°N, 72.897°W.

In 1891, St. Pie had a population of 3,342: 1,673 male and 1,669 female residents.
