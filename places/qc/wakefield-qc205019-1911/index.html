Wakefield, Quebec (1911 census)

Wakefield was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,432. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.701°N, 75.830°W.

Population

In 1911, Wakefield had a population of 1,432: 740 male and 692 female residents. Population density was 12.0 people per square mile.
