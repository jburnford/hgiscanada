Rolette, Panet & Talon, Quebec (1911 census)

Rolette, Panet & Talon was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 574. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.666°N, 70.118°W.

Population

In 1911, Rolette, Panet & Talon had a population of 574: 306 male and 268 female residents. Population density was 2.7 people per square mile.
