Brome, Quebec (1911 census)

Brome was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,600. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q28161378. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.235°N, 72.586°W.
