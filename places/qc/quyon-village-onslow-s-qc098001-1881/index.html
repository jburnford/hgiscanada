Quyon (Village), Onslow S, Quebec (1881 census)

Quyon (Village), Onslow S was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,332. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.548°N, 76.217°W.
