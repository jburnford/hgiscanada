Arundel, Quebec (1861 census)

Arundel was a census subdivision in Argenteuil County, Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 26. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.987°N, 74.633°W.

Population

In 1861, Arundel had a population of 26: 14 male and 12 female residents.
