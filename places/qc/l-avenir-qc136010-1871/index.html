L'Avenir, Quebec (1871 census)

L'Avenir was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,716. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.748°N, 72.314°W.

Population

In 1871, L'Avenir had a population of 1,716: 839 male and 877 female residents. Population density was 39.5 people per square mile.
