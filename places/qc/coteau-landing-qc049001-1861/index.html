Coteau Landing, Quebec (1861 census)

Coteau Landing was a township in Soulanges County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 488. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.259°N, 74.206°W.

Population

In 1861, Coteau Landing had a population of 488: 258 male and 230 female residents.
