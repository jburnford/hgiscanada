Buckland, Quebec (1861 census)

Buckland was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 406. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.559°N, 70.673°W.

Population

In 1861, Buckland had a population of 406: 225 male and 181 female residents.
