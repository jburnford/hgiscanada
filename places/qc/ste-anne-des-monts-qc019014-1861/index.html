Ste. Anne des Monts, Quebec (1861 census)

Ste. Anne des Monts was a census subdivision in Gaspé County, Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 869. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q106815661. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.136°N, 66.160°W.

Population

In 1861, Ste. Anne des Monts had a population of 869: 471 male and 398 female residents. Population density was 3.2 people per square mile.
