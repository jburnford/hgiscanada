Isle of Anticosti, Quebec (1881 census)

Isle of Anticosti was a census subdivision in Chicoutimi & Saguenay County, Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 676. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.843°N, 64.431°W.
