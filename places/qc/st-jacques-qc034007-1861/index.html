St. Jacques, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Jacques was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 3,254. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.947°N, 73.543°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Jacques had a population of 3,254: 1,650 male and 1,604 female residents.
