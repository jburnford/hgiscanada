Gaspé Bay N. & Sydenham S., Quebec (1911 census)

Gaspé Bay N. & Sydenham S. was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,072. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.930°N, 64.543°W.

Population

In 1911, Gaspé Bay N. & Sydenham S. had a population of 1,072: 548 male and 524 female residents. Population density was 14.7 people per square mile.
