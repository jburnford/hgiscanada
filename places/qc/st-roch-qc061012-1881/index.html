St. Roch, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Roch was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,020. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112912763. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.901°N, 73.170°W.

Population
