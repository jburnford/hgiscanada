Grantham, Quebec (1871 census)

Grantham was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 829. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.908°N, 72.556°W.

Population

In 1871, Grantham had a population of 829: 413 male and 416 female residents. Population density was 17.7 people per square mile.
