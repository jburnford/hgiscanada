Ste. Anne des Plaines, Quebec (1911 census)

Ste. Anne des Plaines was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,778. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.765°N, 73.815°W.
