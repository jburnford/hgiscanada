St. Henri, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Henri was a census subdivision in L'Assomption County, Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,843. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112911749. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.769°N, 73.627°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Henri had a population of 2,843: 1,415 male and 1,428 female residents. Population density was 56.9 people per square mile.
