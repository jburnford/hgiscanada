St. Jean l'Évangéliste, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Jean l'Évangéliste was a census subdivision in Bonaventure County, Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,638. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.177°N, 66.361°W.

Population

In 1881, St. Jean l'Évangéliste had a population of 1,638: 855 male and 783 female residents. Population density was 14.5 people per square mile.
