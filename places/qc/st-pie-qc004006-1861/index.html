St. Pie, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Pie was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 4,254. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.510°N, 72.900°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Pie had a population of 4,254: 2,234 male and 2,020 female residents.
