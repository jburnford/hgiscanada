La Baie du Febvre, Quebec (1911 census)

La Baie du Febvre was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,725. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.137°N, 72.707°W.

Population

In 1911, La Baie du Febvre had a population of 1,725: 850 male and 875 female residents. Population density was 40.2 people per square mile.
