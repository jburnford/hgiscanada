St. Jérusalem, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Jérusalem was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,784. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.667°N, 74.281°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Jérusalem had a population of 1,784: 919 male and 865 female residents.
