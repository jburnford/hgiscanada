St. Zotique, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Zotique was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 732. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112912879. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.239°N, 74.300°W.
