St. Timothée, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Timothée was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,959. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.271°N, 74.027°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Timothée had a population of 2,959: 1,469 male and 1,490 female residents.
