Godmanchester, Quebec (1871 census)

Godmanchester was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 2,055. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.080°N, 74.237°W.

Population

In 1871, Godmanchester had a population of 2,055: 1,047 male and 1,008 female residents. Population density was 37.0 people per square mile.
