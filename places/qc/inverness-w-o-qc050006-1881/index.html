Inverness W-O, Quebec (1881 census)

Inverness W-O was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 631. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.268°N, 71.557°W.

Population

In 1881, Inverness W-O had a population of 631: 331 male and 300 female residents. Population density was 9.9 people per square mile.
