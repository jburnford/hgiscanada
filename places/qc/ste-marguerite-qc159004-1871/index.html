Ste. Marguerite, Quebec (1871 census)

Ste. Marguerite was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,571. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.508°N, 70.928°W.
