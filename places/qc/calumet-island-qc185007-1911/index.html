Calumet Island, Quebec (1911 census)

Calumet Island was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,208. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3205666. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.739°N, 76.685°W.
