Ascot, Quebec (1881 census)

Ascot was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,970. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.370°N, 71.861°W.

Population

In 1881, Ascot had a population of 2,970: 1,562 male and 1,408 female residents. Population density was 32.9 people per square mile.
