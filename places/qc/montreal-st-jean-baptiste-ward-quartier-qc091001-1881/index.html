Montreal, St. Jean-Baptiste, Ward—Quartier, Quebec (1881 census)

Montreal, St. Jean-Baptiste, Ward—Quartier was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 5,874. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.523°N, 73.579°W.

Population

In 1881, Montreal, St. Jean-Baptiste, Ward—Quartier had a population of 5,874: 2,808 male and 3,066 female residents. Population density was 9809.8 people per square mile.
