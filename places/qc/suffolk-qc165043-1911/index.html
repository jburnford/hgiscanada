Suffolk, Quebec (1911 census)

Suffolk was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,176. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q28469433. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.913°N, 74.945°W.
