Three Rivers, Parish, Quebec (1861 census)

Three Rivers, Parish was a township in St. Maurice County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 607. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.351°N, 72.632°W.

Population

In 1861, Three Rivers, Parish had a population of 607: 291 male and 316 female residents.
