St. Anaclet, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Anaclet was a census subdivision in Rimouski County, Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,437. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q106644325. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.445°N, 68.375°W.
