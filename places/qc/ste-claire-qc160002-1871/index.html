Ste. Claire, Quebec (1871 census)

Ste. Claire was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 2,481. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.581°N, 70.865°W.

Population

In 1871, Ste. Claire had a population of 2,481: 1,203 male and 1,278 female residents.
