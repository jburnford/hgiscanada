Ste. Marthe, Quebec (1861 census)

Ste. Marthe was a township in Vaudreuil County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,659. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.399°N, 74.300°W.

Population

In 1861, Ste. Marthe had a population of 2,659: 1,382 male and 1,277 female residents.
