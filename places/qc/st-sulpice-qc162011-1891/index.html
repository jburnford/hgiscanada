St. Sulpice, Quebec (1891 census)

St. Sulpice was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 622. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.827°N, 73.359°W.

Population

In 1891, St. Sulpice had a population of 622: 325 male and 297 female residents.
