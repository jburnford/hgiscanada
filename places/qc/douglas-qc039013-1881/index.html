Douglas, Quebec (1881 census)

Douglas was a census subdivision in Gaspé County, Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,260. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.744°N, 64.384°W.

Population

In 1881, Douglas had a population of 1,260: 647 male and 613 female residents.
