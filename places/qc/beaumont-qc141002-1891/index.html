Beaumont, Quebec (1891 census)

Beaumont was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 811. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q2893240. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.818°N, 70.986°W.
