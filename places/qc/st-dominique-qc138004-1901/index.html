St. Dominique, Quebec (1901 census)

St. Dominique was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 1,552. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.581°N, 72.830°W.

Population

In 1901, St. Dominique had a population of 1,552: 752 male and 800 female residents. Population density was 56.4 people per square mile.
