Ascot, Quebec (1911 census)

Ascot was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,799. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.363°N, 71.869°W.

Population

In 1911, Ascot had a population of 2,799: 1,511 male and 1,288 female residents. Population density was 34.3 people per square mile.
