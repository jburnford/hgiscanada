Auckland, Quebec (1911 census)

Auckland was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,248. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.245°N, 71.449°W.

Population

In 1911, Auckland had a population of 1,248: 654 male and 594 female residents. Population density was 17.0 people per square mile.
