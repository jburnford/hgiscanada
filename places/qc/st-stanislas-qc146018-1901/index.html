St. Stanislas, Quebec (1901 census)

St. Stanislas was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 2,294. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.624°N, 72.413°W.

Population

In 1901, St. Stanislas had a population of 2,294: 1,211 male and 1,083 female residents. Population density was 63.4 people per square mile.
