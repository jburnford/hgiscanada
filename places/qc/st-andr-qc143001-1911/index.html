St. André, Quebec (1911 census)

St. André was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 826. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.637°N, 72.561°W.

Population

In 1911, St. André had a population of 826: 435 male and 391 female residents. Population density was 29.4 people per square mile.
