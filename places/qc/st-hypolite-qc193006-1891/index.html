St. Hypolite, Quebec (1891 census)

St. Hypolite was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 856. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.922°N, 74.030°W.

Population

In 1891, St. Hypolite had a population of 856: 442 male and 414 female residents. Population density was 14.1 people per square mile.
