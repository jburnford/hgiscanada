St. Vincent de Paul, Quebec (1891 census)

St. Vincent de Paul was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,519. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112912862. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.627°N, 73.665°W.

In 1891, St. Vincent de Paul had a population of 2,519: 1,279 male and 1,240 female residents.
