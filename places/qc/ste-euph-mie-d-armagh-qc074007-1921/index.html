Ste. Euphémie d'Armagh, Quebec (1921 census)

Ste. Euphémie d'Armagh was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 797. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.795°N, 70.475°W.

Population

In 1921, Ste. Euphémie d'Armagh had a population of 797: 433 male and 364 female residents. Population density was 19.1 people per square mile.
