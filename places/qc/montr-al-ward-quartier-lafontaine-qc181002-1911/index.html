Montréal, ward-quartier Lafontaine, Quebec (1911 census)

Montréal, ward-quartier Lafontaine was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 25,026. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.520°N, 73.566°W.

Population

In 1911, Montréal, ward-quartier Lafontaine had a population of 25,026: 12,021 male and 13,005 female residents.
