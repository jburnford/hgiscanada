St. Eustache, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Eustache was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,703. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.568°N, 73.929°W.

Population

In 1911, St. Eustache had a population of 1,703: 886 male and 817 female residents. Population density was 47.4 people per square mile.
