Isle Perrot, Quebec (1851 census)

Isle Perrot was a township in Vaudreuil County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 891. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.374°N, 73.938°W.

Population

In 1851, Isle Perrot had a population of 891: 433 male and 458 female residents.
