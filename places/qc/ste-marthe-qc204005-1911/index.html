Ste. Marthe, Quebec (1911 census)

Ste. Marthe was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,312. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3464374. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.399°N, 74.300°W.
