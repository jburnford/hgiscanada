St. Canut, Quebec (1871 census)

St. Canut was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 645. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.707°N, 74.077°W.

In 1871, St. Canut had a population of 645: 319 male and 326 female residents.
