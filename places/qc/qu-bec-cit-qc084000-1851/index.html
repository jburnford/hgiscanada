Québec, cité, Quebec (1851 census)

Québec, cité was a city in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 42,052. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.811°N, 71.218°W.

Population

In 1851, Québec, cité had a population of 42,052: 20,966 male and 21,086 female residents.
