St. Patrice de Sherrington, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Patrice de Sherrington was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,838. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.156°N, 73.525°W.
