St. Alexis, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Alexis was a census subdivision in Montcalm County, Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,500. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.943°N, 73.632°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Alexis had a population of 1,500: 747 male and 753 female residents.
