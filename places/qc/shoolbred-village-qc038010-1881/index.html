Shoolbred, Village, Quebec (1881 census)

Shoolbred, Village was a village in Bonaventure County, Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 369. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.140°N, 66.320°W.

Population

In 1881, Shoolbred, Village had a population of 369: 187 male and 182 female residents. Population density was 369.0 people per square mile.
