St. Armand E, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Armand E was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,825. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.046°N, 72.812°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Armand E had a population of 1,825: 880 male and 945 female residents.
