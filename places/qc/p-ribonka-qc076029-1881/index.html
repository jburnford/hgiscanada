Péribonka, Quebec (1881 census)

Péribonka was a census subdivision in Chicoutimi & Saguenay County, Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 322. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.705°N, 71.857°W.

Population

In 1881, Péribonka had a population of 322: 165 male and 157 female residents. Population density was 0.4 people per square mile.
