Baie St. Paul, Quebec (1851 census)

Baie St. Paul was a census subdivision in Saguenay County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,212. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.428°N, 70.662°W.

Population

In 1851, Baie St. Paul had a population of 3,212: 1,636 male and 1,576 female residents. Population density was 14.6 people per square mile.
