Woodbridge, Quebec (1861 census)

Woodbridge was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 500. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.455°N, 69.695°W.

In 1861, Woodbridge had a population of 500: 270 male and 230 female residents.
