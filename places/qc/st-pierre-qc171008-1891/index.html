St. Pierre, Quebec (1891 census)

St. Pierre was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,331. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3463184. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.895°N, 70.607°W.

Population

In 1891, St. Pierre had a population of 1,331: 664 male and 667 female residents.
