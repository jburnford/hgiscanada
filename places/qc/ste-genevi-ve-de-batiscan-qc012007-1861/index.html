Ste. Geneviève de Batiscan, Quebec (1861 census)

Ste. Geneviève de Batiscan was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,171. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.542°N, 72.352°W.

In 1861, Ste. Geneviève de Batiscan had a population of 2,171: 1,133 male and 1,038 female residents.
