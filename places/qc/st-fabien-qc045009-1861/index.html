St. Fabien, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Fabien was a census subdivision in Rimouski County, Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,309. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q106815217. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.276°N, 68.838°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Fabien had a population of 1,309: 674 male and 635 female residents.
