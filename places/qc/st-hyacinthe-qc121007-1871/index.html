St. Hyacinthe, Quebec (1871 census)

St. Hyacinthe was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 2,581. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.621°N, 72.987°W.

Population

In 1871, St. Hyacinthe had a population of 2,581: 1,275 male and 1,306 female residents.
