St. Hilaire, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Hilaire was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,589. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.558°N, 73.173°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Hilaire had a population of 1,589: 779 male and 810 female residents.
