St. Roch, Quebec (1871 census)

St. Roch was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 2,523. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.850°N, 73.623°W.

Population

In 1871, St. Roch had a population of 2,523: 1,261 male and 1,262 female residents. Population density was 59.4 people per square mile.
