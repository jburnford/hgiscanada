Lanoraie, Quebec (1861 census)

Lanoraie was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,057. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3217608. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.982°N, 73.256°W.
