Ste. Anne, Quebec (1861 census)

Ste. Anne was a census subdivision in Terrebonne County, Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,994. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.766°N, 73.817°W.

Population

In 1861, Ste. Anne had a population of 1,994: 1,019 male and 975 female residents.
