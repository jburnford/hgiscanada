St. Roch, North—Nord, Quebec (1891 census)

St. Roch, North—Nord was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 3,331. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.831°N, 71.236°W.

In 1891, St. Roch, North—Nord had a population of 3,331: 1,700 male and 1,631 female residents.
