Fraserville, Town—Ville, Quebec (1911 census)

Fraserville, Town—Ville was a town in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 6,774. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.838°N, 69.536°W.

Population

In 1911, Fraserville, Town—Ville had a population of 6,774: 3,334 male and 3,440 female residents. Population density was 903.2 people per square mile.
