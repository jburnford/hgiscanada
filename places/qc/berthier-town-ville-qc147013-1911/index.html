Berthier, Town—Ville, Quebec (1911 census)

Berthier, Town—Ville was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,335. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.084°N, 73.175°W.
