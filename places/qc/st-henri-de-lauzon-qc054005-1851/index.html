St. Henri de Lauzon, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Henri de Lauzon was a census subdivision in Dorchester County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,069. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q99926359. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.693°N, 71.060°W.

Population

In 1851, St. Henri de Lauzon had a population of 3,069: 1,498 male and 1,571 female residents.
