Deschambault, Quebec (1861 census)

Deschambault was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,334. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q985593. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.682°N, 71.979°W.
