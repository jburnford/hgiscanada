Thetford Mines t-v, Quebec (1911 census)

Thetford Mines t-v was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 7,261. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.082°N, 71.302°W.

Population

In 1911, Thetford Mines t-v had a population of 7,261: 3,756 male and 3,505 female residents. Population density was 3575.1 people per square mile.
