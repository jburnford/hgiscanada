La Présentation, Quebec (1861 census)

La Présentation was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,901. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3212031. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.667°N, 73.062°W.
