Québec, ward-quartier Montcalm (Centre), Quebec (1881 census)

Québec, ward-quartier Montcalm (Centre) was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 3,718. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.809°N, 71.221°W.
