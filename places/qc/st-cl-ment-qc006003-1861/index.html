St. Clément, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Clément was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 3,563. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3461994. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.271°N, 73.892°W.
