St. Hyacinthe, City—Cité, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Hyacinthe, City—Cité was a city in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 9,797. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.629°N, 72.954°W.
