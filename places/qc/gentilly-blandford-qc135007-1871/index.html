Gentilly, Blandford, Quebec (1871 census)

Gentilly, Blandford was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 2,884. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.360°N, 72.211°W.

Population

In 1871, Gentilly, Blandford had a population of 2,884: 1,492 male and 1,392 female residents.
