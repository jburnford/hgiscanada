St. Polycarpe, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Polycarpe was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,603. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.300°N, 74.313°W.

Population

In 1881, St. Polycarpe had a population of 2,603: 1,346 male and 1,257 female residents. Population density was 82.0 people per square mile.
