La Pointe Claire, Quebec (1861 census)

La Pointe Claire was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,785. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.451°N, 73.831°W.

Population

In 1861, La Pointe Claire had a population of 1,785: 922 male and 863 female residents.
