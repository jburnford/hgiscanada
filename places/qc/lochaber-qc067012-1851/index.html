Lochaber, Quebec (1851 census)

Lochaber was a township in Outaouais County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,082. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.653°N, 75.269°W.

Population

In 1851, Lochaber had a population of 1,082: 570 male and 512 female residents.
