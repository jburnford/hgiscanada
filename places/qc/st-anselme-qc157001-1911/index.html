St. Anselme, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Anselme was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,080. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112911875. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.632°N, 70.967°W.

Population

In 1911, St. Anselme had a population of 2,080: 1,027 male and 1,053 female residents. Population density was 54.5 people per square mile.
