Gaspé Bay, North, Quebec (1861 census)

Gaspé Bay, North was a township in Gaspé County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 316. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.889°N, 64.431°W.

Population

In 1861, Gaspé Bay, North had a population of 316: 171 male and 145 female residents.
