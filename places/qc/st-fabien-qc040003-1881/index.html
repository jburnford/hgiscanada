St. Fabien, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Fabien was a census subdivision in Rimouski County, Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,816. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q106815217. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.276°N, 68.838°W.
