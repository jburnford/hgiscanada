Verchères, Quebec (1911 census)

Verchères was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,697. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.745°N, 73.327°W.

Population

In 1911, Verchères had a population of 1,697: 815 male and 882 female residents. Population density was 91.3 people per square mile.
