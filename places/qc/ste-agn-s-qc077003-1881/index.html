Ste. Agnès, Quebec (1881 census)

Ste. Agnès was a township in Charlevoix County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,778. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.679°N, 70.364°W.
