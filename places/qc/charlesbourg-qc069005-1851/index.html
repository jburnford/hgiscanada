Charlesbourg, Quebec (1851 census)

Charlesbourg was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,063. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.899°N, 71.306°W.

Population

In 1851, Charlesbourg had a population of 2,063: 1,004 male and 1,059 female residents.
