Ripon, Quebec (1911 census)

Ripon was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,820. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q138732141. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.795°N, 75.147°W.
