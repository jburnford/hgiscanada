St. Jacques, Quebec (1901 census)

St. Jacques was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 2,580. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.955°N, 73.570°W.

Population

In 1901, St. Jacques had a population of 2,580: 1,275 male and 1,305 female residents. Population density was 88.6 people per square mile.
