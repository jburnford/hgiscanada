St. Patrice, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Patrice was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 950. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.422°N, 71.259°W.

Population

In 1911, St. Patrice had a population of 950: 495 male and 455 female residents. Population density was 26.0 people per square mile.
