St. Gédéon, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Gédéon was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,309. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.528°N, 71.858°W.

In 1911, St. Gédéon had a population of 1,309: 663 male and 646 female residents. Population density was 14.4 people per square mile.
