St. Paschal, Quebec (1871 census)

St. Paschal was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 2,658. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.530°N, 69.782°W.

Population

In 1871, St. Paschal had a population of 2,658: 1,322 male and 1,336 female residents.
