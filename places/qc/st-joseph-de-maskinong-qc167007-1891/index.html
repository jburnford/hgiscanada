St. Joseph de Maskinongé, Quebec (1891 census)

St. Joseph de Maskinongé was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,021. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.209°N, 73.031°W.

Population

In 1891, St. Joseph de Maskinongé had a population of 2,021: 1,049 male and 972 female residents. Population density was 68.1 people per square mile.
