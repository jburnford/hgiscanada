La Baie du Febvre, Quebec (1861 census)

La Baie du Febvre was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,982. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112911889. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.127°N, 72.690°W.

Population

In 1861, La Baie du Febvre had a population of 2,982: 1,477 male and 1,505 female residents.
