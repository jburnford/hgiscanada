St. Raymond, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Raymond was a township in Portneuf County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,701. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.873°N, 71.853°W.

Population

In 1851, St. Raymond had a population of 1,701: 893 male and 808 female residents.
