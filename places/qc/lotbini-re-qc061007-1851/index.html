Lotbinière, Quebec (1851 census)

Lotbinière was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,578. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.516°N, 71.808°W.

Population

In 1851, Lotbinière had a population of 3,578: 1,796 male and 1,782 female residents.
