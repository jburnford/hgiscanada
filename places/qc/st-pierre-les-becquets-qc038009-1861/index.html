St. Pierre les Becquets, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Pierre les Becquets was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,994. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3463195. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.425°N, 72.117°W.
