Barford, Quebec (1911 census)

Barford was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 943. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.097°N, 71.747°W.

Population

In 1911, Barford had a population of 943: 489 male and 454 female residents. Population density was 25.2 people per square mile.
