Richelieu, Quebec (1871 census)

Richelieu was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 760. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.411°N, 73.228°W.

Population

In 1871, Richelieu had a population of 760: 364 male and 396 female residents.
