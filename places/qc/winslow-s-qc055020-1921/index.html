Winslow S., Quebec (1921 census)

Winslow S. was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 909. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.711°N, 71.187°W.

Population

In 1921, Winslow S. had a population of 909: 494 male and 415 female residents. Population density was 11.5 people per square mile.
