Buckingham, Quebec (1911 census)

Buckingham was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,394. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q63242700. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.640°N, 75.443°W.
