Franklin, Quebec (1881 census)

Franklin was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,221. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.034°N, 73.923°W.

Population

In 1881, Franklin had a population of 1,221: 621 male and 600 female residents.
