Ste. Rose, Quebec (1871 census)

Ste. Rose was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,816. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.607°N, 73.777°W.

Population

In 1871, Ste. Rose had a population of 1,816: 885 male and 931 female residents. Population density was 69.2 people per square mile.
