Ste. Justine de Newton, Quebec (1911 census)

Ste. Justine de Newton was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,332. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.359°N, 74.382°W.
