St. Antonin, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Antonin was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,271. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112911899. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.741°N, 69.478°W.
