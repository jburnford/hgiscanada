St. Antoine, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Antoine was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,786. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.641°N, 71.549°W.

Population

In 1881, St. Antoine had a population of 1,786: 865 male and 921 female residents. Population density was 57.9 people per square mile.
