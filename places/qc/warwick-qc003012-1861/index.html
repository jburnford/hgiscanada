Warwick, Quebec (1861 census)

Warwick was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,380. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.973°N, 72.073°W.

Population

In 1861, Warwick had a population of 1,380: 761 male and 619 female residents.
