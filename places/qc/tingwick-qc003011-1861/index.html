Tingwick, Quebec (1861 census)

Tingwick was a township in Arthabaska County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,227. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.885°N, 71.906°W.

Population

In 1861, Tingwick had a population of 2,227: 1,140 male and 1,087 female residents.
