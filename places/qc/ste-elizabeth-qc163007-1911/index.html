Ste. Elizabeth, Quebec (1911 census)

Ste. Elizabeth was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,372. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.101°N, 73.378°W.

Population

In 1911, Ste. Elizabeth had a population of 2,372: 1,175 male and 1,197 female residents. Population density was 56.9 people per square mile.
