St. Pierre, Quebec (1871 census)

St. Pierre was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,292. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.895°N, 70.607°W.

Population

In 1871, St. Pierre had a population of 1,292: 634 male and 658 female residents. Population density was 40.5 people per square mile.
