Hatley, Quebec (1851 census)

Hatley was a census subdivision in Stanstead County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,014. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.229°N, 72.006°W.

Population

In 1851, Hatley had a population of 2,014: 1,033 male and 981 female residents.
