Ste. Sophie, Quebec (1891 census)

Ste. Sophie was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,255. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.831°N, 73.919°W.

Population

In 1891, Ste. Sophie had a population of 1,255: 652 male and 603 female residents.
