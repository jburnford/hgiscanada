Weedon, Quebec (1911 census)

Weedon was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,254. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.689°N, 71.451°W.

Population

In 1911, Weedon had a population of 1,254: 671 male and 583 female residents. Population density was 18.4 people per square mile.
