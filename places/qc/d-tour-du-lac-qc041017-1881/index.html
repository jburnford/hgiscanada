Détour du Lac, Quebec (1881 census)

Détour du Lac was a census subdivision in Temiscouata County, Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,276. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.577°N, 68.861°W.

Population

In 1881, Détour du Lac had a population of 1,276: 687 male and 589 female residents.
