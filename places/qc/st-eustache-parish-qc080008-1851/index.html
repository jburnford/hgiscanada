St. Eustache, Parish, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Eustache, Parish was a township in Two Mountain County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,365. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.559°N, 73.954°W.

Population

In 1851, St. Eustache, Parish had a population of 3,365: 1,647 male and 1,718 female residents.
