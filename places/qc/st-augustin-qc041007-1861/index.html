St. Augustin, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Augustin was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,722. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q142102. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.750°N, 71.479°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Augustin had a population of 1,722: 831 male and 891 female residents.
