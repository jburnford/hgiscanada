St. Jean Port Joli, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Jean Port Joli was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,622. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.196°N, 70.249°W.

Population

In 1881, St. Jean Port Joli had a population of 2,622: 1,312 male and 1,310 female residents. Population density was 95.3 people per square mile.
