Chicoutimi, Township, Quebec (1851 census)

Chicoutimi, Township was a township in Saguenay County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,200. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.387°N, 71.056°W.

Population

In 1851, Chicoutimi, Township had a population of 1,200: 609 male and 591 female residents.
