St. Jacques le Mineur, Quebec (1871 census)

St. Jacques le Mineur was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,835. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.266°N, 73.432°W.

Population

In 1871, St. Jacques le Mineur had a population of 1,835: 894 male and 941 female residents. Population density was 68.2 people per square mile.
