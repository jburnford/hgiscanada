Hull W.-O., Quebec (1921 census)

Hull W.-O. was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 1,005. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.527°N, 75.850°W.

Population

In 1921, Hull W.-O. had a population of 1,005: 503 male and 502 female residents. Population density was 19.4 people per square mile.
