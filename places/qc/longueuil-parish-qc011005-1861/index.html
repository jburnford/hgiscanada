Longueuil, Parish, Quebec (1861 census)

Longueuil, Parish was a parish in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,049. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.533°N, 73.469°W.

Population

In 1861, Longueuil, Parish had a population of 1,049: 577 male and 472 female residents.
