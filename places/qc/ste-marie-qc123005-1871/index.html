Ste. Marie, Quebec (1871 census)

Ste. Marie was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,698. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.436°N, 73.164°W.

Population

In 1871, Ste. Marie had a population of 1,698: 849 male and 849 female residents. Population density was 55.6 people per square mile.
