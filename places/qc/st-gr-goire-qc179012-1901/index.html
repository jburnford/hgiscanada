St. Grégoire, Quebec (1901 census)

St. Grégoire was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 1,706. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.261°N, 72.493°W.

Population

In 1901, St. Grégoire had a population of 1,706: 841 male and 865 female residents. Population density was 38.4 people per square mile.
