St. Damase, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Damase was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,469. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112912038. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.520°N, 73.020°W.
