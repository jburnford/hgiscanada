Montmini, Township, Quebec (1851 census)

Montmini, Township was a township in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 19. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.743°N, 70.335°W.

Population

In 1851, Montmini, Township had a population of 19: 10 male and 9 female residents.
