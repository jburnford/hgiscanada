St. Charles, Quebec (1871 census)

St. Charles was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,176. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112911987. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.648°N, 73.147°W.

Population

In 1871, St. Charles had a population of 1,176: 617 male and 559 female residents. Population density was 41.0 people per square mile.
