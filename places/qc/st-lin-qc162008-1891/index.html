St. Lin, Quebec (1891 census)

St. Lin was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,541. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.853°N, 73.778°W.

Population

In 1891, St. Lin had a population of 1,541: 757 male and 784 female residents.
