Outremont, Village, Quebec (1911 census)

Outremont, Village was a village in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 4,820. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.517°N, 73.611°W.

In 1911, Outremont, Village had a population of 4,820: 2,243 male and 2,577 female residents. Population density was 3428.2 people per square mile.
