St. Côme de Kennebec, Quebec (1891 census)

St. Côme de Kennebec was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,279. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.059°N, 70.518°W.

Population

In 1891, St. Côme de Kennebec had a population of 1,279: 657 male and 622 female residents.
