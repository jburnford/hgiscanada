Nicolet, Town—Ville, Quebec (1851 census)

Nicolet, Town—Ville was a town in Nicolet County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,095. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.212°N, 72.601°W.

Population

In 1851, Nicolet, Town—Ville had a population of 3,095: 1,554 male and 1,541 female residents. Population density was 79.6 people per square mile.
