Boucherville, Quebec (1861 census)

Boucherville was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,813. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q141924. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.589°N, 73.419°W.
