St. Louis de Metabetchouan, Quebec (1891 census)

St. Louis de Metabetchouan was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,155. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.441°N, 72.061°W.

In 1891, St. Louis de Metabetchouan had a population of 1,155: 589 male and 566 female residents.
