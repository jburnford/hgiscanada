St. Pierre, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Pierre was a township in Montmorency County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,022. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.889°N, 71.068°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Pierre had a population of 1,022: 491 male and 531 female residents.
