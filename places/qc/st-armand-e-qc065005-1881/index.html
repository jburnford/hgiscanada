St. Armand E, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Armand E was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,190. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.046°N, 72.811°W.

In 1881, St. Armand E had a population of 1,190: 602 male and 588 female residents. Population density was 24.4 people per square mile.
