Ste. Brigide, Quebec (1861 census)

Ste. Brigide was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,839. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112912933. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.313°N, 73.052°W.
