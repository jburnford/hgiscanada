L’Acadie (part), Quebec (1851 census)

L’Acadie (part) was a township in Huntingdon County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 850. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.338°N, 73.372°W.

Population

In 1851, L’Acadie (part) had a population of 850: 437 male and 413 female residents.
