St. Valentin, Quebec (1871 census)

St. Valentin was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 2,148. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.151°N, 73.313°W.

Population

In 1871, St. Valentin had a population of 2,148: 1,120 male and 1,028 female residents. Population density was 55.1 people per square mile.
