St. Joseph, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Joseph was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,346. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3462609. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.532°N, 74.004°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Joseph had a population of 1,346: 669 male and 677 female residents.
