Notre-Dame du Lac, Quebec (1911 census)

Notre-Dame du Lac was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,868. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112911764. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.615°N, 68.802°W.
