Notre-Dame du Lac, Quebec (1911 census)

Notre-Dame du Lac was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,868. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.615°N, 68.802°W.

Population

In 1911, Notre-Dame du Lac had a population of 1,868: 952 male and 916 female residents. Population density was 37.4 people per square mile.
