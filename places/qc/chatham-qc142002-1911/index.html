Chatham, Quebec (1911 census)

Chatham was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,576. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.670°N, 74.442°W.

Population

In 1911, Chatham had a population of 3,576: 1,832 male and 1,744 female residents. Population density was 34.3 people per square mile.
