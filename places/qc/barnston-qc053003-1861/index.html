Barnston, Quebec (1861 census)

Barnston was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 3,098. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q28770225. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.084°N, 71.901°W.
