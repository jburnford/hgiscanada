St. Simon, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Simon was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,362. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.719°N, 72.846°W.

In 1911, St. Simon had a population of 1,362: 679 male and 683 female residents. Population density was 49.7 people per square mile.
