St. Ours, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Ours was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,394. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q142405. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.884°N, 73.132°W.

Population

In 1851, St. Ours had a population of 3,394: 1,718 male and 1,676 female residents.
