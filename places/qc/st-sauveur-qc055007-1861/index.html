St. Sauveur, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Sauveur was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,821. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112912781. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.878°N, 74.157°W.
