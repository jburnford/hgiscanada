St. Grégoire, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Grégoire was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,484. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.258°N, 72.506°W.

Population

In 1911, St. Grégoire had a population of 1,484: 746 male and 738 female residents. Population density was 32.4 people per square mile.
