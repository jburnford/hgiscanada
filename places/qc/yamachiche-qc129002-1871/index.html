Yamachiche, Quebec (1871 census)

Yamachiche was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 2,365. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.308°N, 72.830°W.

Population

In 1871, Yamachiche had a population of 2,365: 1,179 male and 1,186 female residents. Population density was 57.4 people per square mile.
