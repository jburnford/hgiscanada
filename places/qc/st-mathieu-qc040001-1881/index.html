St. Mathieu, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Mathieu was a census subdivision in Rimouski County, Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,175. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q511005. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.168°N, 68.964°W.
