St. Lin, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Lin was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,585. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112912498. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.852°N, 73.774°W.
