St. Martin, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Martin was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 458. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.978°N, 70.643°W.

Population

In 1881, St. Martin had a population of 458: 249 male and 209 female residents. Population density was 5.6 people per square mile.
