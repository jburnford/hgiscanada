Kingsey Falls, Quebec (1881 census)

Kingsey Falls was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 764. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.885°N, 72.110°W.

Population

In 1881, Kingsey Falls had a population of 764: 391 male and 373 female residents. Population density was 13.0 people per square mile.
