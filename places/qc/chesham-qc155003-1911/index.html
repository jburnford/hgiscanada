Chesham, Quebec (1911 census)

Chesham was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 897. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.376°N, 71.058°W.

Population

In 1911, Chesham had a population of 897: 470 male and 427 female residents. Population density was 11.5 people per square mile.
