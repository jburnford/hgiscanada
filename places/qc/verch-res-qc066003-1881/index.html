Verchères, Quebec (1881 census)

Verchères was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,308. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.745°N, 73.327°W.

Population

In 1881, Verchères had a population of 2,308: 1,158 male and 1,150 female residents. Population density was 79.8 people per square mile.
