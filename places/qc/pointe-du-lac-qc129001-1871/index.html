Pointe du Lac, Quebec (1871 census)

Pointe du Lac was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,467. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.316°N, 72.704°W.

Population

In 1871, Pointe du Lac had a population of 1,467: 751 male and 716 female residents.
