Escoumins, Iberville and Mille Vaches, Quebec (1861 census)

Escoumins, Iberville and Mille Vaches was a township in Saguenay County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,029. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.547°N, 69.343°W.

Population

In 1861, Escoumins, Iberville and Mille Vaches had a population of 1,029: 646 male and 383 female residents.
