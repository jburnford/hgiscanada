Farnham, Quebec (1861 census)

Farnham was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,530. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.270°N, 72.960°W.

Population

In 1861, Farnham had a population of 2,530: 1,335 male and 1,195 female residents.
