Berthier, Quebec (1911 census)

Berthier was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,814. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.089°N, 73.215°W.

Population

In 1911, Berthier had a population of 1,814: 893 male and 921 female residents. Population density was 61.0 people per square mile.
