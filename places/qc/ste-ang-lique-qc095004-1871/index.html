Ste. Angélique, Quebec (1871 census)

Ste. Angélique was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,738. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.640°N, 75.044°W.

Population

In 1871, Ste. Angélique had a population of 1,738: 947 male and 791 female residents.
