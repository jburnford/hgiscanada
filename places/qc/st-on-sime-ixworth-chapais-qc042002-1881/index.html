St. Onésime, Ixworth & Chapais, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Onésime, Ixworth & Chapais was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 789. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.236°N, 69.741°W.

Population

In 1881, St. Onésime, Ixworth & Chapais had a population of 789: 396 male and 393 female residents. Population density was 3.4 people per square mile.
