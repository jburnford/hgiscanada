Sutton, Quebec (1861 census)

Sutton was a census subdivision in Brome County, Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 3,151. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q142521. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.086°N, 72.588°W.

Population

In 1861, Sutton had a population of 3,151: 1,630 male and 1,521 female residents.
