St. Hermas, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Hermas was a census subdivision in Two Mountains County, Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,583. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112912272. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.611°N, 74.197°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Hermas had a population of 1,583: 797 male and 786 female residents.
