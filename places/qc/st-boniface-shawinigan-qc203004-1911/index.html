St. Boniface (Shawinigan), Quebec (1911 census)

St. Boniface (Shawinigan) was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,513. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112911955. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.524°N, 72.846°W.
