St. Eustache, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Eustache was a census subdivision in Two Mountains County, Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,372. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q141505. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.570°N, 73.933°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Eustache had a population of 2,372: 1,167 male and 1,205 female residents.
