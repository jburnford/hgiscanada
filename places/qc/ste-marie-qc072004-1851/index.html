Ste. Marie, Quebec (1851 census)

Ste. Marie was a township in Rouville County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 4,028. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.421°N, 73.141°W.

Population

In 1851, Ste. Marie had a population of 4,028: 2,049 male and 1,979 female residents.
