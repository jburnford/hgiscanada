St. Joachim, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Joachim was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,296. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112912408. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.166°N, 70.778°W.
