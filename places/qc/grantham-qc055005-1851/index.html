Grantham, Quebec (1851 census)

Grantham was a census subdivision in Drummond County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,401. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.858°N, 72.606°W.

Population

In 1851, Grantham had a population of 1,401: 718 male and 683 female residents. Population density was 11.3 people per square mile.
