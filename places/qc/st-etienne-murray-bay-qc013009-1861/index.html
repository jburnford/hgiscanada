St. Etienne (Murray Bay), Quebec (1861 census)

St. Etienne (Murray Bay) was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,766. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.692°N, 70.185°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Etienne (Murray Bay) had a population of 2,766: 1,418 male and 1,348 female residents.
