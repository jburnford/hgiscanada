Wickham, Quebec (1881 census)

Wickham was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 435. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.817°N, 72.408°W.

Population

In 1881, Wickham had a population of 435: 227 male and 208 female residents. Population density was 10.4 people per square mile.
