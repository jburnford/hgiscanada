Roxton Falls, Village, Quebec (1891 census)

Roxton Falls, Village was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 908. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.577°N, 72.524°W.

Population

In 1891, Roxton Falls, Village had a population of 908: 437 male and 471 female residents.
