Bristol, Quebec (1851 census)

Bristol was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,491. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.564°N, 76.369°W.

Population

In 1851, Bristol had a population of 1,491: 862 male and 629 female residents.
