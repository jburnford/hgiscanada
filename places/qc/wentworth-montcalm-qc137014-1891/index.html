Wentworth & Montcalm, Quebec (1891 census)

Wentworth & Montcalm was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 898. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.877°N, 74.445°W.

Population

In 1891, Wentworth & Montcalm had a population of 898: 526 male and 372 female residents. Population density was 5.3 people per square mile.
