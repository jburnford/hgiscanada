Montréal, ward-quartier St. Laurent, Quebec (1881 census)

Montréal, ward-quartier St. Laurent was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 14,318. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.509°N, 73.570°W.
