Québec, Palais, Ward—Quartier, Quebec (1911 census)

Québec, Palais, Ward—Quartier was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,872. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.815°N, 71.209°W.
