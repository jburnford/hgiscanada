Cabano, Packington & UNO, Quebec (1911 census)

Cabano, Packington & UNO was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,261. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.456°N, 68.928°W.

Population
