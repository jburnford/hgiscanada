Baie St. Paul, Quebec (1891 census)

Baie St. Paul was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 3,760. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q139231. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.428°N, 70.663°W.
