Carleton, Quebec (1891 census)

Carleton was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,078. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.166°N, 66.159°W.

Population

In 1891, Carleton had a population of 1,078: 534 male and 544 female residents. Population density was 16.7 people per square mile.
