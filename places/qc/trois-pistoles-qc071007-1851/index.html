Trois Pistoles, Quebec (1851 census)

Trois Pistoles was a township in Rimouski County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,847. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.063°N, 69.083°W.

Population

In 1851, Trois Pistoles had a population of 2,847: 1,434 male and 1,413 female residents.
