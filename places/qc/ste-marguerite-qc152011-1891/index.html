Ste. Marguerite, Quebec (1891 census)

Ste. Marguerite was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,260. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.508°N, 70.926°W.

Population

In 1891, Ste. Marguerite had a population of 1,260: 642 male and 618 female residents. Population density was 36.4 people per square mile.
