Eaton, Quebec (1911 census)

Eaton was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,765. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.376°N, 71.653°W.

Population

In 1911, Eaton had a population of 1,765: 952 male and 813 female residents. Population density was 17.8 people per square mile.
