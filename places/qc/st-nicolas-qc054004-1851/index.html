St. Nicolas, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Nicolas was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,719. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.642°N, 71.334°W.

Population

In 1851, St. Nicolas had a population of 2,719: 1,346 male and 1,373 female residents.
