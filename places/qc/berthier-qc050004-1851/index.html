Berthier, Quebec (1851 census)

Berthier was a township in Berthier County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 4,062. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.088°N, 73.209°W.

Population

In 1851, Berthier had a population of 4,062: 1,913 male and 2,149 female residents.
