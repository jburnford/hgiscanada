St. Jérôme, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Jérôme was a township in Chicoutimi & Saguenay County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,803. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.434°N, 71.885°W.

Population

In 1881, St. Jérôme had a population of 1,803: 916 male and 887 female residents.
