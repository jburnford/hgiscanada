St. Norbert, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Norbert was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 820. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.095°N, 71.862°W.

Population

In 1881, St. Norbert had a population of 820: 408 male and 412 female residents. Population density was 38.5 people per square mile.
