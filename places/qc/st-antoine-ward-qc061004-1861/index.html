St. Antoine Ward, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Antoine Ward was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 15,190. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.498°N, 73.578°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Antoine Ward had a population of 15,190: 7,173 male and 8,017 female residents.
