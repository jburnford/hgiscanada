St. Modeste, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Modeste was a township in Temiscouata County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 733. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.823°N, 69.388°W.
