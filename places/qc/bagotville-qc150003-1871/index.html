Bagotville, Quebec (1871 census)

Bagotville was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,598. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.363°N, 70.908°W.

In 1871, Bagotville had a population of 1,598: 831 male and 767 female residents.
