St. Fabien, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Fabien was a census subdivision in Rimouski County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 988. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.276°N, 68.838°W.

Population

In 1851, St. Fabien had a population of 988: 530 male and 458 female residents. Population density was 14.7 people per square mile.
