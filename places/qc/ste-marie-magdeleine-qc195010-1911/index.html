Ste. Marie-Magdeleine, Quebec (1911 census)

Ste. Marie-Magdeleine was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,039. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.585°N, 73.076°W.
