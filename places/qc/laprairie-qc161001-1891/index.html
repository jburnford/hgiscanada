Laprairie, Quebec (1891 census)

Laprairie was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,574. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q142092. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.419°N, 73.459°W.
