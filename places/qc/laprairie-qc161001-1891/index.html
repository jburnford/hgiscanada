Laprairie, Quebec (1891 census)

Laprairie was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,574. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.419°N, 73.459°W.

Population

In 1891, Laprairie had a population of 1,574: 799 male and 775 female residents.
