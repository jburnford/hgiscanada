St. François, Quebec (1861 census)

St. François was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,890. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3462179. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.863°N, 70.704°W.
