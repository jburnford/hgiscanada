St. Charles Borromée, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Charles Borromée was a census subdivision in Berthier County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,570. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112911994. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.035°N, 73.445°W.
