Tingwick, Quebec (1871 census)

Tingwick was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,177. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.845°N, 71.917°W.

Population

In 1871, Tingwick had a population of 1,177: 632 male and 545 female residents. Population density was 24.8 people per square mile.
