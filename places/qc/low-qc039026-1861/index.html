Low, Quebec (1861 census)

Low was a census subdivision in Outaouais County, Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 822. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q28230530. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.831°N, 76.037°W.
