Barford, Quebec (1861 census)

Barford was a census subdivision in Stanstead County, Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 700. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.085°N, 71.735°W.

Population

In 1861, Barford had a population of 700: 361 male and 339 female residents. Population density was 10.8 people per square mile.
