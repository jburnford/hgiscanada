Youville, Quebec (1911 census)

Youville was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,394. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.539°N, 73.641°W.

Population

In 1911, Youville had a population of 2,394: 1,232 male and 1,162 female residents.
