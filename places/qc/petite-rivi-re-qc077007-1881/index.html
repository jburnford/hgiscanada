Petite Rivière, Quebec (1881 census)

Petite Rivière was a census subdivision in Charlevoix County, Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 765. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.310°N, 70.608°W.

Population

In 1881, Petite Rivière had a population of 765: 387 male and 378 female residents. Population density was 14.3 people per square mile.
