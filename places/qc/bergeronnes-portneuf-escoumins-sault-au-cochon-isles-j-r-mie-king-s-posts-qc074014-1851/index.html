Bergeronnes, Portneuf, Escoumins, Sault au Cochon, Isles Jérémie & King's Posts, Quebec (1851 census)

Bergeronnes, Portneuf, Escoumins, Sault au Cochon, Isles Jérémie & King's Posts was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,284. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.681°N, 69.167°W.

Population

In 1851, Bergeronnes, Portneuf, Escoumins, Sault au Cochon, Isles Jérémie & King's Posts had a population of 1,284: 843 male and 441 female residents.
