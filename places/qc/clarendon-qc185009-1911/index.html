Clarendon, Quebec (1911 census)

Clarendon was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,421. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q2975590. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.631°N, 76.519°W.
