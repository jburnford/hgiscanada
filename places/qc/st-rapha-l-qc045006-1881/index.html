St. Raphaël, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Raphaël was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,644. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112912733. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.784°N, 70.737°W.
