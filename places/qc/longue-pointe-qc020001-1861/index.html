Longue Pointe, Quebec (1861 census)

Longue Pointe was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,055. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.596°N, 73.547°W.

In 1861, Longue Pointe had a population of 1,055: 502 male and 553 female residents.
