Charlesbourg, Quebec (1911 census)

Charlesbourg was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,127. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.868°N, 71.278°W.

Population

In 1911, Charlesbourg had a population of 2,127: 1,088 male and 1,039 female residents. Population density was 74.3 people per square mile.
