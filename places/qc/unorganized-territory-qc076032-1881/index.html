Unorganized Territory, Quebec (1881 census)

Unorganized Territory was a township in Chicoutimi & Saguenay County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,416. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.230°N, 68.075°W.
