Dunham, Quebec (1911 census)

Dunham was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,194. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.150°N, 72.786°W.

Population

In 1911, Dunham had a population of 2,194: 1,138 male and 1,056 female residents. Population density was 24.6 people per square mile.
