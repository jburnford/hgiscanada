Chambly, Village, Quebec (1861 census)

Chambly, Village was a village in Chambly County, Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,379. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.450°N, 73.290°W.

Population

In 1861, Chambly, Village had a population of 1,379: 644 male and 735 female residents.
