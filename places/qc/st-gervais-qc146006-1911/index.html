St. Gervais, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Gervais was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,017. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3462315. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.709°N, 70.862°W.

Population

In 1911, St. Gervais had a population of 2,017: 1,011 male and 1,006 female residents. Population density was 47.9 people per square mile.
