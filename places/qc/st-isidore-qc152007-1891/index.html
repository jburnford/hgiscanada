St. Isidore, Quebec (1891 census)

St. Isidore was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,046. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3462458. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.579°N, 71.097°W.
