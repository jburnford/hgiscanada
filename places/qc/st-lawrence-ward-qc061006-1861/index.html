St. Lawrence Ward, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Lawrence Ward was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 12,898. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.509°N, 73.569°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Lawrence Ward had a population of 12,898: 6,131 male and 6,767 female residents.
