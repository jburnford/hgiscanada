Gaspé Bay N & Sydenham, Township, Quebec (1851 census)

Gaspé Bay N & Sydenham, Township was a township in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 288. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.064°N, 64.774°W.

Population

In 1851, Gaspé Bay N & Sydenham, Township had a population of 288: 154 male and 134 female residents.
