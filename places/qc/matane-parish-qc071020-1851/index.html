Matane, Parish, Quebec (1851 census)

Matane, Parish was a township in Rimouski County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,083. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.830°N, 67.470°W.
