Buckingham, Village, Quebec (1921 census)

Buckingham, Village was a village in Quebec, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 3,835. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.579°N, 75.418°W.

Population

In 1921, Buckingham, Village had a population of 3,835: 1,937 male and 1,898 female residents. Population density was 1208.9 people per square mile.
