St. Polycarpe, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Polycarpe was a census subdivision in Soulanges County, Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 3,178. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.300°N, 74.313°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Polycarpe had a population of 3,178: 1,596 male and 1,582 female residents. Population density was 100.1 people per square mile.
