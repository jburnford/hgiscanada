Grantham, Quebec (1911 census)

Grantham was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 880. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q63243231. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.886°N, 72.527°W.

Population

In 1911, Grantham had a population of 880: 451 male and 429 female residents. Population density was 19.9 people per square mile.
