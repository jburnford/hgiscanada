Longueuil, Quebec (1911 census)

Longueuil was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,270. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.528°N, 73.471°W.

Population

In 1911, Longueuil had a population of 1,270: 629 male and 641 female residents. Population density was 74.0 people per square mile.
