St. Célestin, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Célestin was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,380. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112911983. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.206°N, 72.408°W.

In 1911, St. Célestin had a population of 1,380: 710 male and 670 female residents. Population density was 41.0 people per square mile.
