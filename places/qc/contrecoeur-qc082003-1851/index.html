Contrecoeur, Quebec (1851 census)

Contrecoeur was a census subdivision in Verchères County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,555. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q141547. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.862°N, 73.230°W.

In 1851, Contrecoeur had a population of 1,555: 784 male and 771 female residents.
