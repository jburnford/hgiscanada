Stanstead, Quebec (1911 census)

Stanstead was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,954. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.090°N, 72.127°W.

Population

In 1911, Stanstead had a population of 2,954: 1,546 male and 1,408 female residents. Population density was 26.4 people per square mile.
