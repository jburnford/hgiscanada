St. Lin, Quebec (1881 census)

St. Lin was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,722. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112912498. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.853°N, 73.777°W.
