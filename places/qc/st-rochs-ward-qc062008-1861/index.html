St. Rochs’ Ward, Quebec (1861 census)

St. Rochs’ Ward was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 9,698. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.818°N, 71.229°W.

Population

In 1861, St. Rochs’ Ward had a population of 9,698: 4,802 male and 4,896 female residents.
