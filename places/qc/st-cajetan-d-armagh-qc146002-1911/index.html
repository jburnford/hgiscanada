St. Cajetan d'Armagh, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Cajetan d'Armagh was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,194. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.758°N, 70.583°W.
