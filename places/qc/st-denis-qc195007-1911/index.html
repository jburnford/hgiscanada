St. Denis, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Denis was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,160. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.750°N, 73.129°W.

Population

In 1911, St. Denis had a population of 1,160: 617 male and 543 female residents. Population density was 33.4 people per square mile.
