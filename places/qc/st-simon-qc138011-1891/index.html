St. Simon, Quebec (1891 census)

St. Simon was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,782. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3463379. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.718°N, 72.846°W.
