St. Evariste de Forsyth, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Evariste de Forsyth was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,890. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.953°N, 70.977°W.

Population

In 1911, St. Evariste de Forsyth had a population of 1,890: 961 male and 929 female residents. Population density was 27.5 people per square mile.
