Ste. Rose, par., Quebec (1921 census)

Ste. Rose, par. was a parish in Quebec, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 1,357. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112913106. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.607°N, 73.778°W.
