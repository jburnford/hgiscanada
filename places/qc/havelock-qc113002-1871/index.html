Havelock, Quebec (1871 census)

Havelock was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,433. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.036°N, 73.760°W.

Population

In 1871, Havelock had a population of 1,433: 748 male and 685 female residents. Population density was 40.3 people per square mile.
