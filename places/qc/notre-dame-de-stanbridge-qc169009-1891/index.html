Notre Dame de Stanbridge, Quebec (1891 census)

Notre Dame de Stanbridge was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,245. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q285257. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.183°N, 73.025°W.
