Franklin, Quebec (1891 census)

Franklin was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,133. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3082928. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.034°N, 73.923°W.
