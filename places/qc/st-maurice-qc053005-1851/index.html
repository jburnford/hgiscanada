St. Maurice, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Maurice was a census subdivision in Champlain County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,648. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112912588. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.478°N, 72.609°W.

Population

In 1851, St. Maurice had a population of 1,648: 890 male and 758 female residents. Population density was 14.0 people per square mile.
