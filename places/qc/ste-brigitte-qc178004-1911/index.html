Ste. Brigitte, Quebec (1911 census)

Ste. Brigitte was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 655. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.057°N, 71.218°W.

Population

In 1911, Ste. Brigitte had a population of 655: 347 male and 308 female residents. Population density was 157.4 people per square mile.
