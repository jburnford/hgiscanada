Montminy, Rolette & Panet, Quebec (1901 census)

Montminy, Rolette & Panet was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 1,612. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.678°N, 70.168°W.
