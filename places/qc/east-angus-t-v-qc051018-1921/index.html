East Angus, T-V, Quebec (1921 census)

East Angus, T-V was a town in Quebec, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 3,802. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.482°N, 71.661°W.

Population

In 1921, East Angus, T-V had a population of 3,802: 2,042 male and 1,760 female residents. Population density was 1141.8 people per square mile.
