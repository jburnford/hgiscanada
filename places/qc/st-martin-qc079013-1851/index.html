St. Martin, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Martin was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 3,764. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.554°N, 73.769°W.

Population

In 1851, St. Martin had a population of 3,764: 1,900 male and 1,864 female residents.
