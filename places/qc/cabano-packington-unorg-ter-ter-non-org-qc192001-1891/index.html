Cabano, Packington, & unorg. ter.—Ter. non-org., Quebec (1891 census)

Cabano, Packington, & unorg. ter.—Ter. non-org. was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 330. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.450°N, 68.930°W.

Population

In 1891, Cabano, Packington, & unorg. ter.—Ter. non-org. had a population of 330: 170 male and 160 female residents.
