St. Martin, Quebec (1871 census)

St. Martin was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 2,747. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.563°N, 73.744°W.

Population

In 1871, St. Martin had a population of 2,747: 1,373 male and 1,374 female residents. Population density was 118.0 people per square mile.
