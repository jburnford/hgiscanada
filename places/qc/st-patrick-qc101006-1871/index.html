St. Patrick, Quebec (1871 census)

St. Patrick was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,573. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.060°N, 73.749°W.

Population

In 1871, St. Patrick had a population of 1,573: 805 male and 768 female residents.
