St. François, Quebec (1911 census)

St. François was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,278. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.058°N, 72.855°W.

Population

In 1911, St. François had a population of 2,278: 1,107 male and 1,171 female residents. Population density was 113.0 people per square mile.
