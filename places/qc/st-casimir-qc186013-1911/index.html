St. Casimir, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Casimir was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,640. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.669°N, 72.152°W.

Population

In 1911, St. Casimir had a population of 2,640: 1,297 male and 1,343 female residents. Population density was 100.6 people per square mile.
