Berthier, Quebec (1891 census)

Berthier was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,827. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3464281. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.089°N, 73.215°W.
