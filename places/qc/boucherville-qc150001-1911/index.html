Boucherville, Quebec (1911 census)

Boucherville was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 969. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.592°N, 73.420°W.

Population

In 1911, Boucherville had a population of 969: 496 male and 473 female residents. Population density was 34.7 people per square mile.
