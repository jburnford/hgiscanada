St. Nazaire (part), Quebec (1901 census)

St. Nazaire (part) was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 708. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.740°N, 72.635°W.

Population

In 1901, St. Nazaire (part) had a population of 708: 372 male and 336 female residents. Population density was 43.6 people per square mile.
