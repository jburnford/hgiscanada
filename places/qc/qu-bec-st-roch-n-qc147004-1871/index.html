Québec, St. Roch N, Quebec (1871 census)

Québec, St. Roch N was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 2,853. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.831°N, 71.236°W.

Population

In 1871, Québec, St. Roch N had a population of 2,853: 1,422 male and 1,431 female residents. Population density was 817.2 people per square mile.
