Québec, Jacques-Cartier, Ward—Quartier, Quebec (1861 census)

Québec, Jacques-Cartier, Ward—Quartier was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 9,072. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.813°N, 71.224°W.

Population

In 1861, Québec, Jacques-Cartier, Ward—Quartier had a population of 9,072: 4,532 male and 4,540 female residents.
