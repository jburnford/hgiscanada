St. Joseph de Lévis, Quebec (1891 census)

St. Joseph de Lévis was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 351. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.774°N, 71.080°W.
