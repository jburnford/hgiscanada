St. Barthélemy, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Barthélemy was a census subdivision in Berthier County, Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,291. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3461850. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.215°N, 73.166°W.
