Coaticook, Town—Ville, Quebec (1891 census)

Coaticook, Town—Ville was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 3,086. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.136°N, 71.810°W.

Population

In 1891, Coaticook, Town—Ville had a population of 3,086: 1,521 male and 1,565 female residents.
