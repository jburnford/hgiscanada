Hereford, Quebec (1911 census)

Hereford was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,779. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q63243263. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.098°N, 71.515°W.
