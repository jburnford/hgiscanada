Ste. Anne, Quebec (1911 census)

Ste. Anne was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,381. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.062°N, 70.957°W.

Population

In 1911, Ste. Anne had a population of 2,381: 1,224 male and 1,157 female residents. Population density was 28.1 people per square mile.
