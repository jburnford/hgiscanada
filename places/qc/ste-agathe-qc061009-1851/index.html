Ste. Agathe, Quebec (1851 census)

Ste. Agathe was a township in Lotbinière County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 522. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.392°N, 71.415°W.

Population

In 1851, Ste. Agathe had a population of 522: 264 male and 258 female residents.
