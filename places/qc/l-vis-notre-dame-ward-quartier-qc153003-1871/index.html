Lévis, Notre Dame Ward—Quartier, Quebec (1871 census)

Lévis, Notre Dame Ward—Quartier was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 3,322. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.805°N, 71.181°W.
