Bristol, Quebec (1871 census)

Bristol was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 2,162. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.564°N, 76.369°W.

Population

In 1871, Bristol had a population of 2,162: 1,107 male and 1,055 female residents. Population density was 26.2 people per square mile.
