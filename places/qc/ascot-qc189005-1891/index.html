Ascot, Quebec (1891 census)

Ascot was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 3,934. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.370°N, 71.861°W.

Population

In 1891, Ascot had a population of 3,934: 2,123 male and 1,811 female residents. Population density was 43.5 people per square mile.
