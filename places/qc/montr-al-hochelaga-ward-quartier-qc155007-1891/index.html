Montréal, Hochelaga, Ward—Quartier, Quebec (1891 census)

Montréal, Hochelaga, Ward—Quartier was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 8,540. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.543°N, 73.555°W.

Population

In 1891, Montréal, Hochelaga, Ward—Quartier had a population of 8,540: 4,157 male and 4,383 female residents.
