Ste. Croix, Quebec (1881 census)

Ste. Croix was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,377. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3464232. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.600°N, 71.686°W.
