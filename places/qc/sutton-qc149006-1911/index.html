Sutton, Quebec (1911 census)

Sutton was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,179. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q142521. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.086°N, 72.587°W.

Population

In 1911, Sutton had a population of 2,179: 1,148 male and 1,031 female residents. Population density was 21.1 people per square mile.
