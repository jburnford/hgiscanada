St. Edouard, Quebec (1891 census)

St. Edouard was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,359. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.523°N, 71.737°W.

In 1891, St. Edouard had a population of 1,359: 676 male and 683 female residents.
