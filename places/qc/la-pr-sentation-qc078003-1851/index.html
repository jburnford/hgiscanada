La Présentation, Quebec (1851 census)

La Présentation was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 1,755. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3212031. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.668°N, 73.062°W.
