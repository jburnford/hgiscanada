St. Ignace, Quebec (1851 census)

St. Ignace was a township in Vaudreuil County, Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1851 Census of Canada with a population of 2,143. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.311°N, 74.194°W.

Population

In 1851, St. Ignace had a population of 2,143: 1,053 male and 1,090 female residents.
