Nelson, Quebec (1861 census)

Nelson was a census subdivision in Megantic County, Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 1,078. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q28162211. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.377°N, 71.555°W.
