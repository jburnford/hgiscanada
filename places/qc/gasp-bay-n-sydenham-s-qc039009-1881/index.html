Gaspé Bay N & Sydenham S, Quebec (1881 census)

Gaspé Bay N & Sydenham S was a census subdivision in Gaspé County, Quebec, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 609. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.925°N, 64.527°W.
