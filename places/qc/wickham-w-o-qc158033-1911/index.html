Wickham W.-O., Quebec (1911 census)

Wickham W.-O. was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,328. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.753°N, 72.480°W.

Population

In 1911, Wickham W.-O. had a population of 1,328: 703 male and 625 female residents. Population density was 25.2 people per square mile.
