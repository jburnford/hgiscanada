St. Cyrille, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Cyrille was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,642. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q112912036. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.021°N, 70.201°W.

Population

In 1911, St. Cyrille had a population of 1,642: 873 male and 769 female residents. Population density was 12.7 people per square mile.
