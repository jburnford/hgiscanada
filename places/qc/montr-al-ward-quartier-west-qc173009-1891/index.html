Montréal, ward-quartier West, Quebec (1891 census)

Montréal, ward-quartier West was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,007. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.502°N, 73.556°W.

In 1891, Montréal, ward-quartier West had a population of 1,007: 515 male and 492 female residents. Population density was 10299.5 people per square mile.
