Ste. Hénédine, Quebec (1881 census)

Ste. Hénédine was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,169. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.552°N, 70.986°W.
