Ste. Geneviève, Quebec (1911 census)

Ste. Geneviève was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,075. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.483°N, 73.847°W.

Population

In 1911, Ste. Geneviève had a population of 1,075: 537 male and 538 female residents. Population density was 73.7 people per square mile.
