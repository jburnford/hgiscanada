St. Simon, Quebec (1871 census)

St. Simon was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,186. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.214°N, 69.016°W.

Population

In 1871, St. Simon had a population of 1,186: 605 male and 581 female residents. Population density was 37.8 people per square mile.
