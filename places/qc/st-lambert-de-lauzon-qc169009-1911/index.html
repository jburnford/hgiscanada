St. Lambert de Lauzon, Quebec (1911 census)

St. Lambert de Lauzon was a township in Quebec (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,308. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.586°N, 71.227°W.
