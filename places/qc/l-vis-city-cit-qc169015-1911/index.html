Lévis, City—Cité, Quebec (1911 census)

Lévis, City—Cité was a city in Quebec, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 7,452. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q139208. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.794°N, 71.183°W.

Population

In 1911, Lévis, City—Cité had a population of 7,452: 3,560 male and 3,892 female residents. Population density was 547.9 people per square mile.
