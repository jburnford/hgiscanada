Montréal, ward-quartier St. Laurent, Quebec (1871 census)

Montréal, ward-quartier St. Laurent was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 13,106. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.509°N, 73.570°W.

Population

In 1871, Montréal, ward-quartier St. Laurent had a population of 13,106: 6,009 male and 7,097 female residents. Population density was 26262.6 people per square mile.
