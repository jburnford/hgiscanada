Montreal, St. Jean-Baptiste, Ward—Quartier, Quebec (1861 census)

Montreal, St. Jean-Baptiste, Ward—Quartier was a census subdivision in Quebec, recorded in the 1861 Census of Canada with a population of 2,269. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.523°N, 73.579°W.

Population

In 1861, Montreal, St. Jean-Baptiste, Ward—Quartier had a population of 2,269: 1,192 male and 1,077 female residents.
