263 townships, Alberta (1911 census)

263 townships was a township in Alberta (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 33,048. The administrative centroid was at approximately 53.926°N, 111.607°W.

Population

In 1911, 263 townships had a population of 33,048: 58 male and 39 female residents. Population density was 2.6 people per square mile.
