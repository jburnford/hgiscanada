N. Edmonton vl (T53 R24 MW4), Alberta (1911 census)

N. Edmonton vl (T53 R24 MW4) was a township in Alberta (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 404. The administrative centroid was at approximately 53.597°N, 113.434°W.
