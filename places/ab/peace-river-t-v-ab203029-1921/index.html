Peace River, T-V, Alberta (1921 census)

Peace River, T-V was a town in Alberta, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 980. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q1743985. The administrative centroid was at approximately 56.241°N, 117.277°W.
