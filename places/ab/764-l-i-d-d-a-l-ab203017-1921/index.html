764. L.I.D.- D.A.L., Alberta (1921 census)

764. L.I.D.- D.A.L. was a census subdivision in Alberta, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 571. The administrative centroid was at approximately 55.501°N, 116.224°W.
