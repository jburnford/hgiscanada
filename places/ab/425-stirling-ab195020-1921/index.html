425. Stirling, Alberta (1921 census)

425. Stirling was a census subdivision in Alberta, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 2,079. The administrative centroid was at approximately 52.799°N, 111.962°W.

Population

In 1921, 425. Stirling had a population of 2,079: 1,193 male and 886 female residents. Population density was 6.4 people per square mile.
