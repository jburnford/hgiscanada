Qu'Appelle, Northwest Territories (1891 census)

Qu'Appelle was a township in Northwest Territories (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 6,806. The administrative centroid was at approximately 51.202°N, 102.849°W.

Population

In 1891, Qu'Appelle had a population of 6,806: 3,791 male and 3,015 female residents.
