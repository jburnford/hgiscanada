McLeod, Northwest Territories (1891 census)

McLeod was a census subdivision in Northwest Territories, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 7,203. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.767°N, 112.968°W.

Population

In 1891, McLeod had a population of 7,203: 4,146 male and 3,057 female residents. Population density was 0.5 people per square mile.
