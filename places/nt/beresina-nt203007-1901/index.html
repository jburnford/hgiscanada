Beresina, Northwest Territories (1901 census)

Beresina was a census subdivision in Northwest Territories, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 593. The administrative centroid was at approximately 51.042°N, 101.737°W.

Population

In 1901, Beresina had a population of 593: 315 male and 278 female residents. Population density was 2.9 people per square mile.
