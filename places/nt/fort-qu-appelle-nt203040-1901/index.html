Fort Qu’Appelle, Northwest Territories (1901 census)

Fort Qu’Appelle was a township in Northwest Territories (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 773. The administrative centroid was at approximately 50.753°N, 103.818°W.
