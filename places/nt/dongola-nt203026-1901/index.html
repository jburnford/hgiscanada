Dongola, Northwest Territories (1901 census)

Dongola was a census subdivision in Northwest Territories, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 328. The administrative centroid was at approximately 50.620°N, 101.863°W.

Population

In 1901, Dongola had a population of 328: 180 male and 148 female residents. Population density was 2.9 people per square mile.
