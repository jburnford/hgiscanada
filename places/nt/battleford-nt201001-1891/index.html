Battleford, Northwest Territories (1891 census)

Battleford was a township in Northwest Territories (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,790. The administrative centroid was at approximately 53.441°N, 109.614°W.

Population

In 1891, Battleford had a population of 2,790: 1,415 male and 1,375 female residents.
