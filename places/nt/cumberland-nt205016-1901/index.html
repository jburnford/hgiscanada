Cumberland, Northwest Territories (1901 census)

Cumberland was a census subdivision in Northwest Territories, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 553. The administrative centroid was at approximately 53.912°N, 102.994°W.

Population

In 1901, Cumberland had a population of 553: 276 male and 277 female residents. Population density was 0.4 people per square mile.
