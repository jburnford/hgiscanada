Medicine Hat, Northwest Territories (1891 census)

Medicine Hat was a township in Northwest Territories (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,316. The administrative centroid was at approximately 50.459°N, 110.539°W.

Population

In 1891, Medicine Hat had a population of 1,316: 782 male and 534 female residents.
