Broadview, Northwest Territories (1891 census)

Broadview was a township in Northwest Territories (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 13,676. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.721°N, 102.709°W.

Population

In 1891, Broadview had a population of 13,676: 7,606 male and 6,070 female residents.
