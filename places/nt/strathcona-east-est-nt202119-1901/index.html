Strathcona, East—Est, Northwest Territories (1901 census)

Strathcona, East—Est was a census subdivision in Northwest Territories, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 642. The administrative centroid was at approximately 53.484°N, 113.543°W.

Population

In 1901, Strathcona, East—Est had a population of 642: 351 male and 291 female residents. Population density was 34.8 people per square mile.
