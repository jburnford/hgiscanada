Moose Jaw & Regina, Northwest Territories (1891 census)

Moose Jaw & Regina was a township in Northwest Territories (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 7,565. The administrative centroid was at approximately 50.490°N, 104.997°W.

Population

In 1891, Moose Jaw & Regina had a population of 7,565: 4,343 male and 3,222 female residents.
