Isaac’s Harbor, Nova Scotia (1871 census)

Isaac’s Harbor was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 928. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.197°N, 61.638°W.
