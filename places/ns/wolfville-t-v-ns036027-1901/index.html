Wolfville, T-V, Nova Scotia (1901 census)

Wolfville, T-V was a town in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 1,412. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q475953. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.089°N, 64.361°W.

Population

In 1901, Wolfville, T-V had a population of 1,412: 634 male and 778 female residents. Population density was 545.9 people per square mile.
