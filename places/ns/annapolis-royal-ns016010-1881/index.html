Annapolis Royal, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Annapolis Royal was a census subdivision in Annapolis County, Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,833. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q564536. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.690°N, 65.427°W.
