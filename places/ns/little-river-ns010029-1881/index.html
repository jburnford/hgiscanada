Little River, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Little River was a census subdivision in Halifax (County-Comté) County, Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 320. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.946°N, 63.249°W.

Population

In 1881, Little River had a population of 320: 167 male and 153 female residents. Population density was 12.6 people per square mile.
