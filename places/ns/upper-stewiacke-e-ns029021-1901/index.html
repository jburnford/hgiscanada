Upper Stewiacke E., Nova Scotia (1901 census)

Upper Stewiacke E. was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 714. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.283°N, 62.831°W.
