Bay St. Lawrence, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Bay St. Lawrence was a census subdivision in Victoria County, Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 515. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.031°N, 60.417°W.

Population

In 1881, Bay St. Lawrence had a population of 515: 276 male and 239 female residents. Population density was 26.7 people per square mile.
