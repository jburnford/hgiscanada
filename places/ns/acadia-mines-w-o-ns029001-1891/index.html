Acadia Mines W-O, Nova Scotia (1891 census)

Acadia Mines W-O was a township in Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,451. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.456°N, 63.671°W.

Population

In 1891, Acadia Mines W-O had a population of 1,451: 750 male and 701 female residents.
