Lower Londonderry, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Lower Londonderry was a township in Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,571. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.452°N, 63.744°W.

Population

In 1881, Lower Londonderry had a population of 1,571: 806 male and 765 female residents.
