East Bay S, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

East Bay S was a township in Cape Breton County, Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 665. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.940°N, 60.408°W.

Population

In 1881, East Bay S had a population of 665: 354 male and 311 female residents.
