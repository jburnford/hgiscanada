Gaspereau, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Gaspereau was a census subdivision in Kings County, Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,217. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.003°N, 64.375°W.

Population

In 1881, Gaspereau had a population of 1,217: 636 male and 581 female residents. Population density was 18.0 people per square mile.
