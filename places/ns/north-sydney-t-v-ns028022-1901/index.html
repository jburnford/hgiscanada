North Sydney, T-V, Nova Scotia (1901 census)

North Sydney, T-V was a town in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 4,646. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.198°N, 60.289°W.

Population

In 1901, North Sydney, T-V had a population of 4,646: 2,404 male and 2,242 female residents. Population density was 628.9 people per square mile.
