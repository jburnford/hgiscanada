Port Hood, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Port Hood was a census subdivision in Inverness County, Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,498. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.028°N, 61.476°W.

Population

In 1881, Port Hood had a population of 1,498: 759 male and 739 female residents. Population density was 27.8 people per square mile.
