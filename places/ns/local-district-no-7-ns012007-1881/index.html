Local District No. 7, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Local District No. 7 was a township in Queen's County, Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 665. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.078°N, 64.613°W.
