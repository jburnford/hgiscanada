Dartmouth, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Dartmouth was a census subdivision in Halifax (County-Comté) County, Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,777. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.718°N, 63.542°W.

Population

In 1881, Dartmouth had a population of 1,777: 893 male and 884 female residents.
