Petite Rivière, Nova Scotia (1891 census)

Petite Rivière was a township in Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 3,415. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.237°N, 64.509°W.

Population

In 1891, Petite Rivière had a population of 3,415: 1,734 male and 1,681 female residents.
