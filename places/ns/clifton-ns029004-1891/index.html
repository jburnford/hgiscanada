Clifton, Nova Scotia (1891 census)

Clifton was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,017. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q5133140. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.296°N, 63.412°W.
