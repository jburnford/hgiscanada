Aylesford N, Nova Scotia (1891 census)

Aylesford N was a township in Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,637. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.066°N, 64.894°W.

Population

In 1891, Aylesford N had a population of 1,637: 849 male and 788 female residents.
