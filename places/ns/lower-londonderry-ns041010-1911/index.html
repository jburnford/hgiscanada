Lower Londonderry, Nova Scotia (1911 census)

Lower Londonderry was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 844. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.453°N, 63.733°W.

Population

In 1911, Lower Londonderry had a population of 844: 450 male and 394 female residents. Population density was 9.8 people per square mile.
