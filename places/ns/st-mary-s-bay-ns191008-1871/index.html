St. Mary’s Bay, Nova Scotia (1871 census)

St. Mary’s Bay was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,709. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.487°N, 65.841°W.

Population

In 1871, St. Mary’s Bay had a population of 1,709: 877 male and 832 female residents. Population density was 21.5 people per square mile.
