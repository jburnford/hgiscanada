North River, Nova Scotia (1911 census)

North River was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,069. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.468°N, 63.203°W.

Population

In 1911, North River had a population of 1,069: 550 male and 519 female residents. Population density was 19.1 people per square mile.
