St. Croix, Nova Scotia (1891 census)

St. Croix was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,483. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7587666. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.942°N, 63.944°W.

Population

In 1891, St. Croix had a population of 1,483: 751 male and 732 female residents.
