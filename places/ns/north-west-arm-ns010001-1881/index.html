North West Arm, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

North West Arm was a census subdivision in Halifax (County-Comté) County, Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 460. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.637°N, 63.633°W.
