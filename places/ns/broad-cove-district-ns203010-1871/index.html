Broad Cove District, Nova Scotia (1871 census)

Broad Cove District was a township in Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 986. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.295°N, 61.210°W.
