Inverness, T-V, Nova Scotia (1911 census)

Inverness, T-V was a township in Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,719. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q749228. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.233°N, 61.308°W.
