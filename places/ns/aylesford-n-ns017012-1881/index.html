Aylesford N, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Aylesford N was a township in Kings County, Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,594. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.066°N, 64.894°W.
