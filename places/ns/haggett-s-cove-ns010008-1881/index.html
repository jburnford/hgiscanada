Haggett's Cove, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Haggett's Cove was a census subdivision in Halifax (County-Comté) County, Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,176. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.556°N, 63.872°W.

Population

In 1881, Haggett's Cove had a population of 1,176: 627 male and 549 female residents.
