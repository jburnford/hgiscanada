Rawdon, Nova Scotia (1891 census)

Rawdon was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 855. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.077°N, 63.709°W.

Population

In 1891, Rawdon had a population of 855: 400 male and 455 female residents. Population density was 11.9 people per square mile.
