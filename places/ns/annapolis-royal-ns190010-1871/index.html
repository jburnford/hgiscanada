Annapolis Royal, Nova Scotia (1871 census)

Annapolis Royal was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 2,127. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q564536. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.746°N, 65.430°W.

Population

In 1871, Annapolis Royal had a population of 2,127: 1,039 male and 1,088 female residents. Population density was 41.3 people per square mile.
