Guysborough, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Guysborough was a township in Guysborough County, Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,703. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3122367. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.355°N, 61.556°W.

Population

In 1881, Guysborough had a population of 1,703: 847 male and 856 female residents.
