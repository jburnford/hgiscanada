Lower Onslow, Nova Scotia (1911 census)

Lower Onslow was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 941. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.448°N, 63.345°W.

Population

In 1911, Lower Onslow had a population of 941: 515 male and 426 female residents. Population density was 13.8 people per square mile.
