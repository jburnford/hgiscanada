Cariboo E & W-O, Nova Scotia (1891 census)

Cariboo E & W-O was a township in Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,095. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.741°N, 62.804°W.

Population

In 1891, Cariboo E & W-O had a population of 1,095: 576 male and 519 female residents.
