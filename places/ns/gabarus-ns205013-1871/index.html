Gabarus, Nova Scotia (1871 census)

Gabarus was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,747. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.824°N, 60.286°W.

Population

In 1871, Gabarus had a population of 1,747: 857 male and 890 female residents. Population density was 11.4 people per square mile.
