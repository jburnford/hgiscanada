Truro, Town—Ville, Nova Scotia (1911 census)

Truro, Town—Ville was a town in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 6,107. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q283064. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.378°N, 63.267°W.

Population

In 1911, Truro, Town—Ville had a population of 6,107: 2,785 male and 3,322 female residents. Population density was 1951.1 people per square mile.
