Hantsport, Nova Scotia (1891 census)

Hantsport was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 916. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q660265. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.052°N, 64.183°W.

Population

In 1891, Hantsport had a population of 916: 470 male and 446 female residents. Population density was 913.2 people per square mile.
