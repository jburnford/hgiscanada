Ballantyne’s Cove, Nova Scotia (1891 census)

Ballantyne’s Cove was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,063. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.837°N, 61.969°W.

Population

In 1891, Ballantyne’s Cove had a population of 1,063: 526 male and 537 female residents.
