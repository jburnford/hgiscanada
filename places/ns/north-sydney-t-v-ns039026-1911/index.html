North Sydney, T-V, Nova Scotia (1911 census)

North Sydney, T-V was a town in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 5,418. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q641876. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.198°N, 60.289°W.

Population

In 1911, North Sydney, T-V had a population of 5,418: 2,688 male and 2,730 female residents. Population density was 2167.2 people per square mile.
