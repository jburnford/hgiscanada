Morristown, Nova Scotia (1921 census)

Morristown was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 545. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.740°N, 61.967°W.

In 1921, Morristown had a population of 545: 299 male and 246 female residents. Population density was 11.0 people per square mile.
