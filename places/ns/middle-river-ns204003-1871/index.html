Middle River, Nova Scotia (1871 census)

Middle River was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,006. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.156°N, 60.912°W.

Population

In 1871, Middle River had a population of 1,006: 514 male and 492 female residents. Population density was 18.7 people per square mile.
