Judique, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Judique was a census subdivision in Inverness County, Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,027. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.893°N, 61.448°W.

Population

In 1881, Judique had a population of 2,027: 995 male and 1,032 female residents. Population density was 25.4 people per square mile.
