Springhill, T-V, Nova Scotia (1911 census)

Springhill, T-V was a town in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 5,713. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q1000316. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.643°N, 64.062°W.

Population

In 1911, Springhill, T-V had a population of 5,713: 2,984 male and 2,729 female residents. Population density was 1428.2 people per square mile.
