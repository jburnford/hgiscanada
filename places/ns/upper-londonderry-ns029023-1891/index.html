Upper Londonderry, Nova Scotia (1891 census)

Upper Londonderry was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,150. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.471°N, 63.476°W.

Population

In 1891, Upper Londonderry had a population of 1,150: 591 male and 559 female residents.
