Middle Londonderry, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Middle Londonderry was a township in Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 3,173. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.466°N, 63.605°W.

Population

In 1881, Middle Londonderry had a population of 3,173: 1,645 male and 1,528 female residents.
