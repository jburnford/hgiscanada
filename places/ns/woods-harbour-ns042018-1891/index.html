Woods Harbour, Nova Scotia (1891 census)

Woods Harbour was a township in Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,134. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.570°N, 65.707°W.

Population

In 1891, Woods Harbour had a population of 1,134: 584 male and 550 female residents.
