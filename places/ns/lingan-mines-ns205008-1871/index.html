Lingan Mines, Nova Scotia (1871 census)

Lingan Mines was a township in Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 3,429. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.175°N, 60.046°W.

Population

In 1871, Lingan Mines had a population of 3,429: 1,771 male and 1,658 female residents.
