West Bay, Nova Scotia (1921 census)

West Bay was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 637. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.767°N, 61.164°W.

Population

In 1921, West Bay had a population of 637: 340 male and 297 female residents. Population density was 22.7 people per square mile.
