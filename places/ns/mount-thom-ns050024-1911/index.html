Mount Thom, Nova Scotia (1911 census)

Mount Thom was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 635. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.521°N, 62.967°W.

Population

In 1911, Mount Thom had a population of 635: 308 male and 327 female residents. Population density was 13.1 people per square mile.
