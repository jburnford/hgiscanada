Broad Cove Intervale, Nova Scotia (1871 census)

Broad Cove Intervale was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,442. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.216°N, 61.238°W.

Population

In 1871, Broad Cove Intervale had a population of 1,442: 725 male and 717 female residents. Population density was 25.6 people per square mile.
