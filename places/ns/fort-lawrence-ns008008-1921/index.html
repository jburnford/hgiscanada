Fort Lawrence, Nova Scotia (1921 census)

Fort Lawrence was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 155. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.884°N, 64.194°W.

Population

In 1921, Fort Lawrence had a population of 155: 76 male and 79 female residents. Population density was 4.2 people per square mile.
