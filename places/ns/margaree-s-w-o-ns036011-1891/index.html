Margaree S. W.-O., Nova Scotia (1891 census)

Margaree S. W.-O. was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,067. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.271°N, 61.085°W.
