Rawdon & Uniacke, Nova Scotia (1911 census)

Rawdon & Uniacke was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 516. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.968°N, 63.765°W.
