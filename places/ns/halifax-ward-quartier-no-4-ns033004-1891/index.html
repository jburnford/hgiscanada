Halifax, Ward—Quartier No. 4, Nova Scotia (1891 census)

Halifax, Ward—Quartier No. 4 was a township in Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,715. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.647°N, 63.599°W.
