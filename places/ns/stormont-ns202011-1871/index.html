Stormont, Nova Scotia (1871 census)

Stormont was a township in Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 369. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.260°N, 61.826°W.

In 1871, Stormont had a population of 369: 189 male and 180 female residents.
