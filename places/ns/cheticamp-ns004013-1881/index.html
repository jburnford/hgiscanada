Cheticamp, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Cheticamp was a township in Inverness County, Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,726. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.631°N, 60.933°W.

Population

In 1881, Cheticamp had a population of 2,726: 1,376 male and 1,350 female residents.
