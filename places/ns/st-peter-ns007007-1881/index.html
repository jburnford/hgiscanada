St. Peter, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

St. Peter was a census subdivision in Richmond County, Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,299. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.696°N, 60.845°W.

Population

In 1881, St. Peter had a population of 1,299: 659 male and 640 female residents. Population density was 30.5 people per square mile.
