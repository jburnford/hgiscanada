Parrsboro, T-V, Nova Scotia (1911 census)

Parrsboro, T-V was a town in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,856. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q499355. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.469°N, 64.302°W.

Population

In 1911, Parrsboro, T-V had a population of 2,856: 1,413 male and 1,443 female residents. Population density was 317.3 people per square mile.
