Glace Bay, Nova Scotia (1891 census)

Glace Bay was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 2,459. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.173°N, 59.921°W.

Population

In 1891, Glace Bay had a population of 2,459: 1,219 male and 1,240 female residents.
