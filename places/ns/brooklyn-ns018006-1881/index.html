Brooklyn, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Brooklyn was a census subdivision in Hants County, Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,106. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.996°N, 63.962°W.

Population

In 1881, Brooklyn had a population of 1,106: 578 male and 528 female residents. Population density was 34.7 people per square mile.
