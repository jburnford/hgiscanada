Rockville, Nova Scotia (1901 census)

Rockville was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 693. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.816°N, 66.091°W.

Population

In 1901, Rockville had a population of 693: 329 male and 364 female residents. Population density was 27.7 people per square mile.
