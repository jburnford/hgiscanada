Shubenacadie, Nova Scotia (1901 census)

Shubenacadie was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 1,463. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.130°N, 63.521°W.

Population

In 1901, Shubenacadie had a population of 1,463: 762 male and 701 female residents. Population density was 25.1 people per square mile.
