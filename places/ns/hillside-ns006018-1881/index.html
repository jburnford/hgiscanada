Hillside, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Hillside was a census subdivision in Cape Breton County, Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,030. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.024°N, 60.151°W.

Population

In 1881, Hillside had a population of 1,030: 506 male and 524 female residents.
