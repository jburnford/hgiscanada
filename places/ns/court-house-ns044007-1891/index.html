Court House, Nova Scotia (1891 census)

Court House was a township in Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,175. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.829°N, 66.119°W.

Population

In 1891, Court House had a population of 1,175: 566 male and 609 female residents.
