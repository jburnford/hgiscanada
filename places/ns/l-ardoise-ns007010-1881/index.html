L'Ardoise, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

L'Ardoise was a township in Richmond County, Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,966. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.634°N, 60.739°W.

Population

In 1881, L'Ardoise had a population of 1,966: 994 male and 972 female residents.
