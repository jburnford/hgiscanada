Walton, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Walton was a census subdivision in Hants County, Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 911. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.214°N, 63.901°W.

Population

In 1881, Walton had a population of 911: 483 male and 428 female residents. Population density was 14.8 people per square mile.
