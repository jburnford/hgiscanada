Digby, Nova Scotia (1911 census)

Digby was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,247. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q1224586. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.657°N, 65.776°W.

Population

In 1911, Digby had a population of 1,247: 555 male and 692 female residents. Population density was 623.5 people per square mile.
