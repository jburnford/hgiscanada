Stellarton, T-V, Nova Scotia (1911 census)

Stellarton, T-V was a town in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,910. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3498168. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.550°N, 62.636°W.
