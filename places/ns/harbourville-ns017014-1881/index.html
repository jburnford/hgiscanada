Harbourville, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Harbourville was a census subdivision in Kings County, Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,445. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.126°N, 64.785°W.

Population

In 1881, Harbourville had a population of 1,445: 753 male and 692 female residents. Population density was 44.5 people per square mile.
