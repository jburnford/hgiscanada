Nictaux, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Nictaux was a township in Annapolis County, Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,516. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.881°N, 65.131°W.
