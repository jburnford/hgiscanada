Broad Cove Marsh, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Broad Cove Marsh was a census subdivision in Inverness County, Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,075. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.295°N, 61.210°W.

Population

In 1881, Broad Cove Marsh had a population of 1,075: 510 male and 565 female residents. Population density was 44.3 people per square mile.
