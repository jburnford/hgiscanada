Barrington, Nova Scotia (1911 census)

Barrington was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,244. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.579°N, 65.585°W.

Population

In 1911, Barrington had a population of 1,244: 579 male and 665 female residents. Population density was 34.0 people per square mile.
