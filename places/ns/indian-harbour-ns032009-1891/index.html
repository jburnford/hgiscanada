Indian Harbour, Nova Scotia (1891 census)

Indian Harbour was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 707. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.148°N, 61.858°W.

Population

In 1891, Indian Harbour had a population of 707: 365 male and 342 female residents. Population density was 11.0 people per square mile.
