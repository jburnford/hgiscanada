Berwick, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Berwick was a census subdivision in Kings County, Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,698. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.031°N, 64.663°W.

Population

In 1881, Berwick had a population of 1,698: 855 male and 843 female residents.
