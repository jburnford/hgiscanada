Doherty Creek, Nova Scotia (1911 census)

Doherty Creek was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 540. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.753°N, 63.732°W.

Population

In 1911, Doherty Creek had a population of 540: 282 male and 258 female residents. Population density was 11.2 people per square mile.
