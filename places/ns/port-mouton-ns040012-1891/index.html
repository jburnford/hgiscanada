Port Mouton, Nova Scotia (1891 census)

Port Mouton was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 758. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.884°N, 64.886°W.

Population

In 1891, Port Mouton had a population of 758: 399 male and 359 female residents. Population density was 11.4 people per square mile.
