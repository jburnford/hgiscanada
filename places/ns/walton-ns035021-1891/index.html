Walton, Nova Scotia (1891 census)

Walton was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 687. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.214°N, 63.901°W.

Population

In 1891, Walton had a population of 687: 356 male and 331 female residents.
