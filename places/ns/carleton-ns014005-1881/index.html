Carleton, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Carleton was a township in Yarmouth County, Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 724. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.036°N, 65.920°W.

Population

In 1881, Carleton had a population of 724: 383 male and 341 female residents.
