New Tusket, Nova Scotia (1911 census)

New Tusket was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 621. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.245°N, 65.872°W.

Population

In 1911, New Tusket had a population of 621: 309 male and 312 female residents. Population density was 5.2 people per square mile.
