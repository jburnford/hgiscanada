Red Islands, Nova Scotia (1871 census)

Red Islands was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 776. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.756°N, 60.696°W.

Population

In 1871, Red Islands had a population of 776: 375 male and 401 female residents. Population density was 18.5 people per square mile.
