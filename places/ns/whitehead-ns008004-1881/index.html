Whitehead, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Whitehead was a census subdivision in Guysborough County, Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 867. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q7996025. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.280°N, 61.197°W.
