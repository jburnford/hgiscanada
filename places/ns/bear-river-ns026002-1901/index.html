Bear River, Nova Scotia (1901 census)

Bear River was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 662. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.592°N, 65.601°W.

Population

In 1901, Bear River had a population of 662: 340 male and 322 female residents. Population density was 34.6 people per square mile.
