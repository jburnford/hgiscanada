Waterloo, Nova Scotia (1911 census)

Waterloo was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 769. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.319°N, 64.662°W.

Population

In 1911, Waterloo had a population of 769: 414 male and 355 female residents. Population density was 13.7 people per square mile.
