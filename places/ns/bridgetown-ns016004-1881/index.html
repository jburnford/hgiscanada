Bridgetown, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Bridgetown was a census subdivision in Annapolis County, Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,448. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q913891. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.878°N, 65.314°W.

Population

In 1881, Bridgetown had a population of 1,448: 723 male and 725 female residents.
