Port La Tour, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Port La Tour was a township in Shelburne County, Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,798. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.600°N, 65.508°W.
