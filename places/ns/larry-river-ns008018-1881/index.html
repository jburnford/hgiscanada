Larry River, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Larry River was a township in Guysborough County, Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 738. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.259°N, 61.403°W.

Population

In 1881, Larry River had a population of 738: 366 male and 372 female residents.
