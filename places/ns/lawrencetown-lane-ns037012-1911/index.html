Lawrencetown Lane, Nova Scotia (1911 census)

Lawrencetown Lane was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 615. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.870°N, 65.161°W.
