Sambro, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Sambro was a census subdivision in Halifax (County-Comté) County, Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 494. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.490°N, 63.613°W.

In 1881, Sambro had a population of 494: 259 male and 235 female residents.
