Aylesford S, Nova Scotia (1871 census)

Aylesford S was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 1,571. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.983°N, 64.856°W.

Population

In 1871, Aylesford S had a population of 1,571: 790 male and 781 female residents. Population density was 20.6 people per square mile.
