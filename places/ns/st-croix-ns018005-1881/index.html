St. Croix, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

St. Croix was a township in Hants County, Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,555. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.942°N, 63.944°W.

Population

In 1881, St. Croix had a population of 1,555: 783 male and 772 female residents.
