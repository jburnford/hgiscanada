Bay St. Lawrence, Nova Scotia (1871 census)

Bay St. Lawrence was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 419. The administrative centroid was at approximately 47.031°N, 60.417°W.

Population

In 1871, Bay St. Lawrence had a population of 419: 218 male and 201 female residents. Population density was 21.7 people per square mile.
