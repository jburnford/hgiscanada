Eastern Passage, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Eastern Passage was a census subdivision in Halifax (County-Comté) County, Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 892. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.617°N, 63.477°W.
