Centreville, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

Centreville was a township in Kings County, Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 2,391. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.152°N, 64.544°W.

Population

In 1881, Centreville had a population of 2,391: 1,242 male and 1,149 female residents.
