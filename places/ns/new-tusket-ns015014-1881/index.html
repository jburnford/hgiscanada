New Tusket, Nova Scotia (1881 census)

New Tusket was a township in Digby County, Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 537. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.245°N, 65.872°W.
