Whycocomagh, Nova Scotia (1891 census)

Whycocomagh was a township in Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,509. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.978°N, 61.158°W.

Population

In 1891, Whycocomagh had a population of 1,509: 791 male and 718 female residents.
