Lower Londonderry, Nova Scotia (1891 census)

Lower Londonderry was a township in Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 953. The administrative centroid was at approximately 45.452°N, 63.744°W.

Population

In 1891, Lower Londonderry had a population of 953: 506 male and 447 female residents.
