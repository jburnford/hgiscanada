Centreville, Nova Scotia (1911 census)

Centreville was a township in Nova Scotia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,140. The administrative centroid was at approximately 43.506°N, 65.603°W.

Population

In 1911, Centreville had a population of 1,140: 583 male and 557 female residents.
