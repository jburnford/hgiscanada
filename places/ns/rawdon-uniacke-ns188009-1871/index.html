Rawdon & Uniacke, Nova Scotia (1871 census)

Rawdon & Uniacke was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1871 Census of Canada with a population of 719. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.968°N, 63.765°W.

Population

In 1871, Rawdon & Uniacke had a population of 719: 387 male and 332 female residents. Population density was 4.8 people per square mile.
