Milton, Nova Scotia (1911 census)

Milton was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,291. The administrative centroid was at approximately 44.100°N, 64.815°W.

Population

In 1911, Milton had a population of 1,291: 645 male and 646 female residents. Population density was 15.0 people per square mile.
