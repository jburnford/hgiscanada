Margaree N. E., Nova Scotia (1891 census)

Margaree N. E. was a census subdivision in Nova Scotia, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,461. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.397°N, 60.955°W.

Population

In 1891, Margaree N. E. had a population of 1,461: 747 male and 714 female residents.
