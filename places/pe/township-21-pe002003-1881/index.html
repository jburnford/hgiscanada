Township 21, Prince Edward Island (1881 census)

Township 21 was a census subdivision in Queen's County, Prince Edward Island, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,871. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.443°N, 63.481°W.

Population

In 1881, Township 21 had a population of 1,871: 944 male and 927 female residents. Population density was 55.8 people per square mile.
