Township 26, Prince Edward Island (1891 census)

Township 26 was a township in Prince Edward Island (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,278. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.340°N, 63.689°W.

Population

In 1891, Township 26 had a population of 1,278: 653 male and 625 female residents.
