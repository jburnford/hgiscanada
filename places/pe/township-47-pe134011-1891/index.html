Township 47, Prince Edward Island (1891 census)

Township 47 was a township in Prince Edward Island (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,139. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.437°N, 62.082°W.

Population

In 1891, Township 47 had a population of 1,139: 566 male and 573 female residents.
