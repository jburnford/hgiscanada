Charlottetown & Royalty, C, Prince Edward Island (1911 census)

Charlottetown & Royalty, C was a township in Prince Edward Island (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 11,203. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.243°N, 63.130°W.

Population

In 1911, Charlottetown & Royalty, C had a population of 11,203: 5,145 male and 6,058 female residents. Population density was 1024.0 people per square mile.
