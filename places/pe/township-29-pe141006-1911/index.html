Township 29, Prince Edward Island (1911 census)

Township 29 was a census subdivision in Prince Edward Island, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,500. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.241°N, 63.455°W.

Population

In 1911, Township 29 had a population of 1,500: 754 male and 746 female residents. Population density was 41.6 people per square mile.
