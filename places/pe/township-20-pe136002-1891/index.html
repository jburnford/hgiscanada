Township 20, Prince Edward Island (1891 census)

Township 20 was a census subdivision in Prince Edward Island, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,325. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3259882. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.453°N, 63.549°W.
