Township 65, Prince Edward Island (1881 census)

Township 65 was a census subdivision in Queen's County, Prince Edward Island, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,865. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.170°N, 63.238°W.

Population

In 1881, Township 65 had a population of 1,865: 990 male and 875 female residents. Population density was 65.2 people per square mile.
