Township 59, Prince Edward Island (1911 census)

Township 59 was a census subdivision in Prince Edward Island, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,334. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q3259939. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.138°N, 62.633°W.
