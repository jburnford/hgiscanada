Township 54, Prince Edward Island (1911 census)

Township 54 was a township in Prince Edward Island (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 762. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.281°N, 62.551°W.

Population

In 1911, Township 54 had a population of 762: 413 male and 349 female residents. Population density was 24.9 people per square mile.
