Township 27, Prince Edward Island (1881 census)

Township 27 was a census subdivision in Prince County, Prince Edward Island, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,348. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.298°N, 63.629°W.

Population

In 1881, Township 27 had a population of 1,348: 654 male and 694 female residents. Population density was 38.8 people per square mile.
