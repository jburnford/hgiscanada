Township 24, Prince Edward Island (1911 census)

Township 24 was a township in Prince Edward Island (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,192. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.404°N, 63.283°W.

Population

In 1911, Township 24 had a population of 2,192: 1,094 male and 1,098 female residents. Population density was 57.8 people per square mile.
