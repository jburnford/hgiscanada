Township 51, Prince Edward Island (1911 census)

Township 51 was a township in Prince Edward Island (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 995. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.221°N, 62.722°W.

Population

In 1911, Township 51 had a population of 995: 514 male and 481 female residents. Population density was 35.3 people per square mile.
