Township 63, Prince Edward Island (1881 census)

Township 63 was a census subdivision in King's County, Prince Edward Island, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,243. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.054°N, 62.581°W.

Population

In 1881, Township 63 had a population of 1,243: 648 male and 595 female residents. Population density was 30.8 people per square mile.
