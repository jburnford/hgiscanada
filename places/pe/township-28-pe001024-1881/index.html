Township 28, Prince Edward Island (1881 census)

Township 28 was a township in Prince County, Prince Edward Island (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,692. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.244°N, 63.583°W.
