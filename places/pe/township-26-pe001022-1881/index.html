Township 26, Prince Edward Island (1881 census)

Township 26 was a census subdivision in Prince County, Prince Edward Island, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,346. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.340°N, 63.689°W.

Population

In 1881, Township 26 had a population of 1,346: 663 male and 683 female residents. Population density was 33.5 people per square mile.
