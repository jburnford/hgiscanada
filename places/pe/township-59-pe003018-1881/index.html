Township 59, Prince Edward Island (1881 census)

Township 59 was a township in King's County, Prince Edward Island (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,782. The administrative centroid was at approximately 46.138°N, 62.633°W.

Population

In 1881, Township 59 had a population of 1,782: 917 male and 865 female residents.
