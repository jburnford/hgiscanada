Richmond pt, British Columbia (1911 census)

Richmond pt was a township in British Columbia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,036. The administrative centroid was at approximately 50.019°N, 123.488°W.

Population

In 1911, Richmond pt had a population of 2,036: 1,583 male and 453 female residents. Population density was 0.7 people per square mile.
