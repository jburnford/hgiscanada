Goldstream and Sooke, British Columbia (1891 census)

Goldstream and Sooke was a census subdivision in British Columbia, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 3,270. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.411°N, 123.647°W.

Population

In 1891, Goldstream and Sooke had a population of 3,270: 1,911 male and 1,359 female residents.
