Richmond (pt), British Columbia (1911 census)

Richmond (pt) was a census subdivision in British Columbia, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 7,248. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q236837. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.200°N, 123.040°W.

Population

In 1911, Richmond (pt) had a population of 7,248: 4,837 male and 2,411 female residents. Population density was 83.4 people per square mile.
