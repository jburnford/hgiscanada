Point Grey, British Columbia (1921 census)

Point Grey was a census subdivision in British Columbia, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 13,736. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.243°N, 123.178°W.

Population

In 1921, Point Grey had a population of 13,736: 7,018 male and 6,718 female residents. Population density was 699.9 people per square mile.
