Gabriola Island, British Columbia (1891 census)

Gabriola Island was a census subdivision in British Columbia, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 125. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.160°N, 123.789°W.

Population

In 1891, Gabriola Island had a population of 125: 84 male and 41 female residents.
