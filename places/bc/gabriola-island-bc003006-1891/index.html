Gabriola Island, British Columbia (1891 census)

Gabriola Island was a township in British Columbia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 125. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.160°N, 123.789°W.

Population

In 1891, Gabriola Island had a population of 125: 84 male and 41 female residents.

Boundary continuity (non-identical overlaps)

Spatial polygon overlaps with adjacent census years where the boundary shifted enough that the SAME_AS chain didn't merge them. These show where the territory came from and went to even when it isn't tracked as the same persistent place.

Earlier boundary forms

  • In an earlier year, this CSD was contained in Nanaïmo, Noonas Bay, 1881 (1.9% share).

Later boundary forms

  • In a later year, this CSD became part of Nanaimo, South—Sud, 1901 (2.5% share).
