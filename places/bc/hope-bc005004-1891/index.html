Hope, British Columbia (1891 census)

Hope was a township in British Columbia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 774. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.256°N, 121.091°W.

Population

In 1891, Hope had a population of 774: 438 male and 336 female residents.
