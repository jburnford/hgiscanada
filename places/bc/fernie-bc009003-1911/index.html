Fernie, British Columbia (1911 census)

Fernie was a census subdivision in British Columbia, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 8,645. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.640°N, 114.910°W.

Population

In 1911, Fernie had a population of 8,645: 6,109 male and 2,536 female residents. Population density was 2.5 people per square mile.
