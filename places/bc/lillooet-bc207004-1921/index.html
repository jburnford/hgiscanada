Lillooet, British Columbia (1921 census)

Lillooet was a township in British Columbia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 2,400. The administrative centroid was at approximately 51.284°N, 122.741°W.

Population

In 1921, Lillooet had a population of 2,400: 1,516 male and 884 female residents.
