Lytton, British Columbia (1891 census)

Lytton was a census subdivision in British Columbia, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,338. The administrative centroid was at approximately 50.202°N, 121.310°W.

Population

In 1891, Lytton had a population of 1,338: 759 male and 579 female residents.
