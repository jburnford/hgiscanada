South, British Columbia (1881 census)

South was a township in New Westminster County, British Columbia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 1,640. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.098°N, 122.341°W.

Population

In 1881, South had a population of 1,640: 1,106 male and 534 female residents.
