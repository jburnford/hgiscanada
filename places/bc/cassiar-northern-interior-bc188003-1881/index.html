Cassiar, Northern Interior, British Columbia (1881 census)

Cassiar, Northern Interior was a township in New Westminster County, British Columbia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 3,566. The administrative centroid was at approximately 57.589°N, 128.957°W.

Population

In 1881, Cassiar, Northern Interior had a population of 3,566: 2,001 male and 1,565 female residents.
