Columbia, British Columbia (1921 census)

Columbia was a township in British Columbia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 3,067. The administrative centroid was at approximately 51.208°N, 116.804°W.

In 1921, Columbia had a population of 3,067: 2,064 male and 1,003 female residents.
