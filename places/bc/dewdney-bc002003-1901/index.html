Dewdney, British Columbia (1901 census)

Dewdney was a township in British Columbia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1901 Census of Canada with a population of 3,767. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.764°N, 122.220°W.

Population

In 1901, Dewdney had a population of 3,767: 2,203 male and 1,564 female residents.
