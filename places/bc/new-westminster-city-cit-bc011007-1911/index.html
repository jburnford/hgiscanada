New Westminster, City—Cité, British Columbia (1911 census)

New Westminster, City—Cité was a city in British Columbia, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 13,199. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q876122. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.212°N, 122.923°W.
