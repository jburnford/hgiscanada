Chilliwack, British Columbia (1921 census)

Chilliwack was a census subdivision in British Columbia, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 9,271. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.088°N, 122.091°W.

Population

In 1921, Chilliwack had a population of 9,271: 5,243 male and 4,028 female residents. Population density was 27.3 people per square mile.
