Grand Forks, British Columbia (1911 census)

Grand Forks was a township in British Columbia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,914. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q984028. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.311°N, 118.356°W.

Population

In 1911, Grand Forks had a population of 2,914: 2,012 male and 902 female residents. Population density was 3.6 people per square mile.
