South Vancouver, British Columbia (1921 census)

South Vancouver was a census subdivision in British Columbia, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 32,267. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.228°N, 123.070°W.

Population

In 1921, South Vancouver had a population of 32,267: 16,343 male and 15,924 female residents.
