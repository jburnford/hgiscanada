Vancouver Centre (Vancouver city (pt)), British Columbia (1921 census)

Vancouver Centre (Vancouver city (pt)) was a township in British Columbia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 60,879. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.286°N, 123.109°W.
