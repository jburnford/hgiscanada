Greenwood, British Columbia (1911 census)

Greenwood was a census subdivision in British Columbia, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,557. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q767282. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.209°N, 118.778°W.

Population

In 1911, Greenwood had a population of 1,557: 1,099 male and 458 female residents. Population density was 4.3 people per square mile.
