Prince Rupert, C, British Columbia (1911 census)

Prince Rupert, C was a city in British Columbia, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 4,184. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q1015639. The administrative centroid was at approximately 54.311°N, 130.316°W.

Population

In 1911, Prince Rupert, C had a population of 4,184: 3,030 male and 1,154 female residents. Population density was 892.7 people per square mile.
