Newcastle, British Columbia (1911 census)

Newcastle was a township in British Columbia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,094. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.062°N, 124.184°W.

Population

In 1911, Newcastle had a population of 2,094: 1,361 male and 733 female residents. Population density was 3.4 people per square mile.
