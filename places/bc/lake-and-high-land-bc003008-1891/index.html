Lake and High Land, British Columbia (1891 census)

Lake and High Land was a township in British Columbia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 335. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.511°N, 123.457°W.

Population

In 1891, Lake and High Land had a population of 335: 232 male and 103 female residents.
