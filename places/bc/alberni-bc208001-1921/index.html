Alberni, British Columbia (1921 census)

Alberni was a census subdivision in British Columbia, recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 4,996. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.613°N, 126.008°W.

Population

In 1921, Alberni had a population of 4,996: 3,279 male and 1,717 female residents. Population density was 0.8 people per square mile.
