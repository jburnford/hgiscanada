Kaslo, British Columbia (1911 census)

Kaslo was a census subdivision in British Columbia, recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 1,227. The administrative centroid was at approximately 50.325°N, 117.009°W.

Population

In 1911, Kaslo had a population of 1,227: 873 male and 354 female residents. Population density was 0.5 people per square mile.
