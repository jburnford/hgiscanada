Fort George, British Columbia (1921 census)

Fort George was a township in British Columbia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1921 Census of Canada with a population of 7,050. The administrative centroid was at approximately 56.771°N, 122.922°W.

Population

In 1921, Fort George had a population of 7,050: 4,679 male and 2,371 female residents.
