Quesnelle, British Columbia (1891 census)

Quesnelle was a census subdivision in British Columbia, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 706. The administrative centroid was at approximately 53.033°N, 123.156°W.

Population

In 1891, Quesnelle had a population of 706: 503 male and 203 female residents.
