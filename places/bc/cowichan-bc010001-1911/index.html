Cowichan, British Columbia (1911 census)

Cowichan was a township in British Columbia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 3,864. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.801°N, 124.146°W.

Population

In 1911, Cowichan had a population of 3,864: 2,459 male and 1,405 female residents. Population density was 3.8 people per square mile.
