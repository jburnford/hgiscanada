The Islands, British Columbia (1911 census)

The Islands was a township in British Columbia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 2,318. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.880°N, 123.476°W.

Population

In 1911, The Islands had a population of 2,318: 1,418 male and 900 female residents. Population density was 12.6 people per square mile.
