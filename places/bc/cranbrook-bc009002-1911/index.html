Cranbrook, British Columbia (1911 census)

Cranbrook was a township in British Columbia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 4,394. The community is grounded to Wikidata Q1138837. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.694°N, 115.812°W.

Population

In 1911, Cranbrook had a population of 4,394: 3,382 male and 1,012 female residents. Population density was 1.0 people per square mile.
