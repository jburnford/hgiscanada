Mountain, British Columbia (1891 census)

Mountain was a census subdivision in British Columbia, recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 1,625. The administrative centroid was at approximately 49.287°N, 124.519°W.

Population

In 1891, Mountain had a population of 1,625: 1,106 male and 519 female residents.
