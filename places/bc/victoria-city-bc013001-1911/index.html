Victoria City, British Columbia (1911 census)

Victoria City was a township in British Columbia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1911 Census of Canada with a population of 31,660. The administrative centroid was at approximately 48.428°N, 123.356°W.

Population

In 1911, Victoria City had a population of 31,660: 19,089 male and 12,571 female residents. Population density was 10696.0 people per square mile.
