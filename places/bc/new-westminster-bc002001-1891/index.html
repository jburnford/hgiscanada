New Westminster, British Columbia (1891 census)

New Westminster was a township in British Columbia (Canada West / Ontario), recorded in the 1891 Census of Canada with a population of 21,839. The administrative centroid was at approximately 55.743°N, 127.695°W.

Population

In 1891, New Westminster had a population of 21,839: 13,780 male and 8,059 female residents.
