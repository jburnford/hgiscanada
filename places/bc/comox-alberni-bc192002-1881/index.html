Comox & Alberni, British Columbia (1881 census)

Comox & Alberni was a census subdivision in British Columbia, recorded in the 1881 Census of Canada with a population of 279. The administrative centroid was at approximately 50.119°N, 126.306°W.

Population

In 1881, Comox & Alberni had a population of 279: 170 male and 109 female residents.
